# Pudd’nhead Wilson — A Whisper to the Reader

### *(Paathak Ke Liye Ek Gupt Sandesh)*

---

> **TO THE READER.** *(Paathak Ke Liye)*
> *There is no character, howsoever good and fine, but it can be destroyed by ridicule, howsoever poor and witless. Observe the ass, for instance: his character is about perfect, he is the choicest spirit among all the humbler animals, yet see what ridicule has brought him to. Instead of feeling complimented when we are called an ass, we are left in doubt.—Pudd’nhead Wilson’s Calendar.*
> *(Aisa koi charitra [character] nahi hai, chahe woh kitna bhi achha aur umda kyun na ho, jise mazaak aur uphaas [ridicule] ke zariye tabaah na kiya ja sake, chahe woh mazaak kitna bhi ghatiya aur moorkhta-purna [witless] kyun na ho. Udaharan ke liye, gadhe [ass] ko dekhein: uska charitra lagbhag perfect hai, woh saare vinamra jaanwaaron mein sabse behad shreshth aatma hai, phir bhi dekho mazaak ne uski kya haalat kar di hai. Jab hamein koi gadha kehta hai, toh tareef mehsoos karne ke bajaye, hum shak mein pad jaate hain.)*

---

Ek aisa insaan jo kanooni mamlon (legal matters) se anjaan hai, usse hamesha galtiyan hone ki sambhavna rehti hai jab woh apne pen se kisi court ke scene ko portrait karne (photograph) ki koshish karta hai; aur isliye main is kitab mein kanoon ke chapters ko pehle ek trained barrister—agar unhein yahi kaha jata hai—dwara kade aur thaka dene wale vishleshan (rigid and exhausting revision) aur correction ke bina press mein bhejne ke liye taiyar nahi tha. Yeh chapters ab har ek detail mein sahi hain, kyunki unhein William Hicks ki seedhi nazar ke tehat dobara likha gaya hai, jinhone thirty-five saal pehle southwest Missouri mein kuch samay ke liye kanoon adhyayan kiya tha aur phir apni health ke liye yahan Florence aa gaye the aur abhi bhi Piazza del Duomo se kona ghoomte hi piche ki gali mein majood Macaroni Vermicelli ke ghode-ke-chare wale shed (horse-feed shed) mein exercise aur khane-peene ke badle madad kar rahe hain. Yeh theek us ghar ke thoda pare hai jahan woh patthar jisme Dante cheh sau saal pehle baitha karte the wall mein lagaya gya hai, jab woh aisa dikhava karte (let on) the ki woh unhein Giotto’s campanile (ghanta-ghar) banate hue dekh rahe hain aur phir bhi jaise hi Beatrice apna bachaav karne ke liye chestnut cake ka ek tukda lene ke raaste se guzarti thin (is darr se ki kahin school pahunche se pehle Ghibelline ka vidroh [outbreak] na ho jaye) toh woh dekhkar turant thak jaate the, usi purane stand par jahan woh aaj bhi wahi purani cake bechte hain aur yeh aaj bhi utni hi halki aur achhi hai jitni tab thi, aur yeh koi tareef (flattery) nahi hai, isse bahut door hai. Kanoon par unka haath thoda tang tha (He was a little rusty), lekin unhone is kitab ke liye abhyas kiya (rubbed up), aur woh do ya teen kanooni chapters ab poori tarah sahi aur seedhe hain. Unhone mujhe khud aisa bataya tha.

2 January, 1893 ko mere haathon dwara, Florence se teen miles piche, pahadiyon par sthit Settignano village ke Villa Viviani mein diya gaya—yeh jagah nishchit roop se is planet par majood sabse aakarshak nazaara (most charming view) pradan karti hai, aur iske sath hi kisi bhi planet ya yahan tak ki kisi bhi solar system mein dhoondhe jaane wale sabse khayali aur jaadui sunset (dreamlike and enchanting sunsets) pradan karti hai—aur ghar ke sabse shandaar room (swell room) mein diya gaya, jahan Cerretani senators aur is vansh ke doosre bade amiron (grandees) ke busts (aadhe sharir ki moortiyan) mujh par swikriti se neeche dekh rahi hain, jaise woh Dante par dekha karti thin, aur shaanti se mujhse pooch rahi hain ki main unhein apni family mein apna loon, jise main khushi ke sath karta hoon, kyunki mere sabse door ke poorvaj (remotest ancestors) in robed aur stately antiques ke samne abhi-abhi nikle nau-sikhia bache (spring chickens) jaise hain, aur yeh mere liye ek badi aur santoshjanak badhottari (satisfying lift) hogi, woh cheh sau saal ki badhottari hogi.

— Mark Twain

# Chapter I — Pudd’nhead Wins His Name

### *(Pudd’nhead Ko Uska Naam Milta Hai)*

---

> *Tell the truth or trump—but get the trick.—Pudd’nhead Wilson’s Calendar.*
> *(Sach bolo ya turup ka ikka chalo—lekin chal [trick] haasil karo.)*

---

Is vritant (chronicle) ka scene Mississippi ke Missouri side par sthit Dawson’s Landing naam ka town hai, jo St. Louis se steamboat ke zariye aadhe din ki yatra ki doori par hai.

1830 mein yeh mamuli ek- aur do-manzil ke lakdi ke makanon (frame dwellings) ka ek chhota sa sahuliyat-bhara sangrah (snug collection) tha jinki whitewashed (choone se poti hui) baahri deewarein lagbhag gulab ki belon, honeysuckles aur morning-glories ke aapas mein uljhe ghaatank dhero (tangles) se nazar se chhupi hui thin. Inme se har ek khoobsurat ghar ke samne safed lakdi ke dondon (white palings) se ghira ek garden tha jo gulkhaire (hollyhocks), marigolds, touch-me-nots, prince’s-feathers aur doosre purane fashion ke phoolon se bhabhaka (opulently stocked) raha tha; jabki makanon ki khidkiyon ki dehleez (window-sills) par lakdi ke boxes rakkhe the jinmein moss-rose ke plants the aur terra-cotta (mitti) ke pots the jinmein geranium ki ek aishi prajati ugti thi jiske tivra laal rang ke phoolon ka phailav gulab se dhake ghar ke samne ke hisse ke halkay pink rang ko aag ke dhamake (explosion of flame) ki tarah highlight (accented) karta tha. Jab pots aur boxes ke baahar dehleez (ledge) par kisi cat ke liye jagah hoti thi, toh sunny weather mein cat wahan hoti thi—poori lambai mein phhaili hui, sooriyan aur anandit (blissful), sooraj ki taraf apni baal-daar belly (furry belly) kiye aur naak ke upar ek paw curve kiye hue. Tab woh ghar poora hota tha, aur iska santoosh aur shanti is symbol dwara duniya ke samne zahir (made manifest) hoti thi, jiski gavaahi bilkul achook (infallible) hai. Ek ghar bina kisi cat ke—aur ek achhi tarah se khilayi-pilayi, dulaari (well-petted) aur properly sammanit (revered) cat ke bina—shayad ek perfect ghar ho sakta hai, lekin woh apna adhikaar-patra (title) kaise prove kar sakta hai?

Poori sarkon ke sath, dono sides par, eent ke sidewalks (sidewalks) ke baahri edge par, locust ke trees khade the jinke tana (trunks) lakdi ke boxing se protected the, aur yeh sardi mein chhaon aur spring mein ek meethi fragrance (khushbu) pradan karte the jab kalyon ke guchhe (clusters of buds) baahar aate the. Mukhya sarak, jo river se ek block peeche aur uske parallel chalti thi, akeli business sarak thi. Yeh cheh blocks lambi thi, aur har block mein teen manzil oonchi eent ki do ya teen stores, beech-beech mein majood (interjected) choti lakdi ki shops ke dhero ke upar oonchi uthi thin. Latakte hue swinging signs poori sarak ki lambai mein hawa mein creak (ch चरमराते) the. Woh candy-striped (patti-daar) danda jo Venice ke mahalon se ghire canals ke sath ek garv-bhare aur ancient roop se kulinata (nobility) ko sanket deta hai, Dawson’s Landing ki mukhya sarak ke sath kevel ek mamuli barber shop ko sanket deta tha. Ek mukhya kone par ek ooncha bina paint kiya danda khada tha jo top se bottom tak tin ke pots, pans aur cups se ghira (wreathed) tha, jo mukhya tinmonger (tin ke bartan bechne wale) ka duniya ke samne (jab hawa chalti thi) ek shor-bhara notice tha ki us kone par business ke liye uski shop majood hai.

Is hamlet (chhhote gaon) ka samne ka hissa badi river ke saaf pani se dhota tha; iska sharir peeche ki taraf ek halke dhalan (gentle incline) par phhaila tha; iska sabse piche ka border badhte hue pahadiyon ke base-line par apne gharon ko bikhair (scattered) deta tha; pahadiyan oonchi uthi thin, town ko ek half-moon curve (ardh-chandrakaar ghumaav) mein ghere hue, jo pair se lekar peak tak jangaloon se dhaki (clothed) thin.

Steamboats har ek ghante mein aage-peeche guzarti thin. Choti Cairo line aur choti Memphis line se talluk rakhne wali steamboats hamesha rukti thin; bade Orleans liners sirf ishara karne (hails) par, ya passengers ya maal (freight) utarne ke liye rukte the; aur yahi haal “transients” (kabhi-kabar aane-jaane wale) ke vishal bedon (flotilla) ke sath bhi tha. Yeh baad wale doze bhashon ki rivers se aate the—Illinois, Missouri, Upper Mississippi, Ohio, Monongahela, Tennessee, Red River, White River, aur isi tarah aage bhi; aur har taraf jaane ke liye taiyar the aur har us dimaag mein aane suvidha (comfort) ya zarurat ke saaman se bhare (stocked) the jise Mississippi ki communities chaah sakti thin, Falls of St. Anthony ke barfeele hisse se lekar nau climates ke zariye garm New Orleans tak.

Dawson’s Landing ek gulam rakhne wala (slaveholding) town tha, jiske peeche gulamon dwara joti jaane wali grain aur pork ki ek ameer country thi. Town sleepy (susta), comfortable aur santusht tha. Yeh fifty saal purana tha, aur slowly badh raha tha—actually bahut slowly, lekin phir bhi yeh badh raha tha.

Mukhya citizen York Leicester Driscoll the, lagbhag forty saal ke, county court ke judge. Unhein apne purane Virginian poorvajon (ancestry) par bahut garv tha, aur apni mehmaan-nawazi (hospitalities) aur apne kaafi formal aur shandaar tareeqon (stately manners) mein woh unqi traditions ko barkarar rakhte the. Woh umda, nyay-priya aur udaar (generous) the. Ek gentleman banna—bina kisi daag ya dhabbe (stain or blemish) ke gentleman banna—unka akela religion tha, aur iske prati woh hamesha faithful the. Poori community dwara unki respect, izzat (esteemed) aur pyaar kiya jata tha. Woh acche khase ameer (well off) the, aur gradually apne bhandar ko badha rahe the. Woh aur unki wife lagbhag happy the, lekin poori tarah nahi, kyunki unke koi bache nahi the. Ek bachhe ke khazane ke liye tivra ichha (longing) saalon ke phisalne ke sath-sath stronger aur stronger hoti gayi thin, lekin woh blessing kabhi nahi aayi—aur kabhi nahi aane wali thi.

Is couple ke sath Judge ki vidhwa sister, Mrs. Rachel Pratt rehti thin, aur woh bhi childless thin—childless, aur us kaaran se udaas (sorrowful), aur santusht na hone wali (not to be comforted). Woh auraten achhi aur sadharan (commonplace) log thin, aur apni duty karti thin aur unhein saaf zameer (clear consciences) aur community ki sahamati (approbation) mein apna reward milta tha. Woh Presbyterians thin, Judge ek free-thinker (swatantra-vicharak) the.

Pembroke Howard, lawyer aur bachelor, umra lagbhag forty, ek aur purane Virginian amir (grandee) the jinke poorvaj First Families se prooved the. Woh ek umda, brave, aur prabhavshali prani (majestic creature) the, Virginia rule ke sabse baareek manandando (nicest requirements) ke mutabik ek gentleman, ek samarpit Presbyterian, “code” (maryada-kanoon) par ek authority, aur ek aishi insaan jo hamesha shishtata se (courteously) field mein aapke samne khade hone ke liye taiyar rehte the agar unka koi act ya word aapko shak-bhara lagta, aur aapke pasand ke kisi bhi hathiyar ke sath—brad-awls se lekar topkhana (artillery) tak—use explain karne ke liye taiyar rehte. Woh logon ke beech bahut popular the, aur Judge ke sabse kareebi dost the.

Phir Colonel Cecil Burleigh Essex the, ek aur bade caliber ke F. F. V. (First Families of Virginia)—halanki, unse hamara koi talluk nahi hai.

Percy Northumberland Driscoll, Judge ke bhai, aur unse paanch saal chote, ek married mard the, aur unke hearthstone (bhatthi/aangan) ke chaaron taraf bache rahe the; lekin unpar ek-ek karke measles, croup aur scarlet fever ne hamla kiya, aur isne doctor ko apne effective purane zamane ke tareeqon (antediluvian methods) ka use karne ka ek chance diya; isliye palne (cradles) khaali the. Woh ek kamyab (prosperous) mard the, speculations (satte-baazi/vyapar) ke liye ek achha head rakhne wale, aur unqi fortune badh rahi thi. 1 February, 1830 ko unke ghar do boy babes paada hue: ek unke yahan, doosra unqi gulam ladkiyon mein se ek ke yahan, jiska naam Roxana tha. Roxana twenty saal ki thin. Woh usi din uth kar chaaron taraf ghoom rahi thin, haath poori tarah se bhare (hands full) hone ke sath, kyunki woh dono babies ko sambhal (tending) rahi thin.

Mrs. Percy Driscoll ek hafte ke andar mar gayin. Roxy bacchon ke charge mein rahi. Unka apna tareeqa chalta tha, kyunki Mr. Driscoll jald hi speculations mein absorbed (leeng) ho gaye aur unhein unke apne hi upaayon (devices) par chhod diya.

Usi February ke mahine mein, Dawson’s Landing ko ek naya citizen mila. Yeh Mr. David Wilson the, Scotch parentage (poorvajon) ke ek young bande. Woh apna bhagya (fortune) dhoondhne ke liye New York State ke andaruni hisse mein sthit apne birthplace se is door-daas ke kshetra (remote region) mein bhatak (wandered) aaye the. Woh twenty-five saal ke the, college-bred (padhe-likhe) the, aur unhone ek doosre saal pehle ek Eastern law school mein post-college course khatam kiya tha.

Woh ek sadharan dikhne wale (homely), freckled (jhaiyon-waale), sandy-haired young bande the, ek intelligent blue eye ke sath jisme saaf-safai (frankness) aur saathi-pan (comradeship) tha aur ek pleasant tarah ki chhupi hui chamak (covert twinkle) thi. Agar unki ek badkismat tippani (unfortunate remark) na hoti, toh woh bina kisi shakk ke Dawson’s Landing mein turant ek safal career mein enter kar gaye hote. Lekin unhone village mein bitaye apne pehle hi din apna fatal remark (ghaatank tippani) kiya, aur isne unhein naap (gaged) diya. Unki abhi-abhi citizens ke ek group se mulaqat hui thi jab ek invisible (jo dikh nahi raha tha) dog ne yelp (bhaunkna), snarl (ghurrana) aur howl (rona) shuru kar diya aur apne aap ko poori tarah se na-pasand (disagreeable) bana diya, jispar young Wilson ne kuch aisa kaha, jaise koi zor se soch raha ho—

“Kaash main us dog ke aadhe hisse ka malik (owned half) hota.”

“Kyun?” kisi ne poocha.

“Kyunki main apne aadhe hisse ko maar deta.”

Group ne jigyasa ke sath, yahan tak ki chinta ke sath unke face ko dhoondha (searched), lekin wahan koi roshni nahi mili, koi aisa expression nahi jise woh padh sakein. Woh unse aise peeche hat gaye (fell away) jaise kisi ajeeb/paralaukik (uncanny) cheez se hte hain, aur unpar discuss karne ke liye akele mein (privacy) chale gaye. Ek ne kaha:

“Lagta hai (Pears) ki bewakoof hai.”

“Lagta hai?” doosre ne kaha. “Hai, main sochta hoon aap behtar kahein.”

“Kaha ki kaash woh dog ke aadhe hisse ka malik hota, idiot,” teesre ne kaha. “Usne kya socha ki agle aadhe hisse ka kya hoga agar usne apneaadhe hisse ko maar diya? Kya aap assume karte hain ki usne socha woh zinda rahega?”

“Kyun, usne nishchit roop se aisa socha hoga, jab tak ki woh duniya ka sabse downrightest (poora) bewakoof na ho; kyunki agar usne aisa na socha hota, toh woh poore dog ka malik banna chahta, yeh jaante hue ki agar usne apne aadhe hisse ko maar diya aur agla aadha hissa mar gaya, toh woh us aadhe hisse ke liye bhi waisa hi responsible (zimmedar) hoga jaise ki usne apne bajaye us aadhe hisse ko maara ho. Kya yeh aapko is tareeqe se nahi dikhta, gents?”

“Haan, aisa hi hai. Agar woh samanya dog (general dog) ke ek aadhe hisse ka malik hota, toh aisa hota; agar woh dog ke ek end ka malik hota aur doosra insaan doosre end ka malik hota, toh waisa hi hota, theek same; khhasakar pehle case mein, kyunki agar aap ek general dog ke ek aadhe hisse ko maar dete hain, toh koi aisa mard nahi hai jo bata sake ki woh kiska aadha hissa tha, lekin agar woh dog ke ek end ka malik hota, toh shayad woh uske apne end ko maar sakta aur—”

“Nahi, woh aisa bhi nahi kar sakta tha; woh aisa nahi kar sakta tha aur responsible na ho agar doosra end mar jata, jo ki woh marta. Meri raai mein mard apne sahi dimaag (right mind) mein nahi hai.”

“Meri raai mein uske paas koi dimaag hi nahi hai.”

No. 3 ne kaha: “Well, woh ek lummox (anadi/mota) hai, anyway.”

“Wahi toh woh hai,” No. 4 ne kaha, “woh ek labrick (murkh) hai—bas ek Simon-pure labrick (asli murkh) hai, agar kabhi koi tha.”

“Haan, sir, woh ek dam fool (mahaji-murkh) hai, is tareeqe se main use rakhta hoon,” No. 5 ne kaha. “Koi bhi jo chahta hai different soch sakta hai, lekin yeh mere vichaar (sentiments) hain.”

“Main aapke sath hoon, gentlemen,” No. 6 ne kaha. “Perfect jackass (gadha)—haanh, aur yeh kehna bahut aage nahi ja raha hai ki woh ek **pudd’nhead (gadha/murkh)** hai. Agar woh pudd’nhead nahi hai, toh main koi judge nahi hoon, bas itna hi.”

Mr. Wilson chune gaye the. Yeh incident poore town mein bataya gya, aur har kisi dwara gombhirta se discuss (gravely discussed) kiya gya. Ek hafte ke andar unhone apna pehla naam kho diya; *Pudd’nhead* ne iski jagah le li. Samay ke sath unhein pasand kiya jaane laga, aur kaafi achhi tarah pasand bhi kiya gya; lekin us samay tak nickname achhi tarah se chipak gya tha, aur yeh tik gaya. Us pehle din ke faisale (verdict) ne unhein ek bewakoof bana diya, aur woh ise kharij (set aside) karwane, ya thoda badalwane (modified) mein bhi kabil nahi rahe. Nickname ne jald hi apne sath kisi bhi karrwi ya gair-dostana bhavna (harsh or unfriendly feeling) ko carry karna band kar diya, lekin isne apni jagah banaye rakkhi, aur twenty lambe saalon tak apni jagah barkarar rakhne wala tha.

## The 9 Principles Checklist of Ultralearning

*(Ultralearning Ke 9 Niyamon Ki Printable Checklist)*

Aapke naye adhyayan prayason (learning projects) ke liye, Pudd’nhead Wilson ki tarah galat faisaloon se bachne aur sateekta se aage badhne ke liye yeh ranniti checklist (Checklist) hamesha kaam aayegi:

* **[ ] 1. Metalearning (Pehle Ek Map Banayein):** Adhyayan shuru karne se pehle kul expected samay ka 10% research par lagayein (Why, What, aur How ka hisab nikaalein).
* **[ ] 2. Focus (Apni Chhaku Ko Tez Karein):** Kaam ko talne (procrastination) aur bhatkaav (distraction) ke sroton ko mitaein. 5-minute rule ya Pomodoro ka use karein.
* **[ ] 3. Directness (Seedhe Aage Badhein):** Usi environment ya context mein karke seekhein (learning by doing) jahan skill ko actually apply karna hai.
* **[ ] 4. Drill (Apne Sabse Kamzor Point Par Hamla Karein):** Rate-determining step ko isolate karein aur complex skill ke chote hisson par alag se maharat haasil karein.
* **[ ] 5. Retrieval (Seekhne Ke Liye Test Karein):** Kitab band karke memory se active recall karein. Desirable difficulties ka fayda uthaein.
* **[ ] 6. Feedback (Punches Se Mat Bacho):** Harsh aur honest feedback se signal nikaalein aur noise ko ignore karein. Ego ko beech mein na laein.
* **[ ] 7. Retention (Ek Tooti Hui Balti Ko Mat Bharo):** Spaced-repetition software (SRS) ka use karein, core skills ko proceduralize aur overlearning karein.
* **[ ] 8. Intuition (Upar Build Karne Se Pehle Gehra Khodein):** Gaps ko dhoondhne ke liye Feynman Technique ka use karein aur concrete examples se shuru karein.
* **[ ] 9. Experimentation (Apni Aaram-dayak Seema Se Baahar Explore Karein):** Resources, techniques aur styles ke sath prayog karke extremes ko explore karein.
