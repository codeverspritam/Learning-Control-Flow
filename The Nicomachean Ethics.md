# INTRODUCTION

Aristotle pehle Western thinker the jinhone philosophy ko alag-alag branches mein divide kiya jo aaj bhi pehchane jaate hain: logic, metaphysics, natural philosophy, philosophy of mind, ethics aur politics, rhetoric; unhone in sabhi fields mein major contributions diye. Unka janam Stagira mein hua tha, jo northern Greece ka ek city hai, 384 BC mein. Unke father Nicomachus, Macedon ke Amyntas ke court mein ek doctor the, jo Philip se pehle the — Philip jinhone Greece ke bahut saare hisse conquer kiye the. Baad mein Aristotle Philip ke remarkable bete, Alexander the Great, ke tutor bane.

Jab Aristotle jawan the tab wo 367 mein Athens gaye Plato ke saath padhne ke liye, unki Academy mein, aur wahin rahe jab tak 347 mein Plato ki death nahi ho gayi. Ye wo period tha jab Plato ne Timaeus, Sophist, Statesman, aur Philebus jaisi works likhi, saath hi apni Laws bhi likhi, aur ye sochna interesting (intriguing) hai ki inme se kitna kuch unke brilliant student Aristotle ke challenges ki wajah se aaya hoga. Asia Minor mein kuch waqt bitane ke baad (jahan shayad unhone apne biological works ke liye research ki thi) aur phir Macedon wapas tutor ke roop mein, Aristotle Athens laut aaye aur apna khud ka philosophical school, Lyceum, banaya: iske kuch remains recently modern Athens mein discover hue hain. Unki death 322 BC mein hui, us saal ke baad jab unhe Athens chhodna pada apne former student, emperor Alexander, ki death ke baad.

Kuch popular works ke fragments ko chhod kar, jo writings humein mili hain wo academic treatises hain, kuch zyada polished, kuch kam. Ye modern sense mein publish nahi hui thi, aur ho sakta hai Aristotle inhe revise karte rahe hon. Lyceum mein padhaai ki breadth aur character ko in surviving treatises se andaza lagaya ja sakta hai, jo ek immense range cover karti hain, aur hamesha ek questioning, argumentative (bahas karne wali), aur non-dogmatic (bina zid ke) style mein likhi gayi hain. Ho sakta hai ye works jaisi survive hui hain wo lecture-notes hi hon, aur isi se inki kuch rough features samajh aati hain. Sach ye hai ki humein inke original form aur purpose ke baare mein bahut kam pata hai, ki inhe kis order mein likha gaya tha ya kaise edit kiya gaya.

Ethics par teen works Aristotle ke naam se aayi hain: Nicomachean Ethics (NE) das 'books' mein, Eudemian Ethics (EE) aath 'books' mein, aur so-called Magna Moralia ya 'great ethics'. Ye aakhri wali shayad Aristotle ki khud ki nahi hai balki kisi student ka lecture course record ho sakta hai. Baaki do ke titles unke editors se aaye maane jaate hain jo Aristotle ki death ke baad aaye: Nicomachus Aristotle ke bete the, jabki Eudemus ek student the. Dono works kaafi same topics cover karti hain, aur inke beech ka relation controversial hai, saath hi ek added puzzle bhi hai ki teen books dono works mein common hain: NE ki Books V-VII, EE ki Books IV-VI se correspond karti hain. Zyadatar scholarly opinion ye hai ki Nicomachean Ethics baad ki work hai, aur ye kam se kam first ya second century AD se Aristotle ki ethics par major aur definitive work maani jaati rahi hai. Kuch hisse doosron se zyada polished hain, aur ek common book (Book V, justice par) mein kuch material out of place lagta hai. Aristotle ki writing mein ek certain terse (sanchit/kam shabdon wali) elegance hai, aur ye arguments present karne ke liye ideal hai, jinse unki philosophy bhari padi hai. Ye work happiness ki discussion se shuru hoti hai, phir moral virtues — character ke virtues — ki taraf jaati hai, justice sameit; aur phir intellect ke virtues ki taraf; ye pleasure aur friendship, aur best life mein inke role ki discussion karti hai, phir happiness ki further discussion par wapas aati hai, aur final transition political theory ki taraf hoti hai. Aristotle ethics ko politics ki ek branch maante hain, aur unki work Politics isi ka sequel ban kar design hui thi.

Plato, aur unke teacher Socrates, ne Aristotle ki philosophizing ke liye scene set kiya. 399 BC mein death ki saza paayi, Socrates ne koi written philosophy nahi chhodi aur humein unke views Plato ke dialogues se, saath hi Xenophon ki works se, samajhne padte hain. Aristotle, jinhone Socrates ki death ke chaalis saal baad Plato ke saath padhna shuru kiya, Socrates ko ethical questions mein exclusive interest ka credit dete hain, aur unhe kuch key theses attribute karte hain: ki saari virtues ek tarah ka knowledge hain (VI. 13) aur ki koi bhi wo cheez ke against act nahi karta jo wo best jaanta (ya judge karta) hai (VII. 2). Socrates dwara, aur unke baad Plato dwara, discuss kiye gaye ethical questions ye hain: insaan ko kaise jeena chahiye; virtues kya hain, kya inhe sikhaya ja sakta hai, aur sabse zyada, ye kyun choose karne layak hain. 'The unexamined life is not worth living' (bina jaanche-parkhe zindagi jeene layak nahi), Socrates apni Apology mein declare karte hain — wo speech jo Plato ne likhi thi, unke trial mein unki defence hone ka daava karte hue. Isme Socrates apni lifestyle describe karte hain jisme wo so-called experts se sawaal-jawaab karte hain ye dekhne ke liye ki kya wo apne beliefs ko defend kar sakte hain.

Aristotle ki zyada tar philosophy unke predecessors (pehle wale thinkers) ki writings par ek reflection aur response hai, aur wo metaphysics, natural philosophy, aur philosophy of mind par unke views se khud ko door rakhna chahte hain. Lekin major ethical questions par, Socrates, Plato, aur Aristotle agreement mein hain — halaanki Aristotle kabhi bhi khule shabdon mein ye admit nahi karte. Teeno agree karte hain ki insaano ke liye highest good happiness hai, aur ki life ka ek rational choice apni khud ki happiness ki taraf directed hoga. Sirf wahi life happy hogi jisme insaan traditional virtues (justice, temperance, courage, aur practical wisdom) cultivate karta hai. Plato ke dialogues mein Socrates ko traditional virtues ke against kuch immoralist challenges face karte dikhaya gaya hai, lekin morality par sceptical attacks ki treatment Aristotle ke work mein zyadatar absent hai. Unke major contributions mein happiness kya hai iska ek in-depth study, virtues happiness se kaise related hain, aur different types ke virtue — moral aur intellectual — kya hain, shaamil hai. Wo Socrates dwara knowledge ko diye gaye prominence par sawaal uthate hain moral virtue ke account mein, lekin unka apna considered view phronēsis, practical wisdom, ko moral virtue mein ek key role deta hai, aur wo Socrates se is baat mein agree karte hain ki moral virtues essentially united hain (VI. 13). Aristotle apne teacher Plato ko ek famous chapter mein challenge karte hain jisme Plato ke Form of the Good ki discussion hoti hai (1.6), saath hi ye bhi admit karte hain ki 'ye inquiry ek uphill (mushkil/chadhaai wali) hai, kyunki Forms ko humare apne dosto ne hi admit kiya hai.' Ye insist karte hue ki inquiry ke different subjects ke different starting points hote hain aur alag approaches chahiye, Aristotle ek so-called universal good, ya good ka 'Form' ya 'Idea', jo Plato ki Republic mein sab kuch ki goodness aur being ke foundation ke roop mein introduce kiya gaya tha, ko dismiss karte hain. Wo ise ek impossible concept mante hain aur ethics ke liye anyway irrelevant bhi, jo insaan ke good ka study hai.

Plato ne, saath hi doosre predecessors ne bhi, pleasure aur goodness ke relation ko lambe mein explore kiya tha. Ek dialogue, Protagoras, mein Plato dikhate hain ki Socrates hedonism defend kar rahe hain, jo ye thesis hai ki pleasure hi good hai, Protagoras ki zyada conventional morality ke against. Aristotle ka contribution is debate mein ye tha ki wo pleasure kya hai aur humein isse kaise sochna chahiye, is sawaal mein bahut zyada gehraai mein gaye, saath hi ye bhi pucha ki ye good se kaise related hai. Best life mein pleasure ke sthaan (place) ke sawaal par unka jawaab Plato ki Republic aur Philebus jaisa hi hai: pleasure good nahi hai, lekin best life zaroor sabse pleasant bhi hogi, jisme wo pleasures shaamil honge jo humari rational beings ki nature ke suited hain. Isi tarah Aristotle ethics ke aur bhi central concepts, jaise voluntariness, choice, deliberation, aur practical reasoning, mein bahut gehraai se probe (jaanch) karte hain; inme se kuch neeche detail mein discuss kiye gaye hain.

## Aristotle's Ethical Theory: Its Key Elements

### The Human Good: Happiness

Aristotle ke liye, ethics insaan ke good ka inquiry hai. Action se attain hone wale sabhi goods mein sabse highest kya hai? Har koi — educated aur lowly, healthy aur sick — (wo likhte hain) is baat par verbal agreement rakhte hain: ye happiness hai, eudaimonia (1.4). Aur sab isse doing well ya faring well ke saath equate karte hain. Lekin happiness kya hai, ye ek purani (long-standing) dispute ka matter hai, humein pata chalta hai, jisme teen 'lives' contention mein hain: sensual enjoyment ki, political achievement ki, aur intellectual contemplation ki (1.5). Aristotle best life ke liye ek chautha (fourth) contender bhi add karte hain, lekin usse jaldi dismiss kar dete hain: wealth ka pursuit. Ise dismiss karne ke liye unhe sirf ye point out karna hota hai ki wealth kisi aur cheez ke liye chaahi jaati hai. Highest good ko khud ke liye chaha jaana chahiye; ye ek activity mein consist karna chahiye (kisi state mein nahi jisme insaan hota hai) aur self-sufficient hona chahiye, kisi cheez ki kami na ho. Ye sab confirm karta hai ki happiness, jo in conditions ko satisfy karti hai, sach mein highest good hai. Lekin ek zyada informative jawaab paane ke liye, wo ye idea invoke karte hain ki insaan ka ek function hai — rational activity — aur conclude karte hain ki happiness excellent rational activity hai: unke shabdon mein, virtue ke according rational activity (1.7). 'Function' ergon ka translation hai, literally 'task' ya 'work'. Hum neeche 'function argument' par, aur excellence aur virtue par wapas aayenge.

Ab tak humein modern reader ke liye kaafi kuch surprising mila hai. Ethics happiness ka study kyun ho, aur — shayad — is baat ka nahi ki main dusron ka kya lena hoon, ya right action ka criterion kya hai? Hum is sawaal par neeche wapas aayenge. Happiness ko outstanding rational activity define karna un logo ko confusing (puzzling) lag sakta hai jo maante hain ki happiness ek mental state hai, subjective well-being ki ek state. Is problem ko aasaan karne ke liye, kuch logo ne suggest kiya hai ki eudaimonia ko 'flourishing' (phalna-phoolna) ya 'fulfilment' translate karna chahiye. Clearly 'happiness' se Aristotle kisi bhi tarah ki mental state ki baat nahi kar rahe, aur bilkul bhi aisi state ki nahi jahan subjects ke self-reports invite kiye jaayein aur definitive maane jaayein.

Happiness ki apni investigation mein, yani insaano ke liye best life ki, Aristotle kai assumptions banate hain. Jawaab kisi individual ki preferences ke hisaab se vary nahi karega; aur logo ka apni khud ki happiness ka assessment galat ho sakta hai. Happiness ko pleasure se equate nahi kiya jaana chahiye, lekin phir bhi, wo (Book X mein) pleasure aur highest good ke beech ke is puraane sawaal ko solve karenge ye find karke ki truly happy life sach mein sabse pleasant bhi hogi, halaanki uska highest good hona uski pleasantness ki wajah se nahi hai. Happiness sirf unhe available hai jinki age, gender, aur civic status unhe excellent activities ki wo life pursue karne dete hain jo isse banaati hain. Bacchon ko happy sirf is sense mein bulaya ja sakta hai ki unki lives happiness ka promise karti hain; slaves ki life happiness ko preclude (rok deti) karti hai, aur — hum shayad infer kar sakte hain — waisi hi women ki bhi, halaanki ye unsaid chhoda gaya hai. Sabse zyada striking shayad unka famous 'function argument' ka use hai.

### Happiness and Human Function: Rational Activity

'Function argument' ka use insaan ke good ko uske human function ke through find karne ke liye kiya jaata hai (1.7). Ye ek bure start se shuru hota hai, function rakhne walon ke examples ke saath — flute-players, aankhein, haath, ya paanv — jo irrelevant lagte hain. Flute-players, apne hi title se, wo log hain jinka role flute bajaana hai, aur isse insaano ke liye, as such, kisi role ke baare mein kuch follow nahi hota. Aur halaanki hum aasaani se agree kar sakte hain ki aankh ka function dekhna hai, ye is wajah se hai kyunki poore organism mein yahi iska role hai. Ye parallels humein convince nahi karenge ki insaano ka bhi ek function hai. Balki, Aristotle apni nature ki philosophy se ek key assumption draw kar rahe hain. Insaano ke jeene ka ek tarika hona chahiye, aur unhe kaise jeena chahiye. Ye is wajah se nahi hai ki god ne unhe kisi purpose ke liye banaya — jo Aristotle nahi maante the — balki simply is wajah se ki wo ek certain kism ke living being hain, aur har living species ka apna work ya function hota hai. Insaano ke paas kai capacities hain — Aristotle inhe soul ki capacities kehte hain, lekin soul se unka matlab sirf itna hai jiski wajah se koi cheez alive hoti hai. Kuch capacities lower animals ke saath share hoti hain; reason wo capacity hai jo insaan ko alag banaati hai. To, kyunki kisi being ka function wo hai jo special hai, na ki jo shared hai, reason hi best human life ki key hai.

Rational capacities ko aur investigate karne se pehle, chaliye kuch objections par ruk jaate hain. Pehla, hum ye kyun infer karein ki insaano ko kya karna best hai, sirf isliye ki wo kya kar sakte hain? Bahut saari cheezein hain jo sirf insaan, apni rational capacities ki wajah se, kar sakte hain. Jaise cards mein cheat karna, mass destruction ke weapons banana, ya bacchon ko sex-abuse ke liye groom karna: hum inhe best human life ka part nahi banana chahenge. Ya phir, hum un activities ko best life se kyun exclude karein jo hum doosre animals ke saath share karte hain, jaise offspring ki parwarish karna? (Iska jawaab aasan hai, ye recognize karke ki reason use karke aisa karna, animal ki tarah karne se different maana ja sakta hai.) Sabse zyada serious sawaal ye hai ki insaano ka ek general function insist kyun kiya jaaye? Insaano ke baare mein jo special hai wo shayad ye hai ki individuals apni abilities, preferences, aur goals mein kitne markedly (spasht roop se) alag hain.

Kuch log ek alag objection uthate hain: kisi insaan ke liye good, ek good person jo typically karta hai, usme kyun consist ho? Wo Aristotle par ye ilzaam lagate hain ki wo good ke matlab ko confuse (conflate) kar rahe hain — beneficial wale sense mein (yani insaano ke liye kya good hai), ek different, shayad moral good ke saath (yani ek good person kya karta hai) — jab wo declare karte hain ki kisi F (ek flute-player, ek insaan) ke liye good wahi hoga jo good F karta hai. Lekin ye koi real difficulty nahi hai. Human good dhoondte hue, Aristotle wo good dhoondh rahe hain jo insaano ke concern mein hai, na ki kisi aisi cheez ki jo insaano ke liye good ho, jaise khaana aur paani unke liye good hote hain. Aur jab wo ek good human being ki baat karte hain, unhone illicitly (galat tareeke se) ek morally good person ka notion smuggle nahi kiya hai. Ek good person, itna to bas, ek good specimen hai insaan ka, jaise ek good oak tree ya ek good elephant. Lekin phir moral goodness aur virtues ka kya, ye picture mein kaise aate hain?

### Excellent Rational Activity and the Virtues

Ab tak ek immoralist jaisa Thrasymachus, Plato ki Republic mein, Aristotle ki happiness ki definition se agree kar sakta hai — ek life jo excellence ya virtue (aretē) ke according rational activity hai. Thrasymachus, injustice ki life ki tareef karte hue, yani doosron ko exploit karne aur unse aage nikalne ki, ise ek virtue kehta hai aur ise rational aur sensible bata kar praise karta hai. To key questions ye hain: reason aur rationality kya hain, aur inhe exercise karne ka kaunsa tareeka excellence ya virtue manifest karta hai? Yahan humein translation ki ek problem milti hai. Aretē, jise usually virtue translate kiya jaata hai, iska matlab kisi bhi tarah ki excellence hai, aur ye pruning-hooks (kaatne ke औzaar) par bhi apply ho sakta hai, insaano par bhi. Jaisa abhi note kiya gaya, Plato ka character Thrasymachus deny kar sakta hai ki justice ek virtue hai, simply is wajah se ki wo ise ek excellent quality nahi maanta. To Aristotle apne virtues ke selection ko — excellent rational activities ke selection ko — kaise justify karte hain?

Reason, jaisa hum 1.13 mein seekhte hain, human soul mein different tareekon se feature karta hai. (Jab wo soul ki baat karte hain, jaisa humne dekha, Aristotle ka matlab simply kisi living cheez ki capacities se hai, jab tak wo alive hai.) Ek soul-part, intellect, mein reason full sense mein hota hai, lekin jo part appetites (chahaton) rakhta hai, wo ek secondary tareeke se rational hai. Yani, appetites reason ke responsive hote hain, halaanki khud rational nahi hote. Hum inhe semi-rational label kar sakte hain, halaanki Aristotle ise is tarah nahi rakhte. Kyunki do soul-parts hain, apne alag tareeko se rational, isliye do kism ki virtue hoti hain, character ki virtues — jinka locus appetites hain, aur intellect ki virtues. Moral virtues, ya character ki virtues, ki discussion mein sabse zyada hissa hoga (II–V), jabki VI intellect ki virtues discuss karti hai, jinme se ek vital link moral virtues ke saath, practical wisdom, hai. Character ki virtues ke account mein, humein milta hai ki traditional virtues — courage, temperance, aur justice — jaldi hi discussion mein aa jaati hain, halaanki — jaisa humne dekha — ek morally good person ka notion happiness ki definition mein pehle se implicit nahi tha. Jo hum vain (bekaar) mein dhoondte hain wo ek argument hai ki apni rationality ko sabse best possible tareeke se, 'excellence ke according', exercise karna, traditional moral virtues ko rakhna aur exercise karna hai. Shayad Aristotle ne socha ki aisa proof possible nahi tha; aur kyunki unke audience well-brought-up (achhe se pale-badhe) jawaan log the (lekin bahut zyada jawaan nahi!), ye zaroori bhi nahi tha.

Aristotle ke account mein, virtues ke baare mein kya valuable hai, chahe wo intellect ki virtues ho ya moral virtues, chahe self-regarding (khud se related) ho jaise temperance, ya other-regarding (dusron se related) jaise justice? Unke system mein, unhe virtues banane wali cheez simply ye hai ki, unhe rakh kar aur exercise kar ke, insaan ek life jee raha hota hai jo ek insaan ke liye best life hai. Wo ek person ki eudaimonia mein intrinsically contribute karti hain. (Wo ye allow karte hain ki kuch external goods bhi eudaimonia ke liye necessary conditions hain, ek view par attack karte hue — shayad unhone ise Plato ka view maana — ki virtue hi sufficient hai.) Ise ek consequentialist view se contrast karein jisme human virtues society ya humare neighbours ya khud humare liye jo results laate hain unki wajah se valuable maani jaati hain. Main is contrast par neeche wapas aaunga.

### How Moral Virtues are Acquired and How they 'Lie in a Mean'

Aristotle insist karte hain ki habituation (aadat dalna), na ki teaching, moral virtue ka raasta hai (II. 1). Humein good actions karne ki practice karni hoti hai, sirf virtue ke baare mein padhna kaafi nahi. Halaanki important tarike se sahi, ye oversimplify (zyada simple bana dena) karta hai, aur jaldi hi clear ho jaata hai ki reason ka bhi ek role hai. Jabki moral virtues semi-rational soul part ki excellences hain jisme appetites (emotions samet) hote hain, virtue proper banne ke liye, reason ke responsiveness ki zaroorat hoti hai. Reason ke responsiveness ke saath-saath, ek virtuous person good actions karne mein enjoy karne lagta hai (II.3), aur sahi feelings develop karta hai (fear, anger, waghera ke).

Ab hum Aristotle ke us famous doctrine par aate hain ki moral virtue ek tarah ka mean (madhya/beech ka point) hai. Ek moral virtue rakhna matlab 'ek intermediate tareeke se' feel karne aur act karne ke liye disposed hona; virtues 'mean' ya 'intermediate' states hain. Humein ise 'har cheez mein moderation' ka doctrine nahi samajhna chahiye. Balki, isme feelings rakhna (jaise anger ka) aur responses jo 'intermediate' hain — appropriate ya proportional ke sense mein — chahiye hote hain. Halaanki Aristotle ise excess aur defect, zyada aur kam, se bachne ke roop mein characterize karte hain, sach mein ye idea kuch misleading hai, kyunki har galat hone ka tareeka zyada ya kam involve nahi karta. Zyada helpful ye characterization hai ki intermediate wo hai jo best hai, aur 'sahi time par, sahi objects ke reference mein, sahi logo ke prati, sahi motive ke saath, aur sahi tareeke se' karna aur feel karna hai (II.6). Unki eventual definition ek moral virtue ki ye hai ki ye 'ek state of character hai jo choice se concerned hai, ek mean mein lying, yani humare relative mean, jo reason se determine kiya jaata hai, aur us reason se jise practical wisdom ka aadmi determine karega (II.b)'. Ab tak unhone feelings aur actions ke roles outline kiye hain, aur moral virtue ko ek acquired state of character ke roop mein identify kiya hai jo humein appropriately feel karne aur act karne ke liye choose karne ko dispose karta hai. Jaisa definition ka last clause reveal karta hai, isse account mein ek gap reh jaata hai, jo tab fill hoga jab wo intellect ki virtues discuss karenge, jinme se practical wisdom (phronēsis) sabse prominent hai.

### Virtue Proper and Continence

Do soldiers imagine karein. Ek reasonably fearless hai bina ek dare-devil (khatarnaak roop se nirbhay) hue, usne ye samajh develop ki hai ki kaunse dangers face kiye jaane chahiye, aur wo unhe just appropriate degree ka fear feel karte hue face kar sakta hai. Uska comrade-in-arms (saathi sipahi) different hai; terrors (bhayankar dar) se pareshan hote hue bhi wo apni post hold karta hai aur battle mein wo role play karta hai jiski usse expectation hai. Pehla, na ki doosra, soldier courage ki moral virtue ka credit paata hai, Aristotle ke according. Ya phir, teen citizens imagine karein, jinke paas city ki pleasures tak access hai: achha khaana aur wine, sex ke opportunities. Citizen A inhe appropriate matra mein desire aur enjoy karta hai, due reflection ke saath lekin kisi conflict ki feeling ke bina. B utna hi indulge karta hai jitna A, lekin usse aksar apni over-strong physical pleasures ki appetites ko rein (kaabu) mein rakhna padta hai, jabki C ko pata hai ki usse aisa karna chahiye, lekin kabhi-kabhi wo succumb (haar maan) ho jaata hai aur zyada indulge karta hai jitna usse pata hai ki usse nahi karna chahiye. In sabme se, A ke paas temperance ki virtue hai, jabki B sirf 'continent' ya self-controlled (enkratēs) hai, aur C 'incontinent' hai, doosre shabdon mein self-control ki kami hai halaanki abhi vicious nahi hai, kyunki usse realize hota hai ki usse aise indulge nahi karna chahiye jaise wo karta hai. Aristotle ka verdict, ki A hi morally virtuous hai na ki B, ek aise ethical outlook ko perverse, aur sach mein shocking, laga hai jo Christianity se derive hota hai jo temptation ko overcome karne ko value karta hai. Lekin humein yaad rakhna hoga ki moral virtue, yani character ki excellence, wo best state of character hai jo koi insaan rakh sakta hai. Agar, chah kar, aap ye bana sakte ki aapka godson in mein se ek ban jaaye, aap zaroor chahenge ki wo pehla ho, doosra nahi, aur phir, A ki tarah, unruly ya over-powerful appetites se free ho, na ki wo jise unhe curb karna pade. Humein continence ki koi extended discussion nahi milti, lekin iska opposite — incontinence — ki lambi discussion milti hai, kyunki ye Aristotle ki moral psychology mein ek apparent problem paida karti hai (neeche discuss kiya gaya hai).

### The Virtues of Intellect and Practical Wisdom

Happiness ke account ke liye intellect ki virtues (Book VI) ki discussion zaroori hai, do reasons se. Pehla, virtues ke roop mein, ye best life ke liye zaroori hain: happy hone ke liye insaan ko sochne aur reason karne mein in virtues ko employ karna hota hai. Doosra, jaisa humne dekha, moral virtue ki definition mein reason ka ek essential reference tha, yani, wo reason jo phronimos (practical wisdom wala insaan) kisi bhi diye gaye instance mein virtuous act kya hai ye determine karne ke liye use karta hai. (Brevity ke liye, main aage Greek term, phronimos, use karunga.) To humein khaas kar phronēsis, practical wisdom, ki discussion chahiye.

Theoretical aur practical thinking ke beech ka distinction highlight karte hue, Aristotle ise (VI. 1) necessary truths, jaise mathematics ki, aur contingent truths, jaise ye ki kal sea-battle hogi ya nahi, ke beech ke distinction ke saath align karte hain. Theoretical side par unhe do virtues milti hain, scientific knowledge aur intuitive reason, jo saath mein wisdom (sophia) banaati hain (VI.7). Kyunki scientific knowledge ko proof chahiye hota hai, aur kisi bhi proof ko unproven assumptions se shuru hona padta hai, intuitive reason (nous) chahiye hota hai un starting points ko grasp karne ke liye jo deductive reasoning ke liye zaroori hain jo scientific knowledge require karti hai. Practical side par (aise matters se deal karte hue jo otherwise ho sakte hain, isliye deliberation ke liye suitable hain) wo 'making' — art ki province (yani kisi outcome ko produce karne ki expertise) — aur 'doing', jahan doing ke alawa koi outcome aim nahi kiya jaata, ke beech ek important distinction draw karte hain (VI.5). Practical wisdom (phronēsis) doing se concerned intellectual virtue hai.

Is complex discussion se unke account mein kuch tensions reveal hoti hain: kya phronimos ko general principles chahiye, ya particular cases mein sahi hona kaafi hai? Wo kitna deliberate aur reason karta hai, ya phronēsis zyada tar kisi bhi situation mein jo action call karti hai uske salient features ko 'dekhne' ka matter hai? Ye doosra wala certainly phronēsis ki ek key feature hai. VI ke last do chapters mein aur important points banaye gaye hain, jo phronēsis aur moral virtue ke close connection ko reinforce karte hain. 'Virtue goal ko correct banati hai aur practical wisdom us tak le jaane wale raaste ko correct banati hai': pehli nazar mein ye suggest karta hai ki labour ki ek problematic division hai, aur ki practical wisdom ka role sirf means-end reasoning se zyada kuch nahi hai. Lekin ye poori picture nahi ho sakti jo Aristotle paint karna chahte hain. Kyunki, jab tak reason kisi ke emotional development ko guide na kare, wo pehli jagah moral virtue nahi rakhega. Book VI ke end tak, humein pata chalta hai ki virtues ka initial division moral aur intellectual mein kuch misleading tha. Phronēsis, halaanki ek intellectual virtue hai, moral virtues se independently develop nahi ho sakti, jabki wo, apni taraf se, halaanki person ke non-rational (ya, jaisa humne kaha, semi-rational) part ki virtues hain, sirf reason ki guidance mein hi apni perfection tak pahunch sakti hain.

### The Final Account of Happiness: Contemplation

Book X mein, initial question par wapas aate hue, Aristotle likhte hain: 'Agar happiness virtue ke according activity hai, to reasonable hai ki ye highest virtue ke according ho; aur ye humare andar ki best cheez ki hogi.' Jaisa wo aage argue karte hain, humare andar ki best cheez reason ka ek aspect hai, na ki reason overall. Ye wo aspect hai jo unchanging objects aur necessary truths ka study karta hai; highest activity contemplation hai, aur iska virtue wisdom (sophia) hai, us special sense mein jo ise theoretical, yani philosophical, thinking ki excellence tak confine karta hai. Ek baar phir wo criteria invoke karte hue jo unhone I.7 mein best good ke liye laid down kiye the, wo ye dikhane ki koshish karte hain ki contemplative life sabse zyada end-like aur sabse self-sufficient hai, aur moral virtue ki life ko in dono respects mein (aur doosron mein bhi) surpass karti hai. Kya ye ek emphasis ka change signal karta hai, ya kya Aristotle ne Book I mein hi apni eventual declaration ke liye zameen taiyaar kar di thi ki contemplative life happiest hai, jabki moral virtue ki life sirf secondary tareeke se happiest hai?

Ye ek intense scholarly dispute ka matter hai, aur resolve karna mushkil hai. Interpretation ka ek school Aristotle ko 1.7 mein firmly ek inclusive account of happiness advocate karte hue paata hai, aise ki best life un goods ka best combination include karegi jinhe hum khud ke liye desire karte hain. Sirf isi tarah ye 'doosre goods ke addition se zyada desirable nahi banayi ja sakti'. Lekin, agar Aristotle ne Book I mein aisa inclusive account favour kiya, to ye clash karta lagta hai sirf ek kism ki activity, contemplation, ko best select karne se, aur moral virtue ki practice (uske saathi, practical wisdom, ke saath) ko second place mein daalne se. Interpretation ki doosri line note karti hai ki, happiness ek inclusive end hai wale indications ke saath-saath, Book I ne pehle se hint diya tha ki happiness best activities mein se best ke saath identify hogi (1099a30). Phir bhi, kaam ka zyada tar hissa moral virtues aur action ki philosophy ke related issues, jaise voluntariness, choice, aur deliberation, par focus raha hai. Aisa hone se, reader ko contemplation ki life ki final tareef (X.7 aur 8), aur morally virtuous action ki value ko downplay kiya jaana, surprising lagega.

Practical matters par Aristotle ka focus, phir bhi, final chapter mein wapas aata hai. Apne initial statement ke sach rehte hue ki ethics politics ki ek branch hai, wo poochte hain ki morally good behaviour, aur wo dispositions (moral virtues) jo ise prompt karti hain, kaise best develop ki ja sakti hain. Parents dwara ordinary upbringing ke alawa, good laws bahut saare reasons ke liye essential hain. Laws child-rearing ke kuch aspects ordain karti hain; wo good behaviour ke standards set karti hain, aur log tab behtar respond karte hain jab laws, na ki despots (tanashaah), standards impose karne ki koshish karti hain. To ethics ka ek full study mein law ki, aur best type ki constitution ki, discussions shaamil karni hongi jo wo apni Politics mein aage denge.

## Aristotle's Ethics and Alternative Approaches

Jo log Aristotle ko padhte hain aur ethics mein kuch aur important approaches se familiar hain, wo zaroor poochenge ki ye theories kaise compare hoti hain. Kuch log to yahan tak claim karte hain ki Aristotle jo hum aaj morality samajhte hain wo discuss hi nahi kar rahe. Main briefly do zyada recent, aur famous, ethical theories ko touch karta hoon, aur phir ek newer approach dekhta hoon, so-called neo-Aristotelian Virtue Theory.

### Kant

Kant ki moral theory unki Groundwork of the Metaphysic of Morals mein adumbrate (rooprekha di gayi) ki gayi hai. (Ek baad ki work, Metaphysic of Morals, Groundwork mein liye gaye positions ko develop aur kuch tareeko se mitigate (halka) karti hai.) Iske key tenets mein ye idea shaamil hai ki 'good will' hi ek unconditionally good cheez hai aur ki moral worth rakhne ke liye actions duty ke motive se kiye jaane chahiye. Emotions, feelings, aur inclinations, benevolent (bhale) bhi ho to, action ke moral worth mein kuch contribute nahi karte. Na hi actual na hi intended consequences kisi action ko koi moral worth de sakte hain, sirf duty ke liye kiya jaana hi de sakta hai. Famously wo likhte hain: 'Mujhe kabhi bhi aise act nahi karna chahiye siwaye is tarah se ki main ye bhi will kar sakoon ki mera maxim ek universal law ban jaaye.' Ek alag formulation mein, wo insist karte hain ki insaan ko humesha humanity ko sirf means ke roop mein nahi balki khud mein ek end ke roop mein bhi treat karna chahiye.

Is bahut brief sketch se bhi Aristotle ki theory se kuch seemingly sharp contrasts evident hain. Kant ke liye 'good will' unconditionally good cheez hai, Aristotle ke liye happiness. Jabki Aristotle assume karte hain ki apni khud ki happiness hi wo end hai jise aim karna rational hai, aur yahi wo cheez hai jisse phronimos concerned hai, Kant to yahan tak deny karte hain ki apni khud ki happiness jise wo 'pure practical reason' kehte hain uska koi proper concern honi chahiye. Ye bina shaq iska ek hissa is wajah se hai kyunki Kant happiness ke apne understanding mein Aristotle se different the. Aristotle ke liye virtuous aadmi wo hai jo apni good actions ko enjoy karta hai aur jiske paas appropriate feelings hain sahi act karne ke saath-saath. Iske contrast mein, Kant — effectively — us person ko moral worth dete hain jisse Aristotle sirf 'continent' kehte hain, kyunki (Kant ke account mein) jo matter karta hai wo ye hai ki kya agent duty se motivated hai, na ki unki feelings kya hain. Kant ye allow nahi kar sakte ki moral worth kisi bhi tarah non-rational appetites ya inclinations par depend kare. Aur halaanki dono thinkers ethical life mein reason ke role par important stress dete hain, ye har ek mein kaafi different form leta hai. Universality, Kant ke liye ek maxim ki morality ki hallmark hai. Aristotle, phir bhi, practical wisdom ke apne account mein, circumstances ki particularity par zyada emphasis dete hain, aur is baat par ki phronimos ko har case mein ethically salient features 'dekhna' zaroori hai.

### Consequentialism

Consequentialist theories, jinme utilitarianism sabse famous version hai, ek bahut different form leti hain. Jeremy Bentham aur J. S. Mill — jo Aristotle ke ek close reader the — utilitarianism ke sabse famous advocates hain. Jaisa humne dekha, Kant ki theory duty ke motive par emphasis deti hai aur consequences ko koi role deny karti hai — isi wajah se ise deontological theory maana jaata hai. Consequentialism, jaisa naam se pata chalta hai, actions ke consequences ko unki rightness ke liye ek hi relevant feature maanta hai. Utilitarianism ke liye, kisi action ko right banane wali cheez ye hai ki ye wo action hai (agent ke liye available sab actions mein se) jo general happiness ko maximize karta hai. Jahan tak ye maanta hai ki happiness hi ek intrinsically valuable cheez hai, ye Aristotle ki theory ke zyada close lagta hai. Lekin is baare mein kam se kam do major differences hain. Pehla, Mill happiness ko ek mental state, pleasure, se equate karte hain (halaanki pleasure ke evaluation mein quantity ke saath quality ko bhi admit karte hain). Aur zyada important, utilitarianism insist karti hai ki sab logo ki happiness — sentient non-humans (jaanwar) sameit — hi, na ki sirf agent ki happiness, right action ka criterion hai. Mill likhte hain, 'apni khud ki happiness aur dusron ki happiness ke beech utilitarianism usse ek disinterested aur benevolent spectator ki tarah strictly impartial hone ki demand karti hai' (Utilitarianism, ch. 2). Aur jabki ek consequentialist theory bahut saari virtues ko value karti hai aur unke cultivation ko encourage karti hai, ye unhe sirf instrumentally valuable maanti hai. Yani, ye us wajah se valuable hain jo consequences ye laane mein help karti hain, kyunki virtues right actions karne ki propensities (pravriti) hain, yani wo jo general happiness ko maximize karti hain.

Apne enormous differences ke bawajood, Kantian aur consequentialist ethical theories kuch features share karti hain. Dono primarily is baat se concerned hain ki actions ko right kya banata hai (ya, Kant ke terms mein, actions ko moral worth kya deta hai). Dono seemingly impartiality, ek certain disinterestedness, aur apne concerns se ek detachment require karti hain. Iska matlab ye nahi ki in theories ke liye morality sirf dusron ke saath humare relations ka matter hai: Kant maante hain ki khud ke prati bhi duties hoti hain, aur, consequentialism mein, agent ki apni happiness kisi aur ki jitni hi important hai, na kam na zyada. Lekin dono theories disinterestedness aur impartiality par ek aisa emphasis deti hain jo Aristotle ke us approach se sharply contrast karta hai jise hum agent-centred approach keh sakte hain. Halaanki unki theory kisi bhi tarah se narrowly egoistic nahi hai, ye certainly ego-centred zaroor hai.

### Neo-Aristotelian Virtue Theories

Mid-twentieth century se kai writers ne aisi theories banaane ki koshish ki hain jo Kantian aur consequentialist approaches ki kuch perceived objectionable features se bachein, aur aisa karte hue unhone un cheezon ka appeal kiya hai jo wo essentially Aristotelian themes maante hain. Is task ko approach karne ke do tareeke hain, dono hi best kism ki human life ke notion ko prominent banate hain. Ek approach ye hai ki is idea ko chhod diya jaaye ki ek ethical theory ko right action ka apna khud ka criterion dena hi hoga. Isse iske bajaye is sawaal par zor dena chahiye ki insaan ko kaisa person hona chahiye, aur kaise jeena chahiye, human nature aur shayad us community ki nature ko bhi dhyan mein rakhte hue jisme insaan rehta hai. Doosra tareeka ek aisi theory develop karna hai jo (pehle wale ki tarah) human flourishing aur virtues ko central role deti hai, lekin jo right action ka bhi ek criterion offer karne ka daava karti hai, jo upar discuss ki gayi dono rivals se bahut different kism ka hai.

Is doosre version ki 'Virtue Ethics' ke central tenets ye hain: (1) Ek virtuous action ko virtuous banane wali cheez ye hai ki ye wo hai jo ek virtuous agent relevant circumstances mein karega; (2) Ek virtuous agent wo hai jiske paas virtues hain; (3) Virtues wo character-traits hain jo ek insaan ko flourish karne dete hain, yani best life jeene dete hain. Ab (2) aur (3) ka Aristotelian provenance (mool) clear hai, aur (3) ek highly controversial claim hai, jahan tak ye assume karti hai ki virtues zaroor unhe possess karne wale ko benefit karti hain. Aisa nahi ki wo aise benefit karti hain jaise medicine ek sick person ko karti hai — kyunki healthy hona medicine ke bina bhi attain kiya ja sakta hai, jabki, question mein di gayi theory ke according, virtues ke bina flourishing (eudaimonia) possible hi nahi hai; ye possessor ke liye intrinsically, na ki instrumentally, beneficial hain. Lekin kya humein Aristotle ko (1) ka bhi credit dena chahiye?

'Haan' jawaab II.4 mein ek remark se suggest ho sakta hai: 'Actions, tab, just aur temperate kehlaate hain jab wo aisi hoti hain jaisi just ya temperate aadmi karega.' Lekin arguably is remark ka point kuch aur hai, jaisa context indicate karta hai. Is chapter ka purpose Aristotle ke us claim ko establish karna hai ki aap ek just act kar sakte hain bina abhi just person hue; unhe iski zaroorat hai apne is important view ke liye ki hum just acts karke hi just bante hain. Upar wale quotation mein wo shayad simply point ko drive home kar rahe hon, jaisa ki us quotation ke sequel se suggest hota hai. Agar wo (1) ke prati committed nahi hain, aur agar ye modern Virtue Ethics ke liye ek key tenet maana jaata hai — so-called virtues ki explanatory priority right ya virtuous actions ke upar — to us extent tak modern Virtue Ethics Aristotle ke intended se aage badh jaati hai.

Lekin agar hum deny karein ki Aristotle ne (1) hold kiya, to sawaal phir uthta hai: Aristotle ke liye, kisi diye gaye action ko just ya temperate action kya banata hai? Humne pehle hi dekha hai ki unki moral virtues ki theory is idea ko essentially appeal karti hai ki ek virtuous action wo hai jahan agent kisi given situation mein appropriate ('intermediate' ya 'mean') feel aur karta hai. Lekin, jaisa Aristotle khud admit karte hain (VI. 1), ye kisi aise insaan se zyada helpful nahi hai jo healthy hona chahta hai use ye kehna 'wahi karo jo medical science prescribe karti hai'. Aristotle ka ek aur important claim ye hai ki ek virtuous person kuch actions 'noble (kalon) ki wajah se' choose karta hai. Ye bhi baat clear nahi karta. 'Noble ke liye' act karna kya hai? Kya ek agent ko is thought se act karna hoga ki uska action noble hai? (Shayad nahi, lekin kam se kam 'pride' ki virtue wala aadmi to aisa karega, IV.3.) Aur act karne ke kaunse characteristics ise noble qualify karte hain? Humare text mein in sawaalon ke koi clear jawaab nahi hain, aur 'noble' ki koi systematic discussion nahi hai.

Lekin Aristotle, main sochta hoon, right action ke criterion ki demand ko outright reject kar denge. Wo expect karte hain ki ek well-brought-up person ko kaafi achha idea ho ki kisi bhi given situation mein achhe se act karne ke liye kaunse features aur considerations relevant hain, aur ye considerations kai aur various kism ke honge. Action ke kya consequences hain, khaas kar common good ke liye, ek factor hoga; jo relationship agent ka involved doosri parties ke saath hai, wo bhi crucial hoga. So-called self-regarding virtues ke liye, jaise temperance, wo ye obvious maanenge ki kuch kism ka indulgence ek good human life ke liye appropriate hi nahi hai, na sirf isliye ki ye kisi ki activities ko impair karta hai, balki apne aap mein bhi. Kuch cases mein, khaas kar justice mein, wo isse zyada bata paate hain ki ek action ko just kya banata hai, lekin us respect mein, jaisa wo khud admit karte hain (V. 5), justice doosri virtues se kuch different hai. Hum justice aur friendship par neeche wapas aayenge, Aristotle ke ethical theorizing ke method ki discussion ke baad.

## Aristotle's Method and Meta-ethical Assumptions

### Method

Work ki shuruaat mein hi (1.3) Aristotle apne method ke baare mein thoda kehte hain: wo un many opinions ko examine karenge jo sabse prevalent (prachalit) hain, aur arguable, yani plausible hain. Incontinence aur related 'affections' (VII.1) ki apni discussion introduce karne wale ek famous passage mein wo likhte hain:

*"Humein, jaise doosre sabhi cases mein, apparent facts humare saamne rakhne chahiye aur, pehle difficulties discuss karke, aage badh kar prove karna chahiye, agar possible ho, in affections of the mind ke baare mein sabhi common opinions ki truth, ya, agar ye nahi ho paaye, to zyadatar aur sabse authoritative opinions ki; kyunki agar hum difficulties resolve bhi karein aur common opinions ko undisturbed bhi chhode, to hum case ko sufficiently prove kar chuke honge."*

Lekin unke method ki ye important feature — iska dialectical (bahas-aadharit) character — bahut saare sawaal uthata hai. Humein pata hai ki wo common opinions ko explain karne ki koshish karte hain aur ye ki wo kyun clash karte hain, agar karte hain to. Lekin iss se kaisi theory milti hai? Kya ye sirf common sense morality hai jise regiment aur consistent banaya gaya hai? Kya everyday moral opinions ko carefully dekhna unhe undermine nahi kar sakta — jaise Plato ke kuch main speakers, jaise Thrasymachus, ke immoralist critiques ke manner mein? Aristotle kabhi bhi apni dialectical inquiry ke outcome ke roop mein aisa hone ka envisage nahi karte lagte. Kisi bhi surat mein, unke paas sirf common opinions se zyada tools hain, halaanki ye important zaroor hain. Wo apni doosri philosophical works se bhi kuch theses draw karenge. Humne pehle hi human nature aur human function (1.7) ke unke appeal ki discussion ki hai. Prominent bhi ye thesis hai ki activity ya actuality, potentiality ya capacity se superior hai: isi se ye explain hota hai ki happiness (ek activity) virtue (ek state, yani ek tarah ki potentiality) kyun nahi ho sakti. Soul ke parts (1.13 aur VI. 1) aur gods ki nature (X.7 aur 8) ke baare mein views bhi important hain.

### Meta-ethical Assumptions

Common opinions ko itna weight dene ke bawajood, Aristotle bilkul bhi ethics ke ek relativist ya subjectivist view ko adopt karne ke liye tempted nahi hain. Yani, wo na to ye maante hain ki right aur wrong sirf kisi diye gaye society ke relative ho sakte hain, na hi ye ki right aur wrong simply is baat ka matter hai ki koi ya koi group kya believe karta hai.

Unhone aise views encounter kiye the, aur inhe early on refer karte hain (I.3):

*"Ab noble aur just actions, jinhe political science investigate karti hai, itni variety aur fluctuation exhibit karti hain, ki inhe sirf convention se exist karne wali maana ja sakta hai, nature se nahi."*

Kuch thinkers ne sach mein ye conclusion nikala ki just aur noble (kalon) sirf convention se exist karte hain. Variety aur fluctuation jiski wo baat kar rahe hain wo do tarah ki hai: just ke baare mein views ki variety, aur ye fact ki just circumstance ke hisaab se fluctuate karta hai. Ye facts explain karte hain ki kyun kuch logo ne conclude kiya ki saari morality convention (nomos) ka matter hai, yani, logo ke believe karne ka, ya jo law mein enshrine kiya gaya hai, uska matter hai, aur ki 'nature se' kuch bhi right ya wrong nahi hai. V.7 mein wo is subject par wapas aate hain, is baar ye discuss karte hue ki kya justice simply nomos, law ya convention, ka matter hai, ya kya kuch cheezein nature se just ya unjust hain. Phir wo remark karte hain ki jo view ye suggest karta hai ki saari justice convention ka matter hai, wo justice ki variability ki recognition se prompt hota hai.

Response mein, Aristotle ek aisa position adopt karte hain jiske kaafi merit hain, aur ek mistake expose karte hain jo aaj bhi modern ethics discussions mein commonly paayi jaati hai. Modern terms mein rakhein, to wo moral objectivity insist karte hain, jabki universality deny karte hain. Jo view wo oppose karte hain wo sochta hai ki, universally true moral judgements ke bina, koi objectively true moral judgements nahi ho sakte. Aur ye, wo rightly point out karte hain, ek error hai. Right ya wrong, just ya unjust, ke baare mein judgements objectively true ya false ho sakte hain; unki truth sirf kisi ke unhe true believe karne se zyada kuch hai. Ye follow nahi hota, wo insist karte hain, ki ethical truths ka form 'debt ka payment withhold karna hamesha unjust hai' ya 'aapko hamesha apne father ki authority ko defer karna chahiye' jaisa hona chahiye. Chahe ye usually correct ho, exceptions ho sakte hain, jinhe phronimos, trained moral understanding wala insaan, recognize karega. Aapko ye insist karne ki zaroorat nahi hai ki X-karna hamesha ya universally wrong hai, ye maanne ke liye ki is occasion par X-karna wrong hoga, ye objectively true hai. Ye point unke equity (V.10) ki discussion mein develop kiya gaya hai. Is virtue mein ye recognize karna shaamil hai ki legal justice ko particular case ke suit karne ke liye kahan modify karne ki zaroorat hai.

In objective moral truths ka basis kya hai, ye ek difficult matter hai, aur, jaisa humne upar dekha, Aristotle iska koi clear jawaab nahi dete. Isme wo — arguably — kisi bhi doosre moral philosopher se zyada bura nahi karte. Lekin abhi describe ki gayi mistake ko nail karna (pakadna) ek signal achievement thi.

### Other-Regarding and Self-Regarding Concerns

Jaisa humne dekha hai, Aristotle maante hain ki apni khud ki happiness ko us end ke roop mein banana rational hai jisse main aim karta hoon. Unki ethics is baat ka study hai ki happiness kya hai aur ise kaise achieve kiya jaaye, aur virtues ke unke account mein assume hota hai ki virtues — moral aur intellectual dono — essentially, aisi states hain jo apne possessor ko benefit karti hain. Kya isse morality ka point miss nahi hota, kuch poochenge? Wo dusron ke liye concern ke liye kya room chhodte hain, us zaroorat ke liye ki wo humpar unke claims ko recognize karein, aur, kabhi-kabhi, unhe khud se aur apni khud ki happiness se priority dein?

In sawaalon ka poora jawaab is brief introduction mein nahi diya ja sakta, lekin do ab tak undiscussed topics ko consider karne se help milegi: justice aur friendship.

### Justice (Book V)

'Justice doosre ka good hai' Thrasymachus ne declare kiya (Plato, Republic 1), aur isliye wo isse have karne layak nahi declare karte hain. Aristotle pehli baat se agree karte hain, lekin phir bhi ise ek virtue maante hain. Ek virtue ke roop mein ye agent ke liye bhi good honi chahiye jitni doosron ke liye, halaanki Aristotle ye spell out nahi karte ki ye kaise hai. Iske bajaye, wo kuch important distinctions draw karte hain, jinme se kuch ne tab se justice ke accounts ko dominate kiya hai. Pehle wo 'universal' justice aur injustice ko 'particular' justice aur injustice se distinguish karte hain. Universal justice, wo kehte hain, virtue ka poora hissa hai uske other-regarding aspect mein. Particular justice ek specific kism ki virtue hai, jiski aage important subdivisions hain.

'Virtue ka poora hissa uske other-regarding aspect mein' ek interesting concept hai, ye suggest karta hai ki kisi tareeke se moral virtue ki saari branches, unhe bhi jinhe hum self-regarding maante hain jaise temperance, dusron ke saath humare relations involve karti hain. Lekin Aristotle is intriguing idea ko aage develop nahi karte. Iske bajaye wo 'particular justice' mein jaate hain, distributive justice — wo variety jo goods aur burdens ke fair distribution se deal karti hai — ko rectificatory justice se distinguish karte hue. Pehle mein wo ye crucial observation karte hain ki goods ko fairly share karne ka matlab involved parties ke relevant merits ko dhyan mein rakhna hai, aur un merits ke proportion mein goods ko share karna hai. Lekin wo ye discuss karne ki koshish nahi karte ki dhyan mein rakhne ka right basis kya hai. Aisa karke wo fair distribution ka ek formal lekin substantive nahi account dete hain. Doosre ke liye — rectificatory justice — just punishment discuss karne ke bajaye, jaisa hum expect kar sakte the, wo victim ke liye cheezein sahi karne par focus karte hain — isi se 'rectificatory' label aata hai. In chapters mein (kuch mathematical illustrations ki 'help' se) jo conceptual distinctions wo draw karte hain, wo important hain. Lekin hum vain mein justly act karne ki defence dhoondte hain, jo sceptic ki us worry ka jawaab de sake ki justly act karna doosron ko benefit karta hai, khud ko nahi.

### Friendship

Poori work ka ek-paanchwa hissa (Books VIII aur IX) friendship ko diya gaya hai, jo Aristotle ke liye iski importance ka nishaan hai. In books mein humein ethics mein other-regarding concerns ke role ki further exploration milti hai. Lekin ye un moralities se bahut different shape leta hai jo impartiality par zor deti hain. Unke account mein, jinhe hum apna friend consider kar sakte hain wo fellow citizens tak bhi extend ho sakte hain lekin agent se kuch relation crucial hai. ('Friends' term mein loved ones, jaise relatives, business associates, aur doosre bhi shaamil hain.) Ye idea ki humari dusron ke prati simply as such obligations ho sakti hain, Aristotle se, aur un sabhi moralities se jinse wo familiar the, completely absent hai. Best kism ki friendship, wo maintain karte hain, un logo ke saath friendship hai jinke liye hum well-wish karte hain aur jinke saath hum shared valuable activities mein time spend kar sakte hain, sab kuch unki virtue ki wajah se. Pleasure aur utility par based friendships bhi exist karti hain, wo allow karte hain, lekin sirf pehli kism perfect friendship hai.

Ek taraf hum is restriction se puzzled ho sakte hain, aur protest kar sakte hain ki true friendship un logo ke beech bhi exist kar sakti hai jo virtuous people nahi hain. Doosri taraf, humein lag sakta hai ki Aristotle abhi bhi ek zyada egocentric approach rakhte hain jab wo argue karte hain ki friends ke saath hamara relation kisi tarah khud ke saath hamare relation se derivative hai (IX.3). Self-love (IX.8) par ek important chapter mein humein wo ye recognize karte milte hain, aur poori tarah resolve nahi karte, apne egocentric approach aur apne is correct insistence ke beech ki tension ki friendship mein insaan doosre ke good ko us doosre ki khaatir wish karta hai.

Friendship wali books mein, Aristotle ki writings ke contrasting aspects bahut evident hain. Unki zyada theoretical discussions obscure aur strained lag sakti hain, jaise IX.9 mein unka convoluted proof jo dikhata hai ki happy person ko virtuous friends ki zaroorat kyun hai. Lekin bahut se points par, jaise chapters (VIII.13–IX.3) mein jo specific issues discuss karte hain — friendship ki casuistry — aur unki mothers ke apne bacchon ke liye pyaar ke baare mein insightful remarks mein, humein Aristotle friendship ki nature aur value ki ek sure touch aur zyada plausible grasp dikhaate milte hain.

## Aristotle's Moral Psychology

### Voluntary and Involuntary

Virtue ke students aur legislators, dono ko voluntariness ke basis ke baare mein jaanna zaroori hai (III. 1). Aristotle ne jo main outlines diye hain wo time ke test mein khare utre hain. Knowledge ki kami aur power ki kami (yani 'forced' hona), aaj ke law mein chief excusing conditions, wo criteria hain jo unhone kisi act ke voluntary na hone ke liye identify kiye. Wo force proper aur un cases mein important distinction draw karte hain jahan log claim karte hain ki wo compelled the, lekin jahan external force involved nahi hai. Ye doosre wale hain 'wo cheezein jo bade evils ke fear se ya kisi noble object ke liye ki jaati hain (jaise agar ek tyrant kisi ko kuch base karne ka order de, uske parents aur bacchon ko apne power mein rakhte hue, aur agar wo action karne se wo bach jaate lekin otherwise maar diye jaate)': kyunki insaan ne ye is wajah se kiya ki kuch aur bura na ho, ye actions na to forced hain, na hi involuntary. Inhe 'mixed, lekin voluntary jaisi zyada' label karte hue wo ek vital point add karte hain: chahe ye aapka act ho, aur isliye involuntary nahi, iska matlab ye nahi ki aapko ise karne ke liye blame kiya jaana chahiye. Praise (agar aapne cargo jettison (fenk) karke crew bachaya) ya pardon (agar aapne terrible pressure mein ek wrongful act kiya) appropriate ho sakta hai.

Ye discussion ki ignorance kab kisi act ko involuntary banati hai, complex hai, lekin isme ek important distinction hai. Excuse 'mujhe nahi pata tha' exculpate (dosh-mukt) karne ke liye sufficient nahi hai; jo matter karta hai wo ye hai ki ignorance aapki galti thi ya nahi (III.5). Isliye drunkenness ya anger ki wajah se ignorance mein kiye gaye acts involuntary nahi maane jaate, aur blame ke liye liable hain. III.5 mein Aristotle (sceptics ke against) ye dikhane ki koshish karte hain ki hum apne good aur bad characters ke liye bhi utne hi responsible hain jitne apne good aur bad actions ke liye. Ye moral responsibility ke basis ke modern problem ki discussion ke sabse close hai jo wo aate hain. Wo sceptics ke arguments ko strongly rakhte hain, aur, halaanki wo unhe dismiss karte hain, shayad apne conclusion formulate karte waqt unhe kuch concede (maan) karte hain: 'hum apne states of character ke somehow part-causes hain'.

### Choice

Ek law-court mein, aur praise ya blame ke liye, jo matter karta hai wo ye hai ki act voluntary tha ya nahi. Lekin virtue aur vice ke sawaalon ke liye jo matter karta hai wo ye hai ki aapke acts sirf voluntary hi nahi balki chosen bhi hain ya nahi. Choice, prohairesis, ek important lekin puzzling concept bhi hai. Ye humari everyday choice ki notion se narrower hai (aur sach mein Aristotle ke paas ek concept hai jo usse zyada close hai, hairesis). Ek bachcha choice manifest nahi kar sakta (is technical sense mein), aur jab main anger mein, ya incontinent tareeke se, act karta hoon, mere acts voluntary hote hain — isliye praise ya blame kiye ja sakte hain — lekin chosen nahi hote. Sirf ek adult jiske paas ek settled aur reasoned state of character hai, Aristotle ke sense mein choices bana sakta hai. Wo choice ki discussion karte hain (III.2-3), aur conclude karte hain ki ye 'humari power mein cheezon ka deliberate desire' hai. Virtue ko (II.6) ek state of character ke roop mein define kiya gaya jo choice se concerned hai, doosre shabdon mein, choices mein issue hota hai, aur vice bhi same hai (siwaye is ke ki ek ke choices good hote hain aur doosre ke bad). To, jaisa Aristotle admit karte hain (V.9), unjust hona kaafi mushkil hai! Bahut saare log virtuous acts karte hain bina abhi virtuous hue — jaise wo sab jo abhi virtuous hona seekh rahe hain (II. 1, 4). Unke acts praise kiye ja sakte hain, halaanki unhe abhi ek virtue possess karne ka credit nahi diya ja sakta. Aur bahut saare log bad acts karte hain bina vicious hue; unke bad acts voluntary hain, aur blame deserve karte hain, lekin choices nahi hain. Isके signal examples incontinent actions hain.

### Incontinence

Kya koi ye jaan sakta hai ki best kya hai, lekin us knowledge ke against act kar sakta hai, pleasure ya pain ya anger ya kisi aisi passion se overcome ho kar? Haan, kehte hain 'the many', yani zyadatar log; nahi, counter karte hain Socrates Plato ki dialogue Protagoras mein. Socrates ki theses ki virtue knowledge hai aur ki jo person best jaanta hai wo hamesha wahi karega, wo cheezein hain jinhe Aristotle kai jagah touch karte hain (VI.13, VII.1–3). Pehle hi II.4 mein wo argue kar chuke hain ki, virtue possess karne ke liye, jaanna itna important nahi tha jitna reliably choose karna, apne aap ke liye, virtuous actions. To hum expect karte hain ki wo Socrates ke against 'many' ke saath side lenge, aur common opinion accept karenge ki 'incontinent aadmi, ye jaante hue ki jo wo kar raha hai bad hai, passion ke result mein wahi karta hai' (VII.I). Wo certainly affirm karte hain ki incontinent actions voluntary aur blameworthy hain. Lekin robustly insist karne ke bajaye ki incontinent person poori knowledge mein act karta hai ki jo wo kar raha hai bad hai, wo different tareeko se jaanne ke, aur practical reasoning ke different parts (premisses) ke beech distinctions ka ek set develop karte hain. Ek point par — seemingly Socrates ke saath side lete hue — wo ek incontinent person ko un logo se compare karte hain jo asleep, mad, ya drunk hain. Ye seemingly is wajah se hai kyunki, strong passion ke influence mein — jaise ek aur drink ka desire — incontinent person ka knowledge (jaise, ki ye ek zyada hai) temporarily inaccessible ho jaata hai. Kya Aristotle kisi bhi point par ('the many' ke saath) allow karte hain ki incontinently act karte waqt kisi person ka knowledge aur reasoning unimpaired ho sakta hai? Is difficult chapter (VII.3) ke bahut diverse interpretations hain, aur different answers hain. Ek cheez clear hai: Aristotle apne aap ko us kisi bhi view (Plato ya Socrates ke) se door rakhna chahte hain jo saari vice ko incontinence ya self-control ki kami se equate karti hai. Vicious person ke ulat, incontinent ke paas right overall standards aur choices hain, aur, kam se kam is extent tak, ye jaanta hai ki jo wo abhi kar raha hai bad hai, chahe, kisi doosre tareeke se, act karne ke exact moment par, wo poori knowledge mein aisa nahi karta.

## Concluding Remarks

Kya ethics mein kaam ka relevance aur sach hona bhi sabhi historical periods mein ho sakta hai? Kuch writers ek broadly Aristotelian tradition mein — jaise A. MacIntyre — aisi ambition ko disown karte hain, ye maante hue ki best life ki koi bhi conception aur virtues kya hain, ye necessarily ek diye gaye historical period aur community mein grounded hoti hai. Aristotle aise koi qualms nahi dikhaate; wo ek aisi theory present karne mein concerned lagte hain jo apne scope mein zyada universal hai, human nature ke ek account par based, as such. Lekin, do aur half millennia baad aur morality aur moral theory mein developments ke light mein, aaj ke readers unke kuch assumptions aur values se struck, aur even appalled (chaunk jaana), ho sakte hain. Aristotle slavery, aur women ke liye ek lowly status, ko bina sawaal ke accept karte hain, halaanki apne bacchon ke liye women ke pyaar par unki remarks telling aur sympathetic hain. Translation 'man' ke frequent use ko avoid karne ki koshish nahi karta — jaise 'the truthful man', 'the boastful man', 'the good-tempered man', waghera mein. Kyunki jo virtues aur vices describe kiye gaye hain wo males ke hain, aur unki shuru se hi assumption ye hai ki best life sirf well-born, well-educated male citizens hi jee sakte hain jinhe apni khud ki living kamaane ki zaroorat nahi. Sirf yehi log un virtues (intellectual aur/ya moral) ko develop kar sakte hain jinki practice mein happiness ki life consist karti hai.

Aur virtues aur vices ki list mein humein kuch surprising inclusions milte hain, aur waise hi omissions bhi. Humne pehle hi dekha hai ki impartiality Aristotle ke liye koi ideal nahi thi, halaanki aaj ise kis extent tak ek value maana jaana chahiye, ye ek controversial matter hai. Waise hi ye idea ki humein suffering ko alleviate karne aur apne fellow men ki help karne ki koshish karni chahiye chahe wo humse kitne bhi remote hon, Aristotle ke liye, aur sach mein unke time ke sabhi logo ke liye, kaafi foreign hai. Na to kindness na hi cruelty ka mention milta hai, us proximity ke bawajood jo Aristotle ki ruthless conqueror Alexander se thi. Aristotle ke liye truthfulness ki virtue apni merits ke baare mein appropriately open hone ka matter hai, general truth-telling ka nahi. Do grand-scale virtues shayad pehli nazar mein sabse foreign lagti hain: magnificence — large-scale public donations appropriately karna (IV.2), aur megalopsuchia, literally 'greatness of soul' lekin yahan 'pride' translate ki gayi hai (IV.3). Properly proud aadmi great honour ke worthy hai aur khud ko aisa jaanta hai. Wo appropriately act karta hai; wo few deeds ka aadmi hai lekin great aur noble wale, ek slow step ke saath, ek deep voice, aur ek level utterance ke saath. Lekin yahan bhi modern readers ko kuch admirable features milte hain: proud aadmi apne saath kiye gaye wrongs ko overlook karta hai; apna mind freely bolta hai; equals ke beech apni dignity maintain karta hai lekin lower social status wale logo ke saath unassuming (bina dikhawe ke) hai; gossip nahi karta ya grudges nahi rakhta.

Bahut se readers ke liye Aristotle ki ethics ka sabse alien aspect shayad iska (X. 7, 8) philosophical contemplation ki life ko kisi bhi doosri life se upar, active citizen ki life sameit jo moral virtues develop aur manifest karta hai, ultimate elevation ho. Humne pehle hi work ke end mein is shayad surprising development par scholarly dispute note ki hai. Phir bhi, bahut saari traditions, Western aur Eastern dono, us life ko highest place dete hain jisme insaan apni zyada human nature aur human virtues ki zaroorat se transcend (paar) karta hai. Ek different perspective se, humein surprise ho sakta hai ki Aristotle kabhi bhi, best life ke ek candidate ke roop mein, poet ya dramatist, artist ya sculptor ki life consider nahi karte. Iska reason, main sochta hoon, ek metaphysical assumption mein milta hai: koi bhi activity jiska khud se aage ek end ho, isi wajah se un activities se kam valuable hai jo khud mein ends hain. Ek hi jhatke mein creativity ke saare forms demote (neeche) ho jaate hain, shaayad Aristotle ki apni productive investigations bhi jo sabhi philosophical matters mein hain. Phir bhi, unke is strange claim ke bawajood ki jo log jaante hain wo apna time zyada pleasantly bitaate hain un logo se jo inquire karte hain (X.7), hum shayad Aristotle ki inquiry ki life ke liye thankful ho sakte hain.

# NOTE ON THE TEXT AND TRANSLATION

Ye translation noted (mashhoor) Aristotle scholar, W. D. Ross, ki hai. Ye pehli baar 1925 mein The Works of Aristotle translated into English (Oxford University Press) ke volume ix mein publish hui thi. Ye Nicomachean Ethics ke Oxford Classical Text (ed. Bywater) par based hai, jisse, Ross ne likha tha, wo sirf occasionally hate the, jahan aisa lagta tha ki aisa karne se kaafi kuch gain ho sakta hai. Ye translation 1973 mein J. O. Urmson dwara revise ki gayi thi, aur 1980 mein World's Classics mein J. L. Ackrill ki further revisions ke saath publish hui.

Is present edition ke liye bahut kam aur changes kiye gaye hain. Pehle ke revisers ki tarah, current editor ne Ross ki central terminology ki translation ko unchanged chhoda hai, kuch bahut kam exceptions ke saath. Jaise, logos, jise Ross usually 'rational principle' ya 'rule' translate karte the, maine (editor) isse almost hamesha 'reason' se render kiya hai; aur maine orthos logos ko 'correct reason' translate kiya hai. III. 1 mein 'force' aur uske cognates ab consistently bia ke liye use kiye jaate hain, 'compelled'/'compulsion' anagkē ke cognates ke liye. Maine 'for the sake of the noble' substitute kiya hai jahan Ross aksar 'for honour's sake' rakhte the jab Greek mein kalon use hota tha. V. 5 mein literal translation 'need' ab chreia ke liye 'demand' ki jagah leta hai. Kuch jagahon par ek ab almost obsolete (purana pad chuka) term replace kiya gaya hai (jaise, 'draught' ki jagah 'drink', 'unguent' ki jagah 'perfume'). Assi saal se zyada pehle likhi gayi, ye translation aaj bhi justly admired (sahi maayno mein sarahi jaane wali) hai, aur isse bahut kam further adjustment ki zaroorat padi hai.

Numbering system, jaise 1098b10 jaise references, wahi hai jo sabhi modern editors, translators, aur commentators use karte hain. Ye numbers (Bekker numbers) Aristotle ke works ke 1831 ke Berlin edition se aaye hain. Marginal numbers Greek text ke corresponding hote hain; iska matlab hai ki translation mein kabhi-kabhi correspondence exact nahi hoti. Secondary literature use karne walon ke liye, Bekker line numbers ka hona phir bhi ek invaluable help hai. Jahan translation mein koi passage square brackets mein enclosed hai, us jagah ke corresponding Greek words ko Oxford Classical Text ke editor dwara ek marginal gloss (haashiye ki tippani) maana jaata hai.

Reference II.4, for example, Book II chapter 4 ko refer karta hai. Books mein division antiquity (puraane zamaane) tak jaata hai, jabki chapters mein division zyada recent hai. Kabhi-kabhi chapter breaks ek illogical (bemaani) point par aate hain.

Jo headings aur summaries use kiye gaye hain, saath hi translation ki body mein kabhi-kabhi aane wale numbers, (kuch jo maine revise kiye hain unhe chhod kar) wahi hain jo Ross ne introduce kiye the. Footnoted cross-references bhi Ross ki original translation mein supplied thi. Introduction ki tarah, explanatory endnotes is edition ke liye naye likhe gaye hain. Ye pehle wale notes se kaafi zyada fuller (bhare-pure) hain, lekin bahut hi occasionally maine verbatim (jaise ka taisa), acknowledgement ke saath, Ross ka original note quote kiya hai. Endnotes ko text mein ek asterisk se indicate kiya gaya hai.

Main J. L. Ackrill ki writings (aur lectures) ke prati, aur S. Broadie aur C. Rowe, aur T. H. Irwin ke kaam ke prati, unke respective translations aur notes mein, apna debt (rin/ehsaan) acknowledge karna chahunga, saath hi Books II–IV par C. C. W. Taylor ki commentary ke prati bhi.

Nicomachean Ethics antiquity se lekar aaj tak innumerable (anginat) commentaries ka subject rahi hai, khaas kar St Thomas Aquinas dwara; ek estimate ke according, Middle Ages se lekar aaj tak har decade mein lagbhag ek commentary aayi hai. Jo bhi is work par comment karne ki himmat karte hain, wo apne predecessors (pehle wale scholars) ki mehnat ke profound debt (gehra ehsaan) mein hain.

# OUTLINE OF THE NICOMACHEAN ETHICS 

## BOOK I. THE HUMAN GOOD

**Hamare inquiry ka subject**
1. Saari human activities kisi na kisi good ko aim karti hain: kuch goods doosron ke subordinate (adhin) hote hain.
2. Human good ki science politics hai.

**Science ki nature**
3. Humein subject-matter jitni precision (satikta) allow karta hai usse zyada expect nahi karni chahiye. Student ko discretion (samajh-boojh) ki umar tak pahunch chuka hona chahiye.

**Human good kya hai?**
4. Generally ye agree kiya jaata hai ki ye happiness hai, lekin happiness kya hai iske baare mein various views hain. Shuruaat mein jo chahiye wo facts ke baare mein ek unreasoned (bina reason ke) conviction hai, jaisi ek good upbringing se paida hoti hai.
5. Popular views ki discussion ki good pleasure hai, honour hai, wealth hai; ek chautha (fourth) kism ki life, contemplation ki, future discussion ke liye deferred (taal di gayi).
6. Philosophical view ki discussion ki good ka ek Form hota hai.
7. Good kuch aisa hona chahiye jo final aur self-sufficient ho. Insaan ke characteristic function ko consider karke happiness ki definition tak pahunche.
8. Hamari definition current beliefs se happiness ke baare mein confirm hoti hai.
9. Kya happiness learning ya habituation se acquire hoti hai, ya God dwara ya chance se milti hai?
10. Kya kisi insaan ko us ke jeete-ji happy nahi kaha jaana chahiye?
11. Kya zinda logo ki fortunes (kismat) dead logo ko affect karti hain?
12. Virtue praiseworthy hai, lekin happiness praise se bhi upar hai.

**Virtue ki kisms**
13. Soul ka division, aur uske result mein virtue ka intellectual aur moral mein division.

## BOOK II. MORAL VIRTUE

**Moral virtue, ye kaise acquire hoti hai**
1. Moral virtue, arts ki tarah, corresponding acts ki repetition se acquire hoti hai.
2. Ye acts exactly prescribe nahi kiye ja sakte, lekin excess aur defect se bachna chahiye.
3. Virtuous acts karne mein pleasure ek sign hai ki virtuous disposition acquire ho chuki hai: various considerations moral virtue ka pleasure aur pain ke saath essential connection dikhate hain.
4. Us view par ek objection ki insaan virtuous acts karke virtues acquire karta hai; aur ek reply: virtue possess karne aur usse act karne ke liye needed conditions.

**Moral virtue ki definition**
5. Iska genus: ye ek state of character hai, na ki ek passion, na hi ek capacity.
6. Iska differentia: ye 'intermediate' ko choose karne ki ek disposition hai. Do kism ke intermediate ko distinguish kiya gaya hai.
7. Upar wali proposition ko particular virtues ke reference se illustrate kiya gaya hai.

**Extreme aur mean states ki characteristics: practical corollaries**
8. Extremes ek doosre ke, aur mean ke, opposite hote hain.
9. Mean ko attain karna mushkil hai, aur ise perception se grasp kiya jaata hai, reasoning se nahi.

## BOOK III. MORAL VIRTUE (cont.)

**Voluntary aur involuntary actions, choice, responsibility**
1. Praise aur blame voluntary actions se attach hoti hain, yani wo actions jo (1) bina forced hue, aur (2) circumstances ki knowledge ke saath ki jaati hain.
2. Choice ko voluntary se distinguish kiya gaya hai: choice ka object previous deliberation ka result hota hai.
3. Deliberation ki nature aur uske objects: choice hamari khud ki power mein cheezon ka deliberate desire hai.
4. Rational wish ka object end hai, yani good ya apparent good.
5. Virtue aur vice hamari power mein hain.

**Courage**
6. Courage fear aur confidence ki feelings se concerned hai — strictly speaking, battle mein death ke fear se.
7. Courage ka motive noble hai: opposite vices, cowardice aur rashness, ki characteristics.
8. Paanch (five) kism ke courage jo improperly courage kehlaate hain.
9. Courage ka pain aur pleasure se relation.

**Temperance**
10. Temperance sirf kuch touch ki pleasures tak limited hai.
11. Temperance aur uske opposites, self-indulgence aur 'insensibility', ki characteristics.
12. Self-indulgence, cowardice se zyada voluntary hai: self-indulgent aadmi ki spoilt child se comparison.

## BOOK IV. MORAL VIRTUE (cont.)

**Money se concerned virtues**
1. Liberality (udaarta).
2. Magnificence (bhavyata).

**Honour se concerned virtues**
3. Pride.
4. Ambition aur unambitiousness ke beech ka intermediate virtue.

**Anger se concerned virtue**
5. Good temper.

**Social intercourse ki virtues**
6. Friendliness.
7. Truthfulness.
8. Ready wit.

**Ek quasi-virtue**
9. Shame.

## BOOK V. JUSTICE

**Justice: iska sphere aur outer nature: kis sense mein ye ek mean hai**
1. Just as the lawful (universal justice) aur just as the fair and equal (particular justice): pehla wala consider kiya gaya hai.
2. Just as the fair and equal: distributive aur rectificatory justice mein divided.
3. Distributive justice, geometrical proportion ke according.
4. Rectificatory justice, arithmetical proportion ke according.
5. Exchange mein justice, reciprocity.
6. Political justice aur analogous kism ki justice.
7. Natural aur legal justice.

**Justice: iski inner nature, jisme choice involve hai**
8. Wrongdoing ke degrees ka scale.
9. Kya kisi insaan ke saath voluntarily unjustly treat kiya ja sakta hai? Kya distribution mein injustice ka guilty distributor hai ya recipient? Justice utni aasan nahi hai jitni lag sakti hai, kyunki ye acting ka ek tarika nahi balki ek inner disposition hai.
10. Equity, legal justice ka ek corrective.
11. Kya koi insaan khud ke saath unjustly treat kar sakta hai?

# BOOK I · THE HUMAN GOOD 

## SUBJECT OF OUR INQUIRY

**Saari human activities kisi na kisi good ko aim karti hain: kuch goods doosron ke subordinate hote hain**

**1.** Har art aur har inquiry, aur isi tarah har action aur choice, kisi na kisi good ko aim karti hai maani jaati hai; aur isi wajah se good ko sahi tareeke se ye declare kiya gaya hai ki good wahi hai jise saari cheezein aim karti hain. Lekin ends (maqsad) mein ek certain difference paayi jaati hai; kuch activities hoti hain, kuch products hote hain jo unhe produce karne wali activities se alag hote hain. Jahan actions se alag ends hote hain, wahan products ka nature activities se better hone ka hota hai. Ab, kyunki bahut saari actions, arts, aur sciences hain, unke ends bhi bahut saare hain; medical art ka end health hai, shipbuilding ka ek vessel, strategy ka victory, economics ka wealth. Lekin jahan aisi arts ek single capacity ke under aati hain — jaise bridle-making aur horses ke equipment se related doosri arts riding ki art ke under aati hain, aur ye aur har military action strategy ke under — usi tarah doosri arts bhi aur doosri arts ke under aati hain — in sab mein master arts ke ends subordinate ends se preferred hote hain; kyunki inhi (master arts ke ends) ki khaatir subordinate ends pursue kiye jaate hain. Koi farak nahi padta ki activities khud actions ki ends hain, ya kuch aur jo activities se alag hai, jaise abhi mention ki gayi sciences ke case mein.

**Human good ki science politics hai**

**2.** Agar, to, hum jo cheezein karte hain unka koi end hai, jise hum khud ke liye desire karte hain (baaki sab kuch iski khaatir desire kiya jaata hai), aur agar hum har cheez kisi aur cheez ki khaatir choose nahi karte (kyunki us hisaab se ye process infinity tak jaata rahega, isliye hamari desire empty aur vain hogi), to clearly yahi good aur chief good hona chahiye. To kya iska knowledge life par ek bada influence nahi rakhega? Kya hum, un archers ki tarah jinka ek mark (nishaana) hota hai jise wo aim karte hain, jo right hai use hit karne ki zyada possibility nahi rakhenge? Agar aisa hai, to humein koshish karni chahiye, kam se kam outline mein, ye determine karne ki ki ye kya hai, aur ye kis science ya capacity ka object hai. Ye sabse authoritative art ka lagta hai aur wo jo sabse zyada truly master art hai. Aur politics is nature ki lagti hai; kyunki yahi hai jo ye ordain karta hai ki kaunsi sciences ek state mein study honi chahiye, aur citizens ke har class ko kya seekhna chahiye aur kis point tak seekhna chahiye; aur hum dekhte hain ki sabse zyada esteemed capacities bhi isi ke under aati hain, jaise strategy, economics, rhetoric; ab, kyunki politics baaki sciences ko use karti hai, aur, phir se, kyunki ye legislate karti hai ki humein kya karna hai aur kya se abstain karna hai, is science ka end doosron ke ends ko include karna chahiye, isliye ye end human good hona chahiye. Kyunki agar end ek single insaan ke liye aur ek state ke liye same bhi ho, state ka end har surat mein kuch bada aur zyada complete lagta hai chahe attain karna ho ya preserve karna ho; halaanki ek insaan ke liye is end ko attain karna worthwhile hai, ek nation ya city-states ke liye ise attain karna finer aur zyada godlike hai. Ye, to, wo ends hain jinhe hamari inquiry aim karti hai, kyunki ye political science hai, us term ke ek sense mein.

## NATURE OF THE SCIENCE

**Humein subject-matter jitni precision allow karta hai usse zyada expect nahi karni chahiye. Student ko discretion ki umar tak pahunch chuka hona chahiye**

**3.** Hamari discussion adequate hogi agar isme utni hi clearness ho jitni subject-matter allow karti hai, kyunki precision saari discussions mein equally seek nahi ki jaani chahiye, jaise crafts ke saare products mein bhi nahi hoti. Ab noble aur just actions, jinhe political science investigate karti hai, itni variety aur fluctuation exhibit karti hain, ki inhe sirf convention se exist karne wali maana ja sakta hai, nature se nahi. Lekin goods bhi ek similar fluctuation exhibit karte hain kyunki wo bahut logo ko harm pahunchate hain; kyunki pehle bhi log apni wealth ki wajah se undo (barbaad) hue hain, aur doosre apni courage ki wajah se. Humein, to, aise subjects ke baare mein aur aise premisses ke saath baat karte hue content rehna chahiye truth ko roughly aur outline mein indicate karne mein, aur un cheezon ke baare mein baat karte hue jo sirf most part true hain, aur usi kism ke premisses ke saath, aise conclusions tak pahunchne mein jo isse better na hon. Isi spirit mein, isliye, har type ki statement receive ki jaani chahiye; kyunki ye ek educated aadmi ki nishaani hai ki wo har class of things mein precision dhoondhe sirf utni hi jitni subject ki nature allow karti hai; ye equally foolish hai ek mathematician se probable reasoning accept karna aur ek rhetorician se demonstrative proofs demand karna.

Ab har aadmi un cheezon ko achhe se judge karta hai jo wo jaanta hai, aur inka wo ek good judge hai. Aur isliye jis aadmi ne kisi subject mein education paayi hai wo us subject ka ek good judge hai, aur jis aadmi ne all-round education paayi hai wo generally ek good judge hai. Isliye ek jawaan aadmi political science ke lectures ka proper hearer nahi hai; kyunki wo un actions mein inexperienced hai jo life mein hoti hain, lekin iski discussions inhi se shuru hoti hain aur inhi ke baare mein hoti hain; aur, further, kyunki wo apni passions ko follow karne ki tendency rakhta hai, uska study vain aur unprofitable hoga, kyunki jo end aim kiya gaya hai wo knowledge nahi balki action hai. Aur koi farak nahi padta ki wo years mein jawaan hai ya character mein youthful; defect time par depend nahi karta, balki uske jeene ke tareeke par, aur har successive object ko pursue karne par, jaise passion direct karti hai. Kyunki aise persons ke liye, jaise incontinent ke liye, knowledge koi profit nahi laati; lekin jo log reason ke according desire aur act karte hain, unke liye aise matters ke baare mein knowledge bahut great benefit ki hogi.

Student ke baare mein ye remarks, treatment ka kism jo expect kiya jaana chahiye, aur inquiry ka purpose, hamari preface maani ja sakti hain.

## WHAT IS THE HUMAN GOOD?

**Ye generally agree kiya jaata hai ki ye happiness hai, lekin happiness kya hai iske baare mein various views hain. Shuruaat mein jo chahiye wo facts ke baare mein ek unreasoned conviction hai, jaisi ek good upbringing se paida hoti hai**

**4.** Chaliye hamari inquiry resume karein aur, is fact ko dekhte hue ki saari knowledge aur har pursuit kisi na kisi good ko aim karti hai, state karein ki wo kya hai jise humne kaha ki political science aim karti hai aur action se achievable sabhi goods mein sabse highest kya hai. Verbally is par bahut general agreement hai; kyunki general run ke log bhi aur superior refinement wale log bhi kehte hain ki ye happiness hai, aur living well aur faring well ko being happy ke saath identify karte hain; lekin happiness kya hai, is par wo differ karte hain, aur many log wahi account nahi dete jo wise log dete hain. Kyunki pehle wale sochte hain ki ye kuch plain aur obvious cheez hai, jaise pleasure, wealth, ya honour; wo, phir bhi, ek doosre se differ karte hain — aur aksar to same aadmi bhi ise different cheezon se identify karta hai, health se jab wo bimaar hota hai, wealth se jab wo poor hota hai; lekin, apni ignorance ke conscious hote hue, wo un logo ko admire karte hain jo koi great cheez proclaim karte hain jo unki comprehension se upar hai. Ab kuch log sochte the ki in bahut saare goods ke alawa ek aur good hai jo khud mein good hai aur in sab ki goodness ka cause bhi hai. Saari opinions ko examine karna jo hold ki gayi hain shayad kuch fruitless hoga; unhe examine karna kaafi hai jo sabse prevalent hain ya jo arguable lagti hain.

Humein ye notice karna nahi bhoolna chahiye, phir bhi, ki first principles se aane wale arguments aur unki taraf jaane wale arguments mein ek difference hai. Kyunki Plato bhi, ye sawaal uthane mein right the aur poochte the, jaisa wo aksar karte the, 'Kya hum first principles se aa rahe hain ya unki taraf jaa rahe hain?' Ek difference hai, jaisa ek racecourse mein judges se turning-point tak ke course aur wapas jaane ke raaste mein hota hai. Kyunki, jabki humein us cheez se shuru karna chahiye jo evident hai, cheezein do tareeko se evident hoti hain — kuch humare liye, kuch bina qualification ke. Presumably, to, humein un cheezon se shuru karna chahiye jo humare liye evident hain. Isliye jo bhi noble aur just ke baare mein, aur generally political science ke subjects ke baare mein, intelligently lectures sunna chahta hai, use good habits mein brought up hona chahiye. Kyunki fact hi ek starting-point hai, aur agar ye usse sufficiently plain hai, to use reason ki bhi zaroorat nahi padegi; aur jis aadmi ko well brought up kiya gaya hai uske paas starting-points hain ya wo unhe aasaani se pa sakta hai. Aur uske liye jiske paas na ye hain na wo pa sakta hai, use Hesiod ke shabd sunne dein:

*"Sabse best wahi hai jo sab kuch khud jaanta hai; Achha, wo jo sunta hai jab log sahi advice dete hain; Lekin jo na jaanta hai, na hi dil se sunta hai kisi aur ki wisdom, wo ek useless insaan hai."*

**Popular views ki discussion ki good pleasure hai, honour hai, wealth hai; ek chautha kism ki life, contemplation ki, future discussion ke liye deferred**

**5.** Chaliye, phir bhi, hamari discussion us point se resume karein jahan se hum digress hue the. Jo lives log jeete hain unse judge karein, to zyadatar log, aur sabse vulgar type ke log, (bina kuch ground ke nahi) good, ya happiness, ko pleasure se identify karte lagte hain; yahi wajah hai ki wo enjoyment ki life ko pyaar karte hain. Kyunki, hum keh sakte hain, teen prominent kism ki lives hain — wo jo abhi mention ki gayi, political, aur teesri contemplative life. Ab mankind ka mass evidently apne tastes mein kaafi slavish (gulaam-jaisa) hai, aur ek life jo beasts (jaanwaron) ke suitable hai preferer karta hai, lekin unhe apne view ke liye kuch ground milta hai is fact se ki high places mein bahut se log Sardanapallus ke tastes share karte hain. Life ke prominent kisms ka ek consideration dikhaata hai ki superior refinement aur active disposition wale log happiness ko honour se identify karte hain; kyunki ye, roughly speaking, political life ka end hai. Lekin ye jo hum dhoondh rahe hain uske liye too superficial lagta hai, kyunki ye maana jaata hai ki ye un logo par depend karta hai jo honour dete hain, na ki us par jo ise receive karta hai, lekin humein lagta hai ki good kuch aisa hona chahiye jo apna ho aur jise aasaani se le liya na ja sake. Further, log honour is liye pursue karte lagte hain taaki wo apni merit ke assured ho sakein; kam se kam ye practical wisdom wale log hain jo doosron dwara honoured hona seek karte hain, aur un logo ke beech jo unhe jaante hain, aur unki virtue ke ground par; clearly, to, unke according, kam se kam, virtue better hai. Aur shayad koi ye bhi suppose kar sakta hai ki honour ki jagah yahi political life ka end hai. Lekin ye bhi kuch incomplete lagta hai; kyunki virtue ka possession asleep hone ke saath bhi compatible lagta hai, ya lifelong inactivity ke saath, aur, further, sabse great sufferings aur misfortunes ke saath bhi; lekin jo aadmi aise jee raha ho use koi bhi happy nahi kahega, jab tak wo har cost par ek thesis maintain na kar raha ho. Lekin isse kaafi ho gaya; kyunki ye subject popular discussions mein bhi sufficiently treat kiya jaa chuka hai. Teesra, contemplative life aati hai, jise hum baad mein consider karenge.

Money-making ki life ek aisi life hai jo compulsion ke under undertake ki jaati hai, aur wealth evidently wo good nahi hai jise hum dhoondh rahe hain; kyunki ye sirf useful hai aur kisi aur cheez ki khaatir hai. Aur isliye koi upar diye gaye objects ko hi ends maan sakta hai; kyunki unhe khud ke liye pyaar kiya jaata hai. Lekin ye evident hai ki ye bhi end nahi hain; phir bhi bahut saare arguments inki support mein waste kiye gaye hain. Chaliye is subject ko yahin chhod dete hain.

**Discussion of the philosophical view that there is a Form of good**

**6.** Humein shayad universal good ko consider karna chahiye aur thoroughly discuss karna chahiye ki isse kya matlab hai, halaanki aisi inquiry ek uphill (mushkil) ban jaati hai is fact se ki Forms ko humare apne dosto ne introduce kiya hai. Phir bhi ye shayad better maana jaana chahiye, sach mein hamara duty hona chahiye, ki truth maintain karne ki khaatir hum us cheez ko bhi destroy karein jo humein closely touch karti hai, khaas kar jab hum philosophers ya wisdom ke lovers hain; kyunki, halaanki dono dear hain, piety humein friends se zyada truth ko honour karne ki demand karti hai.

Jin logo ne ye doctrine introduce kiya, unhone un classes ke Ideas posit (sthapit) nahi kiye jinme unhone priority aur posteriority recognize ki (yahi wajah hai ki unhone saare numbers ko embrace karne wale ek Idea ke existence ko maintain nahi kiya); lekin 'good' term substance ki category mein, quality ki category mein, aur relation ki category mein bhi use hota hai, aur jo per se hai, yani substance, wo relative se nature mein prior hai (kyunki doosra ek offshoot aur accident of being jaisa hai); isliye in sab goods par ek common Idea set nahi ho sakta. Further, kyunki 'good' ke utne hi senses hain jitne 'being' ke (kyunki ye substance ki category mein predicate hota hai, jaise god aur reason ke, aur quality mein, yani virtues ke, aur quantity mein, yani jo moderate hai uske, aur relation mein, yani useful ke, aur time mein, yani right opportunity ke, aur place mein, yani right locality aur usi jaisi cheezon ke), clearly ye kuch aisa nahi ho sakta jo universally sabhi cases mein present ho aur single ho; kyunki tab ye sabhi categories mein predicate nahi hota, sirf ek mein hi hota. Further, kyunki jo cheezein ek Idea ke corresponding hoti hain unki ek science hoti hai, to saari goods ki bhi ek hi science hoti; lekin jaisa ki hai, un cheezon ki bhi bahut saari sciences hain jo ek hi category ke under aati hain, jaise opportunity ki, kyunki war mein opportunity strategics se study hoti hai aur disease mein medicine se, aur food mein jo moderate hai wo medicine se study hota hai aur exercise mein gymnastics ki science se. Aur koi ye sawaal pooch sakta hai ki 'a thing itself' se unka matlab kya hai, agar (jaisa case hai) 'man himself' mein aur ek particular man mein man ka account ek hi same hai. Kyunki jahan tak wo insaan hain, wo kisi bhi respect mein differ nahi karenge; aur agar aisa hai, to 'good itself' aur particular goods bhi, jahan tak wo good hain, differ nahi karenge. Lekin phir bhi ye eternal hone ki wajah se koi zyada good nahi hoga, kyunki jo lambe samay tak rehta hai wo us se zyada white nahi hai jo ek din mein perish ho jaata hai. Pythagoreans ek zyada plausible account dete lagte hain good ka, jab wo One ko goods ke column mein rakhte hain; aur unhi ko Speusippus follow karta lagta hai.

Lekin chaliye in matters ko kahin aur discuss karein; jo humne kaha hai uspar ek objection, phir bhi, is fact mein discern (pehchana) ja sakta hai ki Platonists saare goods ke baare mein baat nahi kar rahe the, aur ki jo goods khud ke liye pursue aur pyaar kiye jaate hain unhe ek single Form ke reference se good kaha jaata hai, jabki jo inhe produce karne ya preserve karne ya inke contraries ko prevent karne ki tendency rakhte hain unhe in ki wajah se, aur ek different tareeke se, good kaha jaata hai. Clearly, to, goods ke baare mein do tareeko se baat honi chahiye, aur kuch khud mein good hone chahiye, doosre in ki wajah se. Chaliye, to, khud mein good cheezon ko useful cheezon se alag karein, aur consider karein ki kya pehli wali ek single Idea ke reference se good kahi jaati hain. Kis kism ke goods ko koi khud mein good kahega? Kya wo jo pursue kiye jaate hain doosron se isolated hone par bhi, jaise intelligence, sight, aur kuch pleasures aur honours? Certainly, agar hum inhe bhi kisi aur cheez ki khaatir pursue karte hain, phir bhi koi inhe khud mein good cheezon mein rakhega. Ya kya good ke Idea ke alawa aur kuch bhi khud mein good nahi hai? Us case mein Form empty ho jayega. Lekin agar jo cheezein humne naam li hain wo bhi khud mein good cheezein hain, to good ka account un sab mein kuch identical dikhana padega, jaise whiteness ka account snow mein aur white lead mein identical hai. Lekin honour, wisdom, aur pleasure ke, unki goodness ke respect mein, accounts distinct aur diverse hain. Good, isliye, ek hi Idea ke corresponding kuch common cheez nahi hai.

Lekin phir humara matlab good se kya hai? Ye surely un cheezon jaisa nahi hai jinka sirf naam same hota hai chance se. Kya goods ek hain, to, ek good se derive hone ki wajah se ya sab ek good mein contribute karne ki wajah se, ya wo analogy se ek hain? Certainly, jaise sight body mein hai, waise hi reason soul mein hai, aur doosre cases mein bhi aisa hi hai. Lekin shayad ye subjects abhi ke liye dismiss kar diye jaayein; kyunki inke baare mein perfect precision philosophy ki kisi aur branch ke liye zyada appropriate hogi. Aur similarly Idea ke baare mein bhi; agar koi ek good hai bhi jo universally goods ka predicable hai, ya jiska separate aur independent existence ho sakta hai, clearly ye insaan dwara achieve ya attain nahi kiya ja sakta; lekin hum ab kuch attainable dhoondh rahe hain. Shayad, phir bhi, koi ye soche ki iska knowledge rakhna worthwhile hai attainable aur achievable goods ke reference mein; ya, ise ek tarah ka pattern maan kar, hum un goods ko behtar jaanenge jo humare liye good hain, aur agar hum unhe jaanenge to unhe attain kar payenge. Is argument mein kuch plausibility hai, lekin ye sciences ki procedure se clash karta lagta hai; kyunki inme se sabhi, halaanki wo kisi na kisi good ko aim karte hain aur iski deficiency ko supply karne ki koshish karte hain, good ka knowledge ek taraf chhod dete hain. Phir bhi ye probable nahi hai ki arts ke saare exponents is itni badi aid se ignorant hon, aur ise seek bhi na karein. Ye samajhna bhi mushkil hai ki ek weaver ya carpenter ko apne craft ke respect mein is 'good itself' ko jaan kar kya benefit hoga, ya jisne Idea itself dekhi hai wo isse ek better doctor ya general kaise banega. Kyunki ek doctor is tarah se health ka study nahi karta lagta, balki insaan ki health, ya shayad ek particular insaan ki health; ye individuals hain jinhe wo heal kar raha hai. Lekin in topics par kaafi baat ho gayi.

**Good kuch aisa hona chahiye jo final aur self-sufficient ho. Insaan ke characteristic function ko consider karke happiness ki definition tak pahunche**

**7.** Chaliye phir se us good ki taraf wapas aayein jise hum dhoondh rahe hain, aur poochein ki ye kya ho sakta hai. Ye different actions aur arts mein different lagta hai; ye medicine mein different hai, strategy mein, aur doosri arts mein bhi. To har ek ka good kya hai? Surely wahi jiski khaatir baaki sab kuch kiya jaata hai. Medicine mein ye health hai, strategy mein victory, architecture mein ek house, kisi aur sphere mein kuch aur, aur har action aur pursuit mein ye end hai; kyunki isi ki khaatir sabhi log wo sab kuch karte hain jo wo karte hain. Isliye, agar hum jo kuch karte hain uska koi end hai, ye action se achievable good hoga, aur agar ek se zyada hain, to ye action se achievable goods honge.

To argument ne ek different course se same point tak pahunch liya hai; lekin humein ise aur bhi clearly state karne ki koshish karni chahiye. Kyunki evidently ek se zyada ends hain, aur hum inme se kuch (jaise wealth, flutes, aur generally instruments) kisi aur cheez ki khaatir choose karte hain, clearly saare ends final ends nahi hain; lekin chief good evidently kuch final hai. Isliye, agar sirf ek hi final end hai, ye wahi hoga jise hum dhoondh rahe hain, aur agar ek se zyada hain, to inme se sabse zyada final hi wo hoga jise hum dhoondh rahe hain. Ab hum us cheez ko jo khud mein pursuit ke worthy hai, kisi aur cheez ki khaatir worthy of pursuit cheez se zyada final kehte hain, aur wo cheez jo kabhi kisi aur cheez ki khaatir desirable nahi hoti wo un cheezon se zyada final hai jo khud mein bhi aur us doosri cheez ki khaatir bhi desirable hain, aur isliye hum us cheez ko bina qualification ke final kehte hain jo hamesha khud mein desirable hai aur kabhi kisi aur cheez ki khaatir nahi.

Ab aisi cheez happiness hi, sab se zyada, maani jaati hai; kyunki ise hum hamesha khud ke liye choose karte hain aur kisi aur cheez ki khaatir kabhi nahi, lekin honour, pleasure, reason, aur har virtue ko hum sach mein khud ke liye choose karte hain (kyunki agar inse kuch bhi na milta, phir bhi hum inme se har ek ko choose karte), lekin hum inhe happiness ki khaatir bhi choose karte hain, ye judge karte hue ki inke through hum happy honge. Happiness, doosri taraf, koi bhi in cheezon ki khaatir choose nahi karta, na hi, generally, khud ke alawa kisi aur cheez ki khaatir.

Self-sufficiency ke point of view se bhi same result follow karta lagta hai; kyunki final good ko self-sufficient maana jaata hai. Ab self-sufficient se humara matlab wo nahi jo ek insaan ke liye akela sufficient ho, us aadmi ke liye jo solitary life jeeta hai, balki parents, children, wife ke liye bhi, aur generally apne friends aur fellow citizens ke liye bhi, kyunki insaan citizenship ke liye born hota hai. Lekin isme kuch limit set hona chahiye; kyunki agar hum apni requirement ko ancestors aur descendants aur friends ke friends tak extend karein to hum ek infinite series mein phas jaayenge. Chaliye is sawaal ko kisi aur occasion par examine karein; self-sufficient ko hum ab define karte hain us cheez ke roop mein jo isolated hone par bhi life ko desirable aur kisi cheez ki kami se rahit banati hai; aur aisi hi hum happiness ko maante hain; aur further hum ise sab cheezon mein sabse zyada desirable maante hain, na ki ek good cheez jo doosron ke saath count ki jaati ho — agar aisa count kiya jaata to clearly ye kam se kam ek chhote se good ke addition se zyada desirable ban jaata; kyunki jo add hota hai wo goods ka ek excess ban jaata hai, aur goods mein se greater hamesha zyada desirable hota hai. Happiness, to, kuch final aur self-sufficient hai, aur action ka end hai.

Presumably, phir bhi, ye kehna ki happiness chief good hai ek platitude (ghisi-piti baat) lagta hai, aur ye kya hai iska ek clearer account abhi bhi chahiye. Ye shayad diya ja sakta hai, agar hum pehle insaan ka function ascertain kar sakein. Kyunki jaise ek flute-player, ek sculptor, ya kisi bhi artist ke liye, aur, generally, un sab cheezon ke liye jinka koi function ya activity hai, good aur 'well' function mein reside karta maana jaata hai, waise hi ye insaan ke liye bhi lagega, agar uska koi function hai. To kya carpenter aur tanner ke certain functions ya activities hain, aur insaan ke koi nahi? Kya wo bina function ke born hota hai? Ya jaise eye, hand, foot, aur generally har part ka evidently ek function hai, kya koi ye lay down kar sakta hai ki insaan ka bhi similarly in sab se alag ek function hai? To ye kya ho sakta hai? Life plants mein bhi belong karti lagti hai, lekin hum wo dhoondh rahe hain jo insaan ke liye peculiar hai. Chaliye, isliye, nutrition aur growth ki life ko exclude karein. Uske baad perception ki ek life hogi, lekin ye bhi horse, ox, aur har animal se share hoti lagti hai. Reh jaata hai, to, reason rakhne wale element ki ek active life; iska, ek part reason ke obedient hone ke sense mein hai, doosra reason possess karne aur thought exercise karne ke sense mein. Aur, kyunki 'rational element ki life' ke bhi do meanings hain, humein state karna chahiye ki activity ke sense mein life hi wo hai jo humara matlab hai; kyunki ye term ka zyada proper sense lagta hai. Ab agar insaan ka function soul ki ek aisi activity hai jo reason ko follow ya imply karti hai, aur agar hum 'a so-and-so' aur 'a good so-and-so' kehte hain jinka function ek hi kind mein same hai, jaise ek lyre-player aur ek good lyre-player, aur bina qualification ke sab cases mein aisa hi, goodness ke respect mein eminence function ke naam mein add hoti hai (kyunki lyre-player ka function lyre bajaana hai, aur ek good lyre-player ka use achhi tarah karna hai): agar ye case hai [aur hum insaan ke function ko ek certain kism ki life batate hain, aur ise ek activity ya soul ki actions batate hain jo ek rational principle imply karti hain, aur ek good insaan ke function ko in cheezon ka good aur noble performance batate hain, aur agar koi action tab well performed hoti hai jab ye appropriate virtue ke according perform ki jaati hai: agar ye case hai], human good ek aisi soul ki activity ban jaata hai jo virtue exhibit karti hai, aur agar ek se zyada virtues hain, to best aur most complete ke according.

Lekin humein 'a complete life' bhi add karna chahiye. Kyunki ek swallow (chidiya) se summer nahi ban jaata, na hi ek din se; aur isi tarah ek din, ya thoda time, ek insaan ko blessed aur happy nahi banata.

Ye good ki ek outline maani jaaye; kyunki humein presumably pehle roughly sketch karna chahiye, aur baad mein details fill karni chahiye. Lekin ye lagta hai ki koi bhi insaan wo aage badha aur articulate kar sakta hai jo ek baar well outlined ho chuka hai, aur time aisi kaam mein ek good discoverer ya partner hota hai; isi wajah se arts ki advances hoti hain; kyunki koi bhi jo kami hai use add kar sakta hai. Aur humein ye bhi yaad rakhna chahiye jo pehle kaha gaya hai, aur sab cheezon mein equally precision nahi dhoondhni chahiye, balki har class of things mein utni hi precision jitni subject-matter ke according hai, aur utni hi jitni inquiry ke appropriate hai. Kyunki ek carpenter aur ek geometer right angle ko different tareeko se investigate karte hain; pehla waisa karta hai jahan tak right angle uske kaam ke liye useful hai, jabki doosra ye inquire karta hai ki ye kya hai ya kis kism ki cheez hai; kyunki wo truth ka ek spectator hai. Humein isi tarah act karna chahiye, to, baaki sab matters mein bhi, taaki hamara main task minor questions ke subordinate na ho jaaye. Na hi humein sab matters mein equally cause demand karni chahiye; kuch cases mein itna kaafi hai ki fact well established ho, jaise first principles ke case mein; fact ek primary cheez hai aur first principle hai. Ab first principles mein se kuch hum induction se dekhte hain, kuch perception se, kuch ek certain habituation se, aur baaki doosre tareeko se bhi. Lekin har set of principles ko humein natural way mein investigate karne ki koshish karni chahiye, aur unhe correctly determine karne mein pains lena chahiye, kyunki inka age ke baad aane wali cheezon par great influence hota hai. Kyunki beginning ko poore ka aadhe se zyada maana jaata hai, aur jo bahut se sawaal hum poochte hain unme se bahut saare isi se clear ho jaate hain.

**Hamari definition current beliefs se happiness ke baare mein confirm hoti hai**

**8.** Lekin humein happiness ko sirf apne conclusion aur premisses ke light mein hi nahi, balki jo commonly isके baare mein kaha jaata hai uske light mein bhi consider karna chahiye; kyunki ek true view ke saath saare data harmonize hote hain, lekin ek false view ke saath facts jaldi clash karte hain. Ab goods ko teen classes mein divide kiya gaya hai, aur kuch external describe kiye jaate hain, doosre soul se ya body se related; hum un cheezon ko jo soul se related hain sabse properly aur truly good kehte hain, aur psychical actions aur activities ko hum soul se related class karte hain. Isliye hamara account sound hona chahiye, kam se kam is view ke according, jo ek purana view hai aur philosophers dwara agreed on hai. Ye is baat mein bhi correct hai ki hum end ko certain actions aur activities ke saath identify karte hain; kyunki isse ye soul ke goods mein aata hai, external goods mein nahi. Ek aur belief jo hamare account se harmonize karta hai wo ye hai ki happy aadmi achha jeeta hai aur achha faire karta hai; kyunki humne practically happiness ko ek tarah ki living aur faring well define kiya hai. Jo characteristics happiness mein dhoondhe jaate hain wo bhi, sab, unhi cheez ke belong karte lagte hain jo humne happiness ko define kiya hai. Kyunki kuch log happiness ko virtue se identify karte hain, kuch practical wisdom se, doosre ek kism ki philosophic wisdom se, doosre in cheezon se, ya inme se ek se, pleasure ke saath ya pleasure ke bina nahi; jabki doosre external prosperity bhi include karte hain. Ab in views mein se kuch bahut logo aur purane logo ne hold ki hain, doosri kuch eminent persons ne; aur ye probable nahi hai ki inme se koi bhi entirely mistaken ho, balki ye zyada probable hai ki wo kam se kam kisi ek respect mein, ya even most respects mein, right hon.

Jo log happiness ko virtue se ya kisi ek virtue se identify karte hain unke saath hamara account harmony mein hai; kyunki virtue ke saath virtuous activity belong karti hai. Lekin ye, shayad, koi choti difference nahi banata ki hum chief good ko possession mein rakhte hain ya use mein, state of mind mein ya activity mein. Kyunki state of mind bina koi good result produce kiye bhi exist kar sakti hai, jaise ek aise aadmi mein jo so raha hai ya kisi doosre tareeke se quite inactive hai, lekin activity nahi kar sakti; kyunki jiske paas activity hai wo necessity se act kar raha hoga, aur well act kar raha hoga. Aur jaise Olympic Games mein sabse beautiful aur sabse strong ko crown nahi kiya jaata balki un logo ko jo compete karte hain (kyunki inme se kuch hi victorious hote hain), waise hi wo log jo act karte hain wo win karte hain, aur rightly win karte hain, life mein noble aur good cheezein.

Unki life bhi khud mein pleasant hai. Kyunki pleasure soul ki ek state hai, aur har insaan ke liye wo cheez jiska wo lover kaha jaata hai pleasant hoti hai; jaise sirf ek horse hi horses ke lover ke liye pleasant nahi hai, aur ek spectacle sights ke lover ke liye, balki isi tarah just acts justice ke lover ke liye pleasant hain aur generally virtuous acts virtue ke lover ke liye. Ab zyadatar logo ke liye unki pleasures ek doosre se conflict mein hoti hain kyunki ye nature se pleasant nahi hoti, lekin noble ke lovers un cheezon ko pleasant paate hain jo nature se pleasant hain; aur virtuous actions aisi hi hain, isliye ye aise logo ke liye pleasant hain jitni ki apni khud ki nature mein bhi. Unki life, isliye, ko pleasure ki further zaroorat nahi ek tarah ke adventitious charm ke roop mein, balki isme khud mein hi apna pleasure hai. Kyunki, jo humne kaha hai uske alawa, jo aadmi noble actions mein rejoice nahi karta wo good bhi nahi hai; kyunki koi bhi ek aise aadmi ko just nahi kahega jo justly act karne mein enjoy na kare, na hi kisi aadmi ko liberal jo liberal actions mein enjoy na kare; aur similarly baaki sab cases mein bhi. Agar ye aisa hai, to virtuous actions khud mein pleasant honi chahiye. Lekin ye good aur noble bhi hain, aur in dono attributes ko highest degree mein rakhti hain, kyunki good insaan in attributes ke baare mein achhe se judge karta hai; uska judgement waisa hi hai jaisa humne describe kiya hai. Happiness to duniya ki best, noblest, aur most pleasant cheez hai, aur ye attributes alag nahi hain jaise Delos ke inscription mein hai — 'Most noble wahi hai jo justest hai, aur best hai health; lekin most pleasant ye hai ki humein jo pyaar hai wo win karein.' Kyunki ye saari properties best activities ke belong karti hain; aur inhi mein se, ya ek — sabse best — ko hum happiness se identify karte hain.

Phir bhi evidently, jaisa humne kaha, isko external goods bhi chahiye hote hain; kyunki proper equipment ke bina noble acts karna impossible hai, ya aasaan nahi hai. Bahut si actions mein hum friends aur riches aur political power ko instruments ki tarah use karte hain; aur kuch cheezein aisi hain jinki kami happiness se uski lustre (chamak) le leti hai — good birth, achhe children, beauty; kyunki jo aadmi appearance mein bahut ugly hai ya ill-born hai ya solitary aur childless hai wo bahut likely nahi hai happy hone ke, aur shayad ek aadmi aur bhi kam likely hoga agar uske thoroughly bad children ya friends hon ya usne good children ya friends ko death se kho diya ho. Jaisa humne kaha, to, happiness ko iske alawa is tarah ki prosperity ki bhi zaroorat lagti hai; isi wajah se kuch log happiness ko good fortune se identify karte hain, halaanki doosre ise virtue se identify karte hain.

**Kya happiness learning ya habituation se acquire hoti hai, ya God dwara ya chance se milti hai?**

**9.** Isi wajah se ye sawaal bhi poocha jaata hai, ki kya happiness learning se ya habituation se ya kisi doosre kism ki training se acquire ki jaani hai, ya ye kisi divine providence ki wajah se aati hai ya phir chance se. Ab agar gods ka insaano ke liye koi gift hai, to ye reasonable hai ki happiness god-given ho aur saari human cheezon mein sabse zyada surely god-given ho kyunki ye sabse best hai. Lekin ye sawaal shayad kisi aur inquiry ke liye zyada appropriate hoga; happiness, phir bhi, lagti hai, chahe ye god-sent na ho balki virtue ke aur kuch learning ya training ke process ke result mein aaye, ki ye sabse godlike cheezon mein se ek ho; kyunki jo virtue ka prize aur end hai wo duniya ki best cheez lagti hai, aur kuch godlike aur blessed.

Is view par ye bhi bahut generally shared hogi; kyunki jo bhi log apni virtue ki potentiality ke respect mein maimed (viklaang) nahi hain wo ek certain kism ki study aur care se ise win kar sakte hain. Lekin agar chance se happy hone se better hai isi tarah happy hona, to ye reasonable hai ki facts aise hi hon, kyunki har cheez jo nature ke action par depend karti hai wo nature se utni hi good hai jitni ho sakti hai, aur similarly har cheez jo art ya kisi bhi rational cause par depend karti hai, aur khaas kar agar ye sabhi causes mein se best par depend karti ho. Sabse greatest aur most noble cheez ko chance ke hawale karna ek bahut defective arrangement hoga.

Jis sawaal ko hum pooch rahe hain uska jawaab happiness ki definition se bhi plain hai; kyunki isse ek certain kism ki soul ki virtuous activity kaha gaya hai. Baaki goods mein se, kuch ko necessarily happiness ki conditions ke roop mein pre-exist karna chahiye, aur doosre naturally co-operative aur instruments ki tarah useful hote hain. Aur ye us se agree karega jo humne shuru mein kaha tha; kyunki humne political science ke end ko best end bataya tha, aur political science apni zyadatar pains citizens ko ek certain character ka banane mein spend karti hai, yani good aur noble acts ke capable.

Ye natural hai, to, ki hum na ox ko, na horse ko, na kisi aur animal ko happy kehte hain; kyunki inme se koi bhi aisi activity mein share karne ke capable nahi hai. Isi wajah se ek boy bhi happy nahi hai; kyunki wo abhi aise acts ke capable nahi hai, apni age ki wajah se; aur jo boys happy kahe jaate hain unhe unke liye hone wali hopes ki wajah se congratulate kiya jaa raha hota hai. Kyunki, jaisa humne kaha, sirf complete virtue nahi balki ek complete life bhi chahiye, kyunki life mein bahut saare changes hote hain, aur har tarah ke chances, aur sabse prosperous log bhi old age mein great misfortunes mein fall ho sakte hain, jaise Trojan Cycle mein Priam ke baare mein bataya gaya hai; aur jisne aise chances experience kiye hon aur wretchedly khatam hua ho use koi bhi happy nahi kahega.

**Kya kisi insaan ko us ke jeete-ji happy nahi kaha jaana chahiye?**

**10.** Kya, to, kisi ko bhi apni life mein happy nahi kaha jaana chahiye; kya humein, jaisa Solon kehte hain, end dekhna chahiye? Chahe hum ye doctrine lay down karein bhi, kya ye bhi case hai ki ek aadmi tab happy hota hai jab wo dead hota hai? Ya kya ye quite absurd nahi hai, khaas kar humare liye jo kehte hain ki happiness ek activity hai? Lekin agar hum dead aadmi ko happy nahi kehte, aur agar Solon ka matlab ye nahi hai, balki ye ki tab hi ek aadmi ko safely blessed kaha ja sakta hai, jab wo aakhir mein evils aur misfortunes se pare ho, ye bhi discussion ka matter deta hai; kyunki dono, evil aur good, ek dead aadmi ke liye exist karte lagte hain, utne hi jitne ek aise aadmi ke liye jo alive hai lekin unhe aware nahi hai; jaise honours aur dishonours aur children ki good ya bad fortunes, aur generally descendants ki. Aur ye bhi ek problem present karta hai; kyunki chahe ek aadmi old age tak blessedly jiya ho aur uski life ke worthy ek death hui ho, uske descendants ke saath bahut se reverses ho sakte hain — kuch shayad good hon aur wo life attain kar lein jiske wo deserve karte hain, jabki doosron ke saath opposite ho sakta hai; aur clearly unke aur unke ancestors ke beech relationship ke degrees bhi indefinitely vary kar sakte hain. Ye odd hoga, to, agar dead aadmi in changes mein share kare aur kisi waqt happy ban jaaye, kisi waqt wretched; jabki ye bhi odd hoga agar descendants ki fortunes ka unke ancestors ki happiness par kuch waqt ke liye bhi koi effect na ho.

Lekin humein apni first difficulty par wapas aana chahiye; kyunki shayad iska ek consideration karke hamara present problem solve ho sake. Ab agar humein end dekhna hai aur tabhi ek aadmi ko blessed kehna hai, is roop mein nahi ki wo blessed hai balki is roop mein ki wo pehle aisa tha, surely ye ek paradox hai, ki jab wo happy hai to jo attribute usse belong karta hai wo truly predicate nahi kiya jaana chahiye kyunki hum living men ko happy nahi kehna chahte, un changes ki wajah se jo unke saath ho sakte hain, aur kyunki humne assume kiya hai ki happiness kuch permanent hai aur aasaani se change hone wali cheez bilkul nahi, jabki ek single aadmi fortune ke wheel ke bahut se turns bhi suffer kar sakta hai. Kyunki clearly agar hum uski fortunes ko follow karein, to hum aksar usi aadmi ko happy aur phir wretched kahenge, happy aadmi ko 'a chameleon, aur insecurely based' bana denge. Ya kya uski fortunes ko follow karna quite wrong hai? Life mein success ya failure in par depend nahi karti, lekin human life, jaisa humne kaha, inhe bhi zaroorat mein leti hai, jabki virtuous activities ya unke opposites hi wo cheezein hain jo happiness ya iska reverse determine karti hain.

Jo sawaal humne ab discuss kiya hai wo hamari definition confirm karta hai. Kyunki kisi bhi function of man mein utni permanence nahi hai jitni virtuous activities mein hai (ye sciences ke knowledge se bhi zyada durable maani jaati hain), aur inme bhi sabse valuable zyada durable hain kyunki jo blessed hain wo apni life sabse readily aur sabse continuously inhi mein spend karte hain; kyunki isi wajah se lagta hai ki hum inhe bhoolte nahi hain. Ye attribute, to, happy aadmi ko belong karega, aur wo apni poori life happy rahega; kyunki hamesha, ya baaki sab cheezon ke upar, wo excellent kya hai wahi karega aur contemplate karega, aur wo life ke chances ko sabse nobly aur poori tarah decorously bear karega, agar wo 'truly good' aur 'foursquare beyond reproach' hai.

Ab bahut se events chance se hote hain, aur importance mein different events; good fortune ke ya iske opposite ke small pieces clearly life ke scales ko kisi bhi taraf weigh down nahi karte, lekin great events ki ek multitude agar achhi tarah turn out ho to life ko zyada blessed bana degi (kyunki wo khud aise hain jo life mein beauty add karte hain, aur jis tarah se ek aadmi inhe deal karta hai wo noble aur good ho sakta hai), jabki agar ye badly turn out hon to ye blessedness ko crush aur maim kar dete hain; kyunki ye dono pain saath laate hain aur bahut si activities ko hinder karte hain. Phir bhi in mein bhi nobility shine karti hai, jab ek aadmi resignation ke saath bahut se great misfortunes bear karta hai, pain ki insensibility ki wajah se nahi balki nobility aur soul ki greatness ki wajah se.

Agar activities hi, jaisa humne kaha, life ke character ko determine karti hain, to koi bhi blessed aadmi miserable nahi ban sakta; kyunki wo kabhi hateful aur mean acts nahi karega. Kyunki jo aadmi truly good aur wise hai, hum sochte hain, wo life ke saare chances ko becomingly bear karta hai aur hamesha circumstances ka best banata hai, jaise ek good general apne command mein rakhi army ka best military use karta hai, aur ek good shoemaker use hides se best shoes banata hai jo use diye gaye hain; aur isi tarah baaki sab craftsmen bhi. Aur agar ye case hai, to happy aadmi kabhi miserable nahi ban sakta — halaanki wo blessedness tak nahi pahunchega, agar wo Priam jaisi fortunes se mile.

Na hi, phir se, wo many-coloured aur changeable hai; kyunki na to wo apni happy state se aasaani se ya kisi bhi ordinary misadventures se move hoga, balki sirf bahut se great misadventures se, na hi, agar usse bahut se great misadventures hue hain, wo apni happiness ko short time mein recover karega, balki agar bilkul bhi, to sirf ek lambi aur complete life mein jisme usne bahut si splendid successes attain ki hon.

To hum kyun na kahein ki wo happy hai jo complete virtue ke according active hai aur external goods ke saath sufficiently equipped hai, kisi chance period ke liye nahi balki ek complete life ke through? Ya humein 'aur jo aise jeene aur apni life ke befitting die karne ke liye destined hai' bhi add karna chahiye? Certainly future humare liye obscure hai, jabki happiness, hum claim karte hain, ek end hai aur har tarah se final kuch hai. Agar aisa hai, to hum un living men ko blessed kahenge jinme ye conditions hain, aur honi hain, fulfilled — lekin blessed men. In sawaalon ke liye itna kaafi hai.

**Kya zinda logo ki fortunes dead logo ko affect karti hain?**

**11.** Ye baat ki descendants ki aur ek insaan ke saare friends ki fortunes uski happiness ko bilkul affect na karein ek bahut unfriendly doctrine lagti hai, aur ek jo logo ke opinions ke opposite hai; lekin kyunki jo events hote hain wo numerous hain aur har tarah ke difference admit karte hain, aur kuch humare zyada near aate hain aur doosre kam, to ye ek long — nay, ek infinite — task lagta hai har ek ko detail mein discuss karna; ek general outline shayad kaafi hoga. Agar, to, jaise ek aadmi ke apne kuch misadventures ka life par ek certain weight aur influence hota hai jabki doosre, jaise, lighter hote hain, isi tarah humare friends ke misadventures mein bhi, poori tarah se liye jaayein to differences hain, aur ye farak padta hai ki various sufferings living ko hoti hain ya dead ko (bahut zyada is se bhi ki kya lawless aur terrible deeds ek tragedy mein presuppose kiye jaate hain ya stage par kiye jaate hain), ye difference bhi account mein liya jaana chahiye; ya shayad, us fact ko jo doubt feel kiya jaata hai ki dead kisi good ya evil mein share karte hain ya nahi. Kyunki in considerations se ye lagta hai ki, chahe kuch bhi, good ho ya evil, unhe penetrate kare, ye kuch weak aur negligible hona chahiye, ya to khud mein ya unke liye, ya agar nahi, to kam se kam degree aur kind mein aisa hona chahiye ki ye un logo ko na happy banaye jo happy nahi hain na hi un logo ki blessedness cheene jo happy hain. Friends ki good ya bad fortunes, to, dead par kuch effects rakhti lagti hain, lekin aise kism aur degree ke effects ki na to happy ko not-happy banaye na hi kisi doosre kism ka change produce karein.

**Virtue praiseworthy hai, lekin happiness praise se bhi upar hai**

**12.** In sawaalon ka definitely jawaab de diya gaya hai, ab chaliye consider karein ki kya happiness un cheezon mein hai jo praise ki jaati hain ya un cheezon mein jo prized (kimti maani jaati) hain; kyunki clearly ye potentialities mein place nahi ki jaani chahiye. Har cheez jo praise ki jaati hai lagti hai wo isliye praise ki jaati hai kyunki ye ek certain kism ki hai aur kisi doosri cheez se somehow related hai; kyunki hum just ya brave aadmi ko praise karte hain, aur generally good aadmi ko aur virtue ko bhi, involved actions aur functions ki wajah se, aur hum strong aadmi ko, good runner ko, waghera ko praise karte hain, kyunki wo ek certain kism ka hai aur kisi good aur important cheez se ek certain tareeke se related hai. Ye gods ko di jaane wali praise se bhi clear hai; ye absurd lagta hai ki gods ko humare standard se measure kiya jaaye, lekin hum unhe aisa hi measure karte hain, kyunki praise, jaisa humne kaha, kisi aur cheez ka reference involve karti hai. Lekin agar praise un cheezon ke liye hai jaisi humne describe ki hain, to clearly best cheezon par jo apply hota hai wo praise nahi, balki kuch greater aur better hai, jaisa indeed obvious hai; kyunki hum gods ke saath aur sabse godlike men ke saath jo karte hain wo unhe blessed aur happy kehna hai. Aur isi tarah good cheezon ke saath bhi; koi bhi happiness ko waise praise nahi karta jaise wo justice ko karta hai, balki ise blessed kehta hai, kuch zyada divine aur better mante hue.

Eudoxus bhi apni method mein right lagte hain pleasure ki supremacy ko advocate karne mein; unhone socha ki jo fact ki, ek good hote hue bhi, ye praised nahi hai, wahi indicate karta hai ki ye praised cheezon se better hai, aur ki yahi wo hai jo god aur good hain; kyunki inhi ke reference se doosri sab cheezein judge ki jaati hain. Praise virtue ke liye appropriate hai, kyunki virtue ke result mein log noble deeds karne ki tendency rakhte hain; lekin encomia (prashansaayen) acts ke liye bestow ki jaati hain, chahe body ki ho ya soul ki. Lekin shayad in matters mein niceties un logo ke liye zyada proper hain jinhone encomia ka study kiya hai; humare liye ye clear hai jo kaha ja chuka hai ki happiness un cheezon mein hai jo prized aur perfect hain. Ye is fact se bhi lagta hai ki ye ek first principle hai; kyunki isi ki khaatir hum baaki sab kuch karte hain, aur goods ka first principle aur cause, hum claim karte hain, kuch prized aur divine hai.

## KINDS OF VIRTUE

**Soul ka division, aur uske result mein virtue ka intellectual aur moral mein division**

**13.** Kyunki happiness perfect virtue ke according soul ki ek activity hai, humein virtue ki nature consider karni chahiye; kyunki shayad hum isi tarah happiness ki nature ko behtar dekh payenge. True student of politics bhi, virtue ko sab cheezon se zyada study kiya hua maana jaata hai; kyunki wo apne fellow citizens ko good aur laws ke obedient banana chahta hai. Iske example ke roop mein humare paas Cretans aur Spartans ke lawgivers hain, aur is kism ke baaki bhi jo hue honge. Aur agar ye inquiry political science ke belong karti hai, to clearly iski pursuit hamare original plan ke according hogi. Lekin clearly jo virtue humein study karni hai wo human virtue hai; kyunki jo good hum dhoondh rahe the wo human good tha aur happiness human happiness. Human virtue se humara matlab body ki nahi balki soul ki virtue se hai; aur happiness ko bhi hum soul ki ek activity kehte hain. Lekin agar aisa hai, to clearly student of politics ko soul ke facts ke baare mein somehow jaanna chahiye, jaise wo aadmi jise eyes heal karni hain use poori body ke baare mein bhi jaanna chahiye; aur ye aur bhi zyada zaroori hai kyunki political science medical science se zyada prized aur better hai; lekin doctors mein bhi sabse best educated body ki knowledge acquire karne mein bahut labour spend karte hain. Student of politics ko, to, soul ka study karna chahiye, aur ise in objects ko dhyan mein rakhte hue study karna chahiye, aur utna hi jitna un questions ke liye sufficient hai jinhe hum discuss kar rahe hain; kyunki further precision shayad hamare purposes ki requirement se zyada labour involve karegi.

Iske baare mein kuch cheezein kahi gayi hain, kaafi adequately, hamare school ke bahar ki discussions mein bhi, aur humein inhe use karna chahiye; jaise ki, soul mein ek element irrational hai aur ek mein reason hai. Kya ye body ke parts ki tarah, ya kisi bhi divisible cheez ki tarah, separated hain, ya kya ye definition se distinct hain lekin nature se inseparable, jaise ek circle ki circumference mein convex aur concave, ye present question ko affect nahi karta.

Irrational element ka ek division widely distributed lagta hai, aur nature mein vegetative, matlab wo jo nutrition aur growth cause karta hai; kyunki yahi kism ki soul ki power hai jo humein sab nurslings aur embryos ko assign karni chahiye, aur yahi same power full-grown creatures ko bhi; ye zyada reasonable hai in ko koi different power assign karne se. Ab iski excellence saari species mein common lagti hai, specifically human nahi; kyunki ye part ya faculty sleep mein sabse zyada function karta lagta hai, jabki goodness aur badness sleep mein sabse kam manifest hoti hain (isi wajah se ye saying aati hai ki happy log apni half life ke liye wretched se better off nahi hote; aur ye naturally enough hota hai, kyunki sleep soul ki us respect mein ek inactivity hai jisme ise good ya bad kaha jaata hai), siwaye is ke ki shayad kuch extent tak movements actually soul tak penetrate karte hain, aur is respect mein good men ke dreams ordinary logo ke dreams se better hote hain. Is subject par itna kaafi hai, phir bhi; chaliye nutritive faculty ko akela chhod dein, kyunki iska nature se human excellence mein koi share nahi hai.

Soul mein ek aur irrational element bhi lagta hai — jo ek sense mein, phir bhi, reason mein share karta hai. Kyunki hum continent aadmi ke aur incontinent ke reason ko praise karte hain, aur unki soul ke us part ko jiske paas reason hai, kyunki ye unhe aright aur best objects ki taraf urge karta hai; lekin unme reason ke alawa ek aur natural element bhi paaya jaata hai, jo iske against fight karta hai aur ise resist karta hai. Kyunki bilkul jaise paralysed limbs, jab hum unhe right ki taraf move karne ka intend karte hain, contrary mein left ki taraf turn karte hain, waisa hi soul ke saath bhi hota hai; incontinent logo ke impulses contrary directions mein move karte hain. Lekin jabki body mein hum wo dekh sakte hain jo astray move karta hai, soul mein hum aisa nahi dekhte. No doubt, phir bhi, humein phir bhi ye suppose karna chahiye ki soul mein bhi reason ke alawa kuch aisa hai jo isse resist aur oppose karta hai. Ye baaki elements se kis sense mein distinct hai humein concern nahi karta. Ab ye bhi reason mein share karta lagta hai, jaisa humne kaha; kam se kam continent aadmi mein ye reason ko obey karta hai — aur presumably temperate aur brave aadmi mein ye aur bhi zyada obedient hai; kyunki usme ye, sab matters mein, reason ki tarah hi same voice mein bolta hai.

Isliye irrational element bhi twofold lagta hai. Kyunki vegetative element kisi bhi tarah se reason mein share nahi karta, lekin appetitive aur generally desiring element ek sense mein isme share karta hai, jahan tak ye ise sunta aur obey karta hai; ye wahi sense hai jisme hum apne father ya apne friends ka 'taking account' (khayal rakhna) karte hain, us sense mein nahi jisme hum ek mathematical property ke liye 'accounting' karte hain. Ki irrational element kisi sense mein ek rational principle se persuade hota hai ye advice dene se aur saare reproof aur exhortation se bhi indicate hota hai. Aur agar is element ke baare mein bhi kehna chahiye ki iska ek rational principle hai, to jiske paas rational principle hai (aur jiske paas nahi hai bhi) wo twofold hoga, ek subdivision iske paas strict sense mein aur khud mein hone ke saath, aur doosra iske paas obey karne ki tendency ke saath jaise koi apne father ka karta hai.

Virtue bhi is difference ke according kisms mein distinguish ki jaati hai; kyunki hum kehte hain ki kuch virtues intellectual hain aur doosri moral, philosophic wisdom aur understanding aur practical wisdom intellectual hoti hain, liberality aur temperance moral. Kyunki ek insaan ke character ke baare mein baat karte hue hum ye nahi kehte ki wo wise hai ya usme understanding hai, balki ye ki wo good-tempered ya temperate hai; phir bhi hum wise aadmi ko bhi uski state of mind ke respect mein praise karte hain; aur states of mind mein se jinhe praise merit karti hai unhe hum virtues kehte hain.

# BOOK II · MORAL VIRTUE

## MORAL VIRTUE, HOW IT IS ACQUIRED

**Moral virtue, arts ki tarah, corresponding acts ki repetition se acquire hoti hai**

**1.** Virtue, to, do kisms ki hai, intellectual aur moral; intellectual virtue mostly apni birth aur apni growth teaching ko owe karti hai (isi wajah se isse experience aur time chahiye hote hain), jabki moral virtue habit ke result mein aati hai, isi wajah se iska naam (ēthikē) bhi ethos (habit) word se ek slight variation se banaya gaya hai. Isse ye bhi plain hai ki koi bhi moral virtue humme nature se paida nahi hoti; kyunki jo bhi cheez nature se exist karti hai wo apni nature ke contrary ek habit form nahi kar sakti. Jaise wo stone jo nature se neeche move karta hai use upar move karne ki habit nahi daali ja sakti, chahe koi use das hazaar baar upar throw karke train karne ki koshish kare; na hi fire ko neeche move karne ki habit daali ja sakti hai, na hi koi aur cheez jo nature se ek tareeke se behave karti hai use doosre tareeke se behave karne ke liye trained kiya ja sakta hai. Na to nature se, na hi nature ke contrary, virtues humme arise hoti hain; balki hum nature se inhe receive karne ke liye adapted hote hain, aur habit se perfect banaye jaate hain.

Phir, un saari cheezon mein se jo humme nature se aati hain, hum pehle potentiality acquire karte hain aur baad mein activity exhibit karte hain (ye senses ke case mein plain hai; kyunki humein ye senses often seeing ya often hearing se nahi mile, balki iske ulta, humare paas ye inhe use karne se pehle the, aur inhe use karke humne inhe paaya nahi); lekin virtues hum pehle unhe exercise karke paate hain, jaisa arts ke case mein bhi hota hai. Kyunki jo cheezein humein karne se pehle seekhni hoti hain, unhe hum karke hi seekhte hain, jaise log building karke builders bante hain aur lyre bajaakar lyre-players; isi tarah hum just acts karke just bante hain, temperate acts karke temperate, brave acts karke brave.

Ye states mein jo hota hai usse confirm hota hai; kyunki legislators citizens mein habits form karke unhe good banate hain, aur yahi har legislator ki wish hai, aur jo log isse effect nahi karte wo apna mark miss kar dete hain, aur isi mein ek good constitution ek bad se differ karta hai.

Phir, yahi wahi causes aur wahi means hain jinse har virtue produce bhi hoti hai aur destroy bhi, aur isi tarah har art bhi; kyunki lyre bajaane se hi achhe aur bure dono kism ke lyre-players produce hote hain. Aur yahi corresponding statement builders ke liye aur baaki sab ke liye bhi true hai; log good ya bad builders achha ya bura building karne ke result mein banenge. Kyunki agar aisa na hota, to teacher ki koi zaroorat na hoti, balki sab log apne craft mein good ya bad born hote. Ye, to, virtues ke saath bhi case hai; doosre logo ke saath apni transactions mein hum jo acts karte hain unhe karke hum just ya unjust bante hain, aur danger ki presence mein hum jo acts karte hain unhe karke, aur fear ya confidence feel karne ki habit daal kar, hum brave ya cowardly bante hain. Yahi appetites aur anger ki feelings ke liye bhi true hai; kuch log temperate aur good-tempered ban jaate hain, doosre self-indulgent aur irascible, appropriate circumstances mein ek ya doosre tareeke se behave karke. Isliye, ek shabd mein, states of character similar activities se arise hote hain. Isi wajah se jo activities hum exhibit karte hain wo ek certain kism ki honi chahiye; kyunki states of character in activities ke beech ke differences ke corresponding hote hain. Isliye koi choti difference nahi banta ki hum apni bahut jawaani se ek kism ki ya doosre kism ki habits form karte hain; ye ek bahut great difference banata hai, balki poori difference.

**Ye acts exactly prescribe nahi kiye ja sakte, lekin excess aur defect se bachna chahiye**

**2.** Kyunki, to, present inquiry theoretical knowledge ko baaki inquiries ki tarah aim nahi karti (kyunki hum ye jaanne ke liye inquire nahi kar rahe ki virtue kya hai, balki good banne ke liye, kyunki nahi to hamari inquiry ka koi use na hota), humein actions ki nature examine karni chahiye, yani hum unhe kaise karein; kyunki yehi wo states of character ki nature bhi determine karte hain jo produce hoti hain, jaisa humne kaha hai.

Ab, ki humein correct reason ke according act karna chahiye ye ek common principle hai aur ise assume kiya jaana chahiye — iski discussion baad mein hogi, yani correct reason kya hai, aur ye baaki virtues se kaise related hai. Lekin isse pehle ye agree kar liya jaana chahiye, ki conduct ke matters ka poora account outline mein diya jaana chahiye, precisely nahi, jaisa humne shuruaat mein hi kaha tha ki jo accounts hum demand karte hain wo subject-matter ke according hone chahiye; conduct se related matters aur ye sawaal ki humare liye kya good hai, inme koi fixity nahi hai, jitni health ke matters mein bhi nahi hai. General account jab is nature ka hai, to particular cases ka account isse bhi zyada exactness mein lacking hai; kyunki ye kisi art ya precept ke under nahi aate, balki agents ko khud har case mein consider karna padta hai ki occasion ke liye kya appropriate hai, jaisa medicine ki art mein ya navigation mein bhi hota hai.

Lekin halaanki hamara present account is nature ka hai, humein jitni help kar sakein utni deni chahiye. Pehle, to, chaliye ye consider karein, ki aisi cheezon ki nature hoti hai ki wo defect aur excess se destroy ho jaayein, jaisa hum strength aur health ke case mein dekhte hain (kyunki imperceptible cheezon par light gain karne ke liye humein sensible cheezon ke evidence ka use karna chahiye); exercise chahe excessive ho ya defective, strength ko destroy kar deta hai, aur isi tarah drink ya food jo ek certain amount se upar ya neeche ho wo health ko destroy kar deta hai, jabki jo proportionate hai wo isse produce, increase, aur preserve karta hai. Isi tarah ye temperance aur courage aur baaki virtues ke case mein bhi hai. Kyunki jo aadmi har cheez se bhaag jaata hai aur har cheez se darta hai aur kisi cheez ke against apna ground nahi hold karta wo coward ban jaata hai, aur jo aadmi kuch bhi nahi darta lekin har danger ko meet karne jaata hai wo rash ban jaata hai; aur isi tarah jo aadmi har pleasure mein indulge karta hai aur kisi se bhi abstain nahi karta wo self-indulgent ban jaata hai, jabki jo aadmi har pleasure se shun karta hai, jaise boors karte hain, wo ek tarah se insensible ban jaata hai; temperance aur courage, to, excess aur defect se destroy hoti hain, aur mean se preserve hoti hain.

Lekin sirf inke origination aur growth ke sources aur causes hi inke destruction ke sources aur causes ke same nahi hain, balki inki actualization ka sphere bhi same hoga; kyunki ye un cheezon ke liye bhi true hai jo sense ke liye zyada evident hain, jaise strength ke liye; ye bahut food lene aur bahut exertion undergo karne se produce hoti hai, aur strong aadmi hi in cheezon ko sabse zyada karne ke able hoga. Isi tarah ye virtues ke saath bhi hai; pleasures se abstain karke hum temperate bante hain, aur jab hum aise ban jaate hain tab hi hum sabse zyada in se abstain karne ke able hote hain; aur isi tarah courage ke case mein bhi; kyunki fearful cheezon ko despise karne aur unke against apna ground stand karne ki habit daal kar hum brave bante hain, aur jab hum aise ban jaate hain tab hi hum unke against apna ground sabse zyada stand karne ke able honge.

**Virtuous acts karne mein pleasure ek sign hai ki virtuous disposition acquire ho chuki hai: various considerations moral virtue ka pleasure aur pain ke saath essential connection dikhate hain**

**3.** Humein states of character ke sign ke roop mein us pleasure ya pain ko lena chahiye jo acts ke saath supervene karta hai; kyunki jo aadmi bodily pleasures se abstain karta hai aur isi fact mein delight leta hai wo temperate hai, jabki jo aadmi isse annoyed hota hai wo self-indulgent hai, aur jo terrible cheezon ke against apna ground stand karta hai aur isme delight leta hai ya kam se kam pained nahi hota wo brave hai, jabki jo aadmi pained hota hai wo coward hai. Kyunki moral virtue pleasures aur pains se concerned hai; pleasure ki wajah se hi hum bad cheezein karte hain, aur pain ki wajah se hum noble cheezon se abstain karte hain. Isliye humein apni bahut jawaani se ek particular tareeke se brought up hona chahiye tha, jaise Plato kehte hain, taaki hum un cheezon mein delight lein aur pained hon jinme humein hona chahiye; yahi right education hai. Phir, agar virtues actions aur passions se concerned hain, aur har passion aur har action ke saath pleasure aur pain aata hai, isi wajah se virtue bhi pleasures aur pains se concerned hogi. Ye is fact se bhi indicate hota hai ki punishment in hi means se inflict ki jaati hai; kyunki ye ek tarah ki cure hai, aur cures ki nature hoti hai contraries se effect hona.

Phir, jaisa humne abhi kaha, soul ki har state ki ek nature hoti hai un cheezon ke relative aur concerned jinse ye worse ya better banayi jaati hai; lekin pleasures aur pains ki wajah se hi log bad bante hain, in ko pursue aur avoid karke — chahe wo pleasures aur pains jo unhe nahi karni chahiye, ya jab unhe nahi karni chahiye, ya jaise unhe nahi karni chahiye, ya kisi doosre similar tareeke se wrong jaakar. Isi wajah se log virtues ko certain states of impassivity aur tranquillity bhi define karte hain; well nahi, phir bhi, kyunki wo absolutely baat karte hain, aur ye nahi kehte 'jaisa hona chahiye' aur 'jaisa nahi hona chahiye' aur 'jab hona chahiye ya nahi hona chahiye', aur baaki cheezein jo add ki ja sakti hain. Hum assume karte hain, to, ki is kism ki virtue pleasures aur pains ke respect mein best karne ki tendency rakhti hai, aur vice iske contrary karti hai.

Following facts bhi humein dikhaa sakte hain ki virtue aur vice inhi same cheezon se concerned hain. Choice ke teen objects aur avoidance ke teen hote hue, noble, advantageous, pleasant, aur inke contraries, base, injurious, painful, in sab ke baare mein good aadmi right jaane ki tendency rakhta hai aur bad aadmi wrong jaane ki, khaas kar pleasure ke baare mein; kyunki ye animals ke liye bhi common hai, aur ye choice ke saare objects ke saath bhi aata hai; kyunki even noble aur advantageous bhi pleasant appear karte hain.

Phir, ye hamari infancy se hi hamare saath grow hui hai; isi wajah se is passion ko rub off karna mushkil hai, kyunki ye humari life mein engrained hai. Aur hum apni actions ko bhi, kuch zyada kuch kam, pleasure aur pain ke rule se measure karte hain. Isi wajah se, to, hamari poori inquiry inhi ke baare mein honi chahiye; kyunki delight aur pain ko rightly ya wrongly feel karne ka humari actions par koi choti effect nahi hai.

Phir, pleasure ke against fight karna anger ke against fight karne se harder hai, Heraclitus ki phrase use karein to, lekin art aur virtue hamesha usi cheez se concerned hote hain jo harder hai; kyunki good bhi tab better hai jab ye harder hai. Isliye is wajah se bhi virtue aur political science dono ka poora concern pleasures aur pains ke saath hai; kyunki jo aadmi inhe well use karta hai wo good hoga, jo badly use karta hai bad.

Ki virtue, to, pleasures aur pains se concerned hai, aur ki un acts se jinse ye arise hoti hai, ye increase bhi hoti hai aur, agar wo differently kiye jaayein, destroy bhi hoti hai, aur ki jin acts se ye arose wahi acts hain jinme ye khud ko actualize karti hai — ise kaha hua maan lein.

**Us view par ek objection ki insaan virtuous acts karke virtues acquire karta hai; aur ek reply: virtue possess karne aur usse act karne ke liye needed conditions**

**4.** Ye sawaal poocha ja sakta hai, ki humne jo kaha ki humein just acts karke just banna chahiye, aur temperate acts karke temperate, iska matlab kya hai; kyunki agar log just aur temperate acts karte hain, to wo already just aur temperate hain, exactly jaise, agar wo grammatical ya musical cheezein karte hain, to wo grammarians aur musicians hain.

Ya kya ye arts ke case mein bhi true nahi hai? Ye possible hai kuch grammatical karna, chahe chance se ya kisi doosre ki guidance ke under. Ek aadmi grammarian tab banega, to, jab usne kuch grammatical kiya bhi ho aur ise grammatically kiya bhi ho; aur iska matlab hai ise apne andar ki grammatical knowledge ke according karna.

Phir, arts ka case aur virtues ka case similar nahi hain; kyunki arts ke products ki goodness khud mein hoti hai, isliye ye kaafi hai ki unme ek certain character ho, lekin agar virtues ke according acts khud mein ek certain character rakhte hain to ye follow nahi hota ki wo justly ya temperately kiye gaye hain. Agent ko bhi ek certain condition mein hona chahiye jab wo inhe karta hai; pehli baat, usse knowledge honi chahiye, doosri baat, usse acts choose karne chahiye, aur unhe unki khud ki khaatir choose karna chahiye, aur teesri baat, uska action ek firm aur unchangeable character se proceed karna chahiye. Ye arts ke possession ki conditions mein reckon nahi ki jaati, siwaye bare knowledge ke; lekin virtues ke possession ki condition ke roop mein knowledge ka little ya koi weight nahi hai, jabki baaki conditions ki bahut zyada, sach mein sab kuch, importance hai, yani wahi conditions jo often just aur temperate acts karne se result hoti hain.

Actions, to, just aur temperate kehlaate hain jab wo aisi hoti hain jaisi just ya temperate aadmi karega; lekin jo insaan ye karta hai wo just aur temperate nahi hai, balki wo jo inhe waise bhi karta hai jaise just aur temperate log karte hain. Ye well kaha gaya hai, to, ki just acts karke hi just aadmi produce hota hai, aur temperate acts karke temperate aadmi; ye kiye bina koi bhi good banne ki prospect bhi nahi rakhega.

Lekin zyadatar log ye nahi karte, balki theory mein refuge lete hain aur sochte hain ki wo philosophers ban rahe hain aur is tarah good ban jaayenge, somewhat un patients ki tarah behave karte hue jo apne doctors ko attentively sunte hain, lekin jo unhe order kiya jaata hai usme se kuch bhi nahi karte. Jaise doosre log aisi treatment ke course se body mein well nahi banaye jaayenge, waise hi pehle log philosophy ke aise course se soul mein well nahi banaye jaayenge.

## DEFINITION OF MORAL VIRTUE

**Iska genus: ye ek state of character hai, na ki ek passion, na hi ek capacity**

**5.** Ab humein consider karna chahiye ki virtue kya hai. Kyunki soul mein jo cheezein paayi jaati hain wo teen kism ki hain — passions, capacities, states of character — virtue inme se ek honi chahiye. Passions se mera matlab appetite, anger, fear, confidence, envy, joy, friendly feeling, hatred, longing, emulation, pity, aur generally wo feelings jinke saath pleasure ya pain aata hai; capacities se matlab wo cheezein jinki wajah se humein ye feelings feel karne ke capable kaha jaata hai, jaise angry hone ya pained hone ya pity feel karne ke; states of character se matlab wo cheezein jinki wajah se hum passions ke reference mein well ya badly stand karte hain, jaise anger ke reference mein hum badly stand karte hain agar hum ise violently ya too weakly feel karte hain, aur well agar hum ise ek intermediate tareeke se feel karte hain; aur isi tarah baaki passions ke reference mein bhi.

Ab na to virtues na hi vices passions hain, kyunki hum apni passions ke ground par good ya bad nahi kahe jaate, balki apni virtues aur apne vices ke ground par kahe jaate hain, aur kyunki hum apni passions ke liye na to praise kiye jaate hain na blame (kyunki jo aadmi fear ya anger feel karta hai use praise nahi kiya jaata, na hi jo aadmi simply anger feel karta hai use blame kiya jaata hai, balki wo aadmi jo ise ek certain tareeke se feel karta hai), lekin apni virtues aur apne vices ke liye hum praise ya blame kiye jaate hain.

Phir, hum anger aur fear bina choice ke feel karte hain, lekin virtues choice ke modes hain ya choice involve karti hain. Further, passions ke respect mein humein moved kaha jaata hai, lekin virtues aur vices ke respect mein humein moved nahi balki ek particular tareeke se disposed kaha jaata hai. In reasons ki wajah se bhi ye capacities nahi hain; kyunki humein passions feel karne ki simple capacity ke liye na to good ya bad kaha jaata hai, na hi praise ya blame kiya jaata hai; phir, humare paas capacities nature se hain, lekin hum nature se good ya bad nahi banaye jaate; humne isके baare mein pehle baat ki hai.

Agar, to, virtues na to passions hain na hi capacities, jo reh jaata hai wo ye hai ki wo states of character hon.

Isi tarah humne state kiya hai ki virtue apne genus ke respect mein kya hai.

**Iska differentia: ye 'intermediate' ko choose karne ki ek disposition hai. Do kism ke intermediate ko distinguish kiya gaya hai**

**6.** Humein, phir bhi, virtue ko sirf ek state of character ke roop mein describe nahi karna chahiye, balki ye bhi kehna chahiye ki ye kis kism ki state hai. Hum remark kar sakte hain, to, ki har virtue ya excellence us cheez ko good condition mein laati hai jiski ye excellence hai aur us cheez ke work ko well kiye jaane ka cause banti hai; jaise eye ki excellence eye aur uske work dono ko good banati hai; kyunki eye ki excellence se hi hum well dekhte hain. Similarly horse ki excellence horse ko khud mein good aur running mein aur apne rider ko carry karne mein aur enemy ke attack ka awaiting karne mein good banati hai. Isliye, agar ye har case mein true hai, to insaan ki virtue bhi wo state of character hogi jo insaan ko good banati hai aur jo use apna khud ka work well karne deti hai.

Ye kaise hota hai humne pehle state kar diya hai, lekin ye virtue ki specific nature ke following consideration se bhi plain hoga. Har us cheez mein jo continuous aur divisible hai, more, less, ya ek equal amount lena possible hai, aur ye chahe cheez ke khud ke terms mein ho ya humare relative ho; aur equal excess aur defect ke beech ek intermediate hai. Object mein intermediate se mera matlab wo hai jo dono extremes se equidistant hai, jo sab ke liye ek aur same hai; humare relative intermediate se matlab wo jo na too much hai na too little — aur ye ek nahi hai, na hi sab ke liye same hai. Jaise, agar das many hai aur do few, to six intermediate hai, object ke terms mein liya gaya; kyunki ye ek equal amount se exceed karta hai aur exceed hota hai; ye arithmetical proportion ke according intermediate hai. Lekin humare relative intermediate is tarah nahi liya jaana chahiye; agar das pounds ek particular person ke khaane ke liye too much hai aur do too little, to ye follow nahi hota ki trainer six pounds order karega; kyunki ye bhi shayad us person ke liye too much hai jise ise lena hai, ya too little — Milo ke liye too little, athletic exercises mein beginner ke liye too much. Yahi true hai running aur wrestling ke liye bhi. Isliye kisi bhi art ka master excess aur defect avoid karta hai, lekin intermediate seek karta hai aur ise choose karta hai — object mein nahi balki humare relative intermediate.

Agar ye is tarah hai, to, ki har art apna work well karti hai — intermediate ki taraf dekh kar aur apne works ko is standard se judge karke (isliye hum aksar art ke good works ke baare mein kehte hain ki inme se kuch bhi na to hataya ja sakta hai na add kiya ja sakta hai, ye implying karte hue ki excess aur defect art ke works ki goodness ko destroy karte hain, jabki mean ise preserve karta hai; aur good artists, jaisa hum kehte hain, apne kaam mein isi ki taraf dekhte hain), aur agar, further, virtue kisi bhi art se zyada exact aur better hai, jaise nature bhi hai, to virtue mein bhi intermediate ko aim karne ki quality honi chahiye.

Mera matlab moral virtue se hai; kyunki yahi wo hai jo passions aur actions se concerned hai, aur inme excess, defect, aur intermediate hota hai. Jaise, fear aur confidence aur appetite aur anger aur pity aur generally pleasure aur pain, in sab ko too much aur too little dono feel kiya ja sakta hai, aur dono cases mein well nahi; lekin inhe right times par, right objects ke reference mein, right people ki taraf, right motive ke saath, aur right way mein feel karna, yahi intermediate aur best dono hai, aur yahi virtue ki characteristic hai. Isi tarah actions ke respect mein bhi excess, defect, aur intermediate hota hai. Ab virtue passions aur actions se concerned hai, jinme excess ek kism ka failure hai, aur defect bhi hai, jabki intermediate praise kiya jaata hai aur ek kism ka success hai; aur praise kiya jaana aur successful hona dono hi virtue ki characteristics hain. Isliye virtue ek kism ka mean hai, kyunki, jaisa humne dekha hai, ye us cheez ko aim karta hai jo intermediate hai.

Phir, bahut se tareekon se fail hona possible hai (kyunki evil unlimited ki class se belong karta hai, jaisa Pythagoreans ne conjecture kiya, aur good limited ki class se), jabki succeed hona sirf ek hi tareeke se possible hai (isi wajah se ek aasan hai aur doosra difficult — mark ko miss karna aasan, hit karna difficult); in reasons ki wajah se bhi, to, excess aur defect vice ki characteristics hain, aur mean virtue ki:

*"Kyunki log good hone ke sirf ek tareeke mein hote hain, lekin bad hone ke bahut saare tareekon mein."*

Virtue, to, choice se concerned ek state of character hai, ek mean mein lying, yani humare relative mean, jo reason se determine hota hai, aur us reason se jise practical wisdom wala aadmi determine karega. Ab ye do vices ke beech ek mean hai, jo excess par depend karta hai aur jo defect par depend karta hai; aur phir se ye ek mean isliye hai kyunki vices respectively right se short fall karte hain ya exceed karte hain, dono passions aur actions mein, jabki virtue us cheez ko find bhi karti hai aur choose bhi karti hai jo intermediate hai. Isliye jo ye hai uske respect mein, yani wo definition jo iski essence state karti hai, virtue ek mean hai, lekin jo best aur right hai uske respect mein ek extreme hai.

Lekin har action ya har passion mean admit nahi karti; kyunki kuch ke naam already badness imply karte hain, jaise spite, shamelessness, envy, aur actions ke case mein adultery, theft, murder; kyunki in sab aur aise cheezon ke naam se hi ye imply hota hai ki wo khud bad hain, in ke excesses ya deficiencies nahi. Ye possible nahi hai, to, inke respect mein kabhi right hona; hamesha wrong hi hona hai. Na hi aisi cheezon ke respect mein goodness ya badness right woman ke saath, right time par, aur right way mein adultery commit karne par depend karti hai, balki simply inme se kuch bhi karna wrong jaana hai. Ye equally absurd hoga, to, expect karna ki unjust, cowardly, aur self-indulgent action mein ek mean, ek excess, aur ek deficiency ho; kyunki us hisaab se to excess ka bhi ek mean hoga aur deficiency ka bhi, excess ka ek excess, aur deficiency ki ek deficiency. Lekin jaise temperance aur courage ka koi excess aur deficiency nahi hai kyunki jo intermediate hai wo ek sense mein ek extreme hai, waise hi jo actions humne mention ki hain unka bhi koi mean nahi hai na koi excess aur deficiency, balki chahe wo kaise bhi kiye jaayein, wo wrong hi hain; kyunki generally excess aur deficiency ka koi mean nahi hai, na mean ka koi excess aur deficiency hai.

**Upar wali proposition ko particular virtues ke reference se illustrate kiya gaya hai**

**7.** Humein, phir bhi, sirf ye general statement hi nahi banani chahiye, balki ise individual facts par bhi apply karna chahiye. Kyunki conduct ke baare mein statements mein se jo general hain wo widely apply hoti hain, lekin jo particular hain wo zyada true hain, kyunki conduct ka relation individual cases se hai, aur hamare statements ko in cases ke facts se harmonize karna chahiye. Hum ye cases apni table se le sakte hain. Fear aur confidence ki feelings ke respect mein courage mean hai; jo log exceed karte hain unme se, jo fearlessness mein exceed karta hai uska koi naam nahi hai (bahut si states ka koi naam nahi hai), jabki jo aadmi confidence mein exceed karta hai wo rash hai, aur jo fear mein exceed karta hai aur confidence mein short falls karta hai wo coward hai. Pleasures aur pains ke respect mein — sab ke nahi, aur pains ke respect mein utna zyada nahi — mean temperance hai, excess self-indulgence. Pleasures ke respect mein deficient persons often nahi milte; isi wajah se aise persons ka bhi koi naam nahi hai. Lekin chaliye unhe 'insensible' kehte hain.

Money dene aur lene ke respect mein mean liberality hai, excess aur defect prodigality aur meanness. In actions mein log contrary tareekon se exceed aur short fall karte hain; prodigal spending mein exceed karta hai aur taking mein short falls karta hai, jabki mean aadmi taking mein exceed karta hai aur spending mein short falls karta hai. (Abhi hum sirf ek outline ya summary de rahe hain, aur isse satisfied hain; baad mein ye states zyada exactly determine ki jaayengi.) Money ke respect mein doosre dispositions bhi hain — ek mean, magnificence (kyunki magnificent aadmi liberal aadmi se differ karta hai; pehla large sums se deal karta hai, doosra small ones se), ek excess, tastelessness aur vulgarity, aur ek deficiency, niggardliness; ye liberality ke opposite states se differ karte hain, aur inke difference ka mode baad mein state kiya jaayega.

Honour aur dishonour ke respect mein mean proper pride hai, excess ek tarah ki 'empty vanity' ke roop mein jaana jaata hai, aur deficiency undue humility hai; aur jaise humne kaha liberality magnificence se related thi, isse small sums se deal karke differ karte hue, waise hi ek state similarly proper pride se related hai, small honours se concerned rehte hue jabki wo great se concerned hai. Kyunki honour ko jitna chahiye utna, jitne se zyada chahiye utna, aur jitne se kam chahiye utna desire karna possible hai, aur jo aadmi apni desires mein exceed karta hai use ambitious kaha jaata hai, jo aadmi short falls karta hai unambitious, jabki intermediate person ka koi naam nahi hai. Dispositions bhi nameless hain, siwaye is ke ki ambitious aadmi ki disposition ko ambition kaha jaata hai. Isi wajah se log jo extremes par hain wo middle place ka claim karte hain; aur hum khud kabhi-kabhi intermediate person ko ambitious kehte hain aur kabhi unambitious, aur kabhi ambitious aadmi ko praise karte hain aur kabhi unambitious ko. Aise karne ka reason aage state kiya jaayega; lekin abhi baaki states ke baare mein baat karein jaisi method indicate ki gayi hai.

Anger ke respect mein bhi ek excess, ek deficiency, aur ek mean hai. Halaanki inke naam shayad hi honge, phir bhi kyunki hum intermediate person ko good-tempered kehte hain, chaliye mean ko good temper kehte hain; extremes par ke persons mein se, jo exceed karta hai use irascible kehte hain, aur uski vice irascibility, aur jo aadmi short falls karta hai use unirascible kism ka insaan, aur deficiency unirascibility.

Teen aur means bhi hain, jinme ek doosre se ek certain likeness hai, lekin ek doosre se differ bhi karti hain: kyunki ye sab words aur actions mein intercourse se concerned hain, lekin isme differ karti hain ki ek is sphere mein truth se concerned hai, doosri do pleasantness se; aur inme se ek amusement dene mein exhibit hoti hai, doosri life ke general circumstances mein. Isliye humein in ke baare mein bhi baat karni chahiye, taaki hum ye behtar dekh sakein ki har cheez mein mean praiseworthy hai, aur extremes na to praiseworthy hain na right, balki blame ke worthy hain. Ab in states mein se zyadatar ka bhi koi naam nahi hai, lekin humein koshish karni chahiye, jaise doosre cases mein bhi, khud naam invent karne ki taaki hum clear ho sakein aur follow karna easy ho. Truth ke respect mein, to, intermediate ek truthful kism ka person hai aur mean ko truthfulness kaha ja sakta hai, jabki jo pretence exaggerate karta hai wo boastfulness hai aur us se characterized person ek boaster hai, aur jo understate karta hai wo mock modesty hai aur us se characterized person mock-modest hai. Amusement dene mein pleasantness ke respect mein, intermediate person ready-witted hai aur disposition ready wit, excess buffoonery hai aur us se characterized person ek buffoon, jabki jo aadmi short falls karta hai wo ek tarah ka boor hai aur uski state boorishness. Pleasantness ke baaki kism ke respect mein, jo generally life mein exhibit hoti hai, jo aadmi right way mein pleasant hai wo friendly hai aur mean friendliness hai, jabki jo exceed karta hai wo ek obsequious person hai agar uska koi end view mein nahi hai, ek flatterer agar wo apne khud ke advantage ko aim kar raha hai, aur jo aadmi short falls karta hai aur sab circumstances mein unpleasant hai wo ek quarrelsome aur surly kism ka insaan hai.

Passions mein bhi aur passions se concerned means hain; kyunki shame ek virtue nahi hai, phir bhi modest aadmi ko praise extend ki jaati hai. Kyunki in matters mein bhi ek aadmi ko intermediate kaha jaata hai, aur doosra exceed karta hai, jaise bashful aadmi jo har cheez se ashamed hota hai; jabki jo short falls karta hai ya kisi bhi cheez se bilkul ashamed nahi hota wo shameless hai, aur intermediate person modest hai. Righteous indignation envy aur spite ke beech ek mean hai, aur ye states hamare neighbours ki fortunes par feel kiye jaane wale pain aur pleasure se concerned hain; jo aadmi righteous indignation se characterized hai wo undeserved good fortune se pained hota hai, envious aadmi, usse aage jaakar, har good fortune se pained hota hai, aur spiteful aadmi pained hone se itna short falls karta hai ki wo rejoice bhi karta hai. Lekin in states ko describe karne ka opportunity kahin aur milega; justice ke respect mein, kyunki iska ek simple meaning nahi hai, hum, baaki states describe karne ke baad, iski do kisms ko distinguish karenge aur kahenge ki har ek kaise ek mean hai; aur isi tarah hum rational virtues ka bhi treat karenge.

## CHARACTERISTICS OF THE EXTREME AND MEAN STATES: PRACTICAL COROLLARIES

**Extremes ek doosre ke, aur mean ke, opposite hote hain**

**8.** Teen kism ki disposition hai, to, inme se do vices hain, respectively excess aur deficiency involve karte hue, aur ek virtue hai, yani mean, aur sab ek sense mein sab ke opposite hain; kyunki extreme states intermediate state ke aur ek doosre ke, dono ke contrary hain, aur intermediate extremes ke; jaise equal greater hai less ke relative, less hai greater ke relative, waise hi middle states excessive hain deficiencies ke relative, deficient hain excesses ke relative, dono passions aur actions mein. Kyunki brave aadmi coward ke relative rash appear karta hai, aur cowardly rash aadmi ke relative; aur isi tarah temperate aadmi self-indulgent appear karta hai insensible aadmi ke relative, insensible self-indulgent ke relative, aur liberal aadmi prodigal appear karta hai mean aadmi ke relative, mean prodigal ke relative. Isi wajah se extremes par ke log intermediate aadmi ko ek doosre ki taraf push karte hain, aur brave aadmi ko coward rash kehta hai, cowardly rash aadmi kehta hai, aur correspondingly baaki cases mein bhi.

Ye states isi tarah ek doosre se opposed hote hue, sabse greatest contrariety extremes ki ek doosre se hai, intermediate se zyada; kyunki ye ek doosre se further hain intermediate se, jaise great small se further hai aur small great se, dono equal se zyada. Phir, intermediate ke liye kuch extremes ek certain likeness dikhaate hain, jaise rashness ki courage se aur prodigality ki liberality se; lekin extremes ek doosre se greatest unlikeness dikhaate hain; ab contraries un cheezon ke roop mein define kiye jaate hain jo ek doosre se furthest hain, isliye jo cheezein further apart hain wo zyada contrary hain.

Intermediate ke liye kuch cases mein deficiency, kuch mein excess, zyada opposed hai; jaise, rashness nahi, jo ek excess hai, balki cowardice, jo ek deficiency hai, courage se zyada opposed hai, aur insensibility nahi, jo ek deficiency hai, balki self-indulgence, jo ek excess hai, temperance se zyada opposed hai. Ye do reasons se hota hai, ek khud cheez se drawn; kyunki ek extreme intermediate ke zyada near aur liker hai, isliye hum ise nahi balki iske contrary ko intermediate ke opposite rakhte hain. Jaise, kyunki rashness courage ke zyada like aur near lagti hai, aur cowardice zyada unlike, hum courage ke opposite doosre ko rakhte hain; kyunki jo cheezein intermediate se further hain wo isse zyada contrary lagti hain. Ye, to, ek cause hai, khud cheez se drawn; doosri khud humse drawn hai; kyunki jin cheezon ki taraf hum khud zyada naturally tend karte hain wo intermediate ke zyada contrary lagti hain. Jaise, hum khud pleasures ki taraf zyada naturally tend karte hain, aur isliye self-indulgence ki taraf zyada aasaani se carried away hote hain, propriety ki taraf se zyada. Hum mean ke contrary describe karte hain, to, un directions ko jinme hum zyada aksar great lengths tak jaate hain; aur isliye self-indulgence, jo ek excess hai, temperance ke zyada contrary hai.

**Mean ko attain karna mushkil hai, aur ise perception se grasp kiya jaata hai, reasoning se nahi**

**9.** Ki moral virtue ek mean hai, to, aur kis sense mein ye aisi hai, aur ki ye do vices ke beech ek mean hai, ek excess involve karti hai, doosri deficiency, aur ki ye aisi hai kyunki iska character passions aur actions mein jo intermediate hai use aim karna hai, ye sufficiently state kiya ja chuka hai. Isliye good hona koi easy task nahi hai. Kyunki har cheez mein middle find karna koi easy task nahi hai, jaise circle ka middle find karna sab ke liye nahi balki us ke liye hai jo jaanta hai; isi tarah, koi bhi angry ho sakta hai — ye aasan hai — ya money de ya spend kar sakta hai; lekin ise right person ke saath, right extent tak, right time par, right motive ke saath, aur right way mein karna, ye sab ke liye nahi hai, na hi ye aasan hai; isi wajah se goodness rare bhi hai aur laudable aur noble bhi.

Isliye jo intermediate ko aim karta hai use pehle us se depart karna chahiye jo iske zyada contrary hai, jaise Calypso advise karti hain — "Ship ko us surf aur spray se pare hold karo." Kyunki extremes mein se ek zyada erroneous hai, ek kam; isliye, kyunki intermediate ko hit karna extreme mein hard hai, humein ek second best ke roop mein, jaisa log kehte hain, evils mein se least lena chahiye; aur ye best isi tarah kiya jaayega jaisa humne describe kiya hai.

Lekin humein un cheezon ko bhi consider karna chahiye jinki taraf hum khud bhi aasaani se carried away hote hain; kyunki humme se kuch ek cheez ki taraf tend karte hain, kuch doosri taraf; aur ye us pleasure aur pain se recognizable hoga jo hum feel karte hain. Humein khud ko contrary extreme ki taraf ghaseet lena chahiye; kyunki hum error se well door jaakar hi intermediate state mein pahunchenge, jaise log bent sticks ko straighten karte waqt karte hain.

Ab har cheez mein pleasant ya pleasure ka sabse zyada guard karna chahiye; kyunki hum ise impartially judge nahi karte. Humein, to, pleasure ki taraf waise feel karna chahiye jaise log ke elders Helen ki taraf feel karte the, aur sab circumstances mein unki saying repeat karni chahiye; kyunki agar hum pleasure ko is tarah dismiss karein to hum astray jaane ke kam likely hain. Aisa karke, to, (matter ko sum up karein) hum best intermediate ko hit karne ke able honge.

Lekin ye no doubt difficult hai, khaas kar individual cases mein; kyunki ye determine karna aasan nahi hai ki kaise aur kis se aur kis provocation par aur kitni der tak angry hona chahiye; kyunki hum bhi kabhi-kabhi un logo ko praise karte hain jo short fall karte hain aur unhe good-tempered kehte hain, lekin kabhi hum un logo ko praise karte hain jo angry ho jaate hain aur unhe manly kehte hain.

Jo aadmi, phir bhi, goodness se thoda deviate karta hai wo blame nahi kiya jaata, chahe wo more ki direction mein aisa kare ya less ki, balki sirf wo aadmi jo widely deviate karta hai; kyunki wo notice hone se fail nahi hota. Lekin kis point tak aur kis extent tak ek aadmi deviate kare isse pehle wo blameworthy ban jaata hai ye reasoning se determine karna aasan nahi hai, jitna koi bhi aur cheez jo senses se perceive ki jaati hai; aisi cheezein particular facts par depend karti hain, aur decision perception par rehta hai.

Itna, to, plain hai, ki intermediate state sab cheezon mein praise ki jaani chahiye, lekin ki humein kabhi excess ki taraf, kabhi deficiency ki taraf incline karna chahiye; kyunki isi tarah hum sabse aasaani se intermediate aur right ko hit kar payenge.

# BOOK III · MORAL VIRTUE (cont.) 

## VOLUNTARY AND INVOLUNTARY ACTIONS, CHOICE, RESPONSIBILITY

**Praise aur blame voluntary actions se attach hoti hain, yani wo actions jo (1) bina forced hue, aur (2) circumstances ki knowledge ke saath ki jaati hain**

**1.** Kyunki virtue passions aur actions se concerned hai, aur voluntary actions par praise aur blame bestow ki jaati hai, jabki involuntary actions par pardon, aur kabhi-kabhi pity bhi, voluntary aur involuntary ko distinguish karna presumably necessary hai un logo ke liye jo virtue ki nature study kar rahe hain, aur useful bhi hai legislators ke liye honours aur punishments assign karne ke liye.

Wo cheezein, to, involuntary maani jaati hain jo force se ya ignorance ki wajah se hoti hain; aur wo forced hai jiska moving principle outside ho, ek aisa principle jisme person dwara act karne wale ne kuch bhi contribute na kiya ho — ya, better, jisse act kiya jaa raha ho, jaise agar use kahin wind se le jaaya jaaye, ya un logo dwara jinke power mein wo ho. Lekin un cheezon ke respect mein jo greater evils ke fear se ya kisi noble object ke liye ki jaati hain (jaise agar ek tyrant kisi ko kuch base karne ka order de, uske parents aur bacchon ko apne power mein rakhte hue, aur agar wo action karne se wo bach jaate lekin otherwise maar diye jaate), ye debate ki ja sakti hai ki kya aisi actions involuntary hain ya voluntary.

Kuch aisa hi hota hai storm mein goods overboard fenkne ke respect mein bhi; kyunki abstract mein koi bhi apni goods voluntarily nahi fenkta, lekin condition ke saath ki isse khud ki aur uski crew ki safety secure hogi, koi bhi sensible aadmi aisa karta hai. Aise actions, to, mixed hain, lekin voluntary actions se zyada like hain; kyunki inhe us time choose kiya jaata hai jab wo kiye jaate hain, aur ek action ka end occasion ke relative hota hai. Dono terms, to, 'voluntary' aur 'involuntary', action ke moment ke reference se use kiye jaane chahiye. Ab aadmi voluntarily act karta hai; kyunki jo principle aisi actions mein body ke instrumental parts ko move karta hai wo usme hai, aur jin cheezon ka moving principle khud aadmi mein hai wo uske power mein hain karna ya na karna. Aisi actions, isliye, voluntary hain, lekin abstract mein shayad involuntary; kyunki koi bhi khud mein aisi act ko choose nahi karega.

Aisi actions ke liye log kabhi-kabhi praise bhi kiye jaate hain, jab wo kuch base ya painful endure karte hain kuch great aur noble objects gain karne ke badle mein; opposite case mein wo blame kiye jaate hain, kyunki koi noble end ke liye nahi ya kisi trifling end ke liye greatest indignities endure karna ek inferior person ka mark hai. Kuch actions par praise indeed bestow nahi ki jaati, lekin pardon di jaati hai, jab koi ek wrongful act karta hai aisi pressure ke under jo human nature ko overstrain kare aur jise koi bhi withstand na kar sake. Lekin kuch acts, shayad, humein compel nahi kiye ja sakte, balki humein most fearful sufferings ke baad death ka face karna chahiye; kyunki jin cheezon ne Euripides ke Alcmaeon ko compel kiya apni mother ko slay karne ke liye wo absurd lagti hain. Kabhi-kabhi ye determine karna difficult hota hai ki kis cost par kya choose kiya jaana chahiye, aur kis gain ke badle mein kya endure kiya jaana chahiye, aur usse bhi zyada difficult apne decisions par abide karna hai; kyunki rule ke roop mein jo expect kiya jaata hai wo painful hai, aur jo humein karne ke liye compel kiya jaata hai wo base hai, isi wajah se un logo ke liye praise aur blame bestow ki jaati hai jinhe compel kiya gaya hai ya nahi.

Kis kism ke acts, to, forced kahe jaane chahiye? Hum jawaab dete hain ki bina qualification ke actions tab aisi hoti hain jab cause external circumstances mein ho aur agent kuch bhi contribute na kare. Lekin jo cheezein khud mein involuntary hain, lekin abhi aur in gains ke badle mein choose ki jaati hain, aur jinka moving principle agent mein hai, wo khud mein involuntary hain, lekin abhi aur in gains ke badle mein voluntary. Ye voluntary acts se zyada like hain; kyunki actions particulars ki class mein hain, aur yahan ke particular acts voluntary hain. Kis kism ki cheezein choose ki jaani chahiye, aur kis badle mein, ye state karna aasan nahi hai; kyunki particular cases mein bahut si differences hain.

Lekin agar koi kahe ki pleasant aur noble objects mein ek forcing power hai, jo humein without se compel karti hai, to sabhi acts uske liye forced honge; kyunki inhi objects ke liye sab log wo sab kuch karte hain jo wo karte hain. Aur jo log force se aur unwillingly act karte hain wo pain ke saath act karte hain, lekin jo log apni pleasantness ya nobility ke liye acts karte hain wo pleasure ke saath karte hain; ye absurd hai external circumstances ko responsible banana, aur khud ko nahi, aise attractions se aasaani se caught hone ke roop mein, aur khud ko noble acts ke liye responsible banana lekin pleasant objects ko base acts ke liye responsible. Forced, to, wo lagta hai jiska moving principle outside ho, forced person kuch bhi contribute na kare.

Har cheez jo ignorance ki wajah se ki jaati hai wo voluntary nahi hai; sirf wahi involuntary hai jo pain aur regret produce kare. Kyunki jis aadmi ne ignorance ki wajah se kuch kiya ho, aur apni action par least vexation bhi feel na kare, usne voluntarily act nahi kiya, kyunki use pata nahi tha wo kya kar raha hai, na hi involuntarily, kyunki wo pained nahi hai. In logo mein se jo ignorance ki wajah se act karte hain, jo regret karta hai wo ek involuntary agent maana jaata hai, aur jo aadmi regret nahi karta wo, kyunki different hai, ek not voluntary agent kaha ja sakta hai; kyunki, kyunki wo doosre se differ karta hai, better hai ki uska apna naam ho.

Ignorance ki wajah se act karna 'in ignorance' act karne se bhi different lagta hai; kyunki jo aadmi drunk hai ya rage mein hai wo ignorance ke result mein nahi balki mention ki gayi causes mein se ek ke result mein act karta lagta hai, phir bhi knowingly nahi balki ignorance mein.

Ab har wicked aadmi ignorant hai ki usse kya karna chahiye aur kya se abstain karna chahiye, aur is kism ki error log ko unjust aur generally bad banati hai; lekin term 'involuntary' use hone ki tendency rakhta hai us case mein nahi jab koi apne advantage ke baare mein ignorant ho — kyunki ye mistaken purpose nahi hai jo action ko involuntary banata hai (ye log ko wicked banata hai), na hi universal ki ignorance (kyunki iske liye log blame kiye jaate hain), balki particulars ki ignorance, yani action ke circumstances aur wo objects jinse ye concerned hai. Kyunki inhi par pity aur pardon dono depend karte hain, kyunki jo insaan in mein se kisi ka bhi ignorant hai wo involuntarily act karta hai.

Shayad ye achha hoga, isliye, inki nature aur number determine karein. Ek aadmi ignorant ho sakta hai, to, ki wo kaun hai, wo kya kar raha hai, kya ya kis par wo act kar raha hai, aur kabhi-kabhi kis instrument se wo isse kar raha hai, aur kis end ke liye (jaise wo soch sakta hai ki uska act kisi ki safety ki taraf conduce karega), aur kaise wo ise kar raha hai (jaise gently ya violently). Ab in sab mein se koi bhi ignorant nahi ho sakta jab tak wo mad na ho, aur evidently wo agent ke baare mein bhi ignorant nahi ho sakta; kyunki wo khud ko kaise na jaane? Lekin wo kya kar raha hai iske baare mein ek aadmi ignorant ho sakta hai, jaise log kehte hain 'ye unke mooh se baat karte hue slip out ho gaya', ya 'unhe pata nahi tha ki ye secret hai', jaise Aeschylus ne mysteries ke baare mein kaha, ya ek aadmi keh sakta hai usne 'ise let go off kiya jab wo sirf iski working dikhaana chahta tha', jaise ek aadmi ne catapult ke saath kiya. Phir, koi apne son ko enemy soch sakta hai, jaise Merope ne kiya, ya ki ek pointed spear par ek button hai, ya ki ek stone pumice-stone hai; ya koi kisi aadmi ko ek draught de sakta hai use save karne ke liye, aur really use kill kar de; ya koi kisi aadmi ko touch karna chahe, jaise log sparring mein karte hain, aur really use wound kar de. Ignorance, to, in cheezon mein se kisi se bhi relate ho sakti hai, aur jo aadmi in mein se kisi ka bhi ignorant tha wo involuntarily act kiya hua maana jaata hai, aur khaas kar agar wo sabse important points par ignorant tha; aur ye action ke circumstances aur iska end maane jaate hain. Further, is kism ki ignorance ki wajah se involuntary kahe jaane wale act ka karna painful hona chahiye aur regret involve karna chahiye.

Kyunki jo force se ya ignorance ki wajah se kiya jaata hai wo involuntary hai, voluntary wo lagega jiska moving principle khud agent mein hai, jo action ke particular circumstances se aware ho. Presumably anger ya appetite ki wajah se kiye gaye acts ko rightly involuntary nahi kaha jaata. Kyunki pehli baat, is showing par to koi bhi doosra animal voluntarily act nahi karega, na hi bacche; aur doosri baat, kya iska matlab ye hai ki hum appetite ya anger ki wajah se hone wali acts mein se koi bhi voluntarily nahi karte, ya ki hum noble acts voluntarily karte hain aur base acts involuntarily? Kya ye absurd nahi hai, jab ek hi same cheez cause hai? Lekin ye surely odd hoga in cheezon ko involuntary describe karna jinhe humein desire karna chahiye; aur humein kuch cheezon par angry hona chahiye aur kuch cheezon ke liye appetite hona chahiye, jaise health ke liye aur learning ke liye. Aur jo involuntary hai wo painful maana jaata hai, lekin jo appetite ke according hai wo pleasant maana jaata hai. Phir, calculation par committed errors aur anger mein committed errors ke beech involuntariness ke respect mein kya difference hai? Dono se bachna chahiye, lekin irrational passions human reason se kam human nahi maani jaati, isliye anger ya appetite se proceed karne wali actions bhi aadmi ki actions hain. Ye odd hoga, to, inhe involuntary treat karna.

**Choice ko voluntary se distinguish kiya gaya hai: choice ka object previous deliberation ka result hota hai**

**2.** Voluntary aur involuntary dono delimit ho chuke hain, humein aage choice discuss karni chahiye; kyunki ye virtue se sabse closely bound up maani jaati hai, aur actions se zyada characters ko discriminate karti hai.

Choice, to, voluntary lagti hai, lekin voluntary ke jaisi hi same cheez nahi; doosri zyada widely extend karti hai. Kyunki bacche aur lower animals dono voluntary action mein share karte hain, lekin choice mein nahi, aur spur of the moment kiye gaye acts humein voluntary describe karte hain, lekin chosen nahi. Jo log kehte hain ki ye appetite hai ya anger ya wish ya ek kism ki opinion, wo right nahi lagte. Kyunki choice irrational creatures ke liye bhi common nahi hai, lekin appetite aur anger hain. Phir, incontinent aadmi appetite ke saath act karta hai, lekin choice ke saath nahi; jabki continent aadmi iske contrary choice ke saath act karta hai, lekin appetite ke saath nahi. Phir, appetite choice ke contrary hai, lekin appetite appetite ke contrary nahi. Phir, appetite pleasant aur painful se relate karta hai, choice na to painful se na hi pleasant se.

Aur bhi kam ye anger hai; kyunki anger ki wajah se hone wale acts doosron se kam choice ke objects maane jaate hain.

Lekin ye wish bhi nahi hai, halaanki ye isi ke near lagta hai; kyunki choice impossibles se relate nahi kar sakti, aur agar koi kahe ki usne unhe choose kiya to wo silly maana jaayega; lekin impossibles ke liye bhi ek wish ho sakti hai, jaise immortality ke liye. Aur wish un cheezon se bhi relate kar sakti hai jo khud ke efforts se kisi bhi tarah brought about nahi ki ja sakti, jaise ki ek particular actor ya athlete competition mein jeete; lekin koi bhi aisi cheezein choose nahi karta, balki sirf wo cheezein jo wo sochta hai ki uske apne efforts se brought about ho sakti hain. Phir, wish end se relate karti hai, choice means se; jaise, hum healthy hona wish karte hain, lekin hum wo acts choose karte hain jo humein healthy banayenge, aur hum happy hona wish karte hain aur kehte hain ki hum aisa karte hain, lekin hum well nahi keh sakte ki hum aisa hone ko choose karte hain; kyunki, generally, choice un cheezon se relate karti lagti hai jo humari khud ki power mein hain.

Isi wajah se, ye opinion bhi nahi ho sakti; kyunki opinion sab kism ki cheezon se relate karti maani jaati hai, eternal cheezon se aur impossible cheezon se bhi utni hi jitni humari khud ki power mein cheezon se; aur ye apni falsity ya truth se distinguish ki jaati hai, apni badness ya goodness se nahi, jabki choice zyada in se distinguish ki jaati hai. Ab general opinion ke saath shayad koi bhi ise identical nahi kehta. Lekin ye kisi bhi kism ki opinion ke saath bhi identical nahi hai; kyunki good ya bad ko choose karke hum ek certain character ke insaan hain, jo hum certain opinions hold karke nahi hain. Aur hum kuch good ya bad get ya avoid karna choose karte hain, lekin humari opinions hoti hain ki ek cheez kya hai ya kis ke liye good hai ya kaise uske liye good hai; hum shayad hi kuch get ya avoid karne ke baare mein opine karte hain. Aur choice praise ki jaati hai right object se related hone ke liye ya right hone ke liye, opinion true hone ke liye. Aur hum wahi choose karte hain jo humein best pata hai ki good hai, lekin hum wahi opine karte hain jo humein least bhi nahi pata ki good hai; aur ye same log nahi maane jaate jo best choices banate hain aur best opinions rakhte hain, balki kuch ke paas fairly good opinions maane jaate hain, lekin vice ki wajah se wo wahi choose karte hain jo unhe nahi karna chahiye. Agar opinion choice se pehle aati hai ya isके saath accompany karti hai, isse koi farak nahi padta; kyunki hum ye consider nahi kar rahe, balki ye ki kya choice kisi kism ki opinion ke identical hai.

To ye kya hai, ya kis kism ki cheez hai, kyunki ye un cheezon mein se koi nahi jo humne mention ki hain? Ye voluntary lagta hai, lekin sab jo voluntary hai wo choice ka object nahi hai. Kya ye, to, wahi hai jo earlier deliberation se decide ki gayi ho? Kam se kam choice reason aur thought involve karti hai. Yahan tak ki naam bhi ye suggest karta lagta hai ki ye wahi hai jo doosri cheezon se pehle choose kiya jaata hai.

**Deliberation ki nature aur uske objects: choice hamari khud ki power mein cheezon ka deliberate desire hai**

**3.** Kya hum har cheez ke baare mein deliberate karte hain, aur kya har cheez deliberation ka ek possible subject hai, ya kya kuch cheezon ke baare mein deliberation impossible hai? Humein presumably wo nahi kehna chahiye jiske baare mein ek fool ya ek madman deliberate karega, balki wo jiske baare mein ek sensible aadmi deliberate karega, deliberation ka ek subject. Ab eternal cheezon ke baare mein koi bhi deliberate nahi karta, jaise material universe ke baare mein ya diagonal aur square ke side ki incommensurability ke baare mein. Lekin hum un cheezon ke baare mein bhi deliberate nahi karte jo movement involve karti hain lekin hamesha same tareeke se hoti hain, chahe necessity se ho ya nature se ya kisi aur cause se, jaise solstices aur stars ke risings; na un cheezon ke baare mein jo kabhi ek tareeke se, kabhi doosre tareeke se hoti hain, jaise droughts aur rains; na chance events ke baare mein, jaise treasure ka finding. Lekin hum saari human affairs ke baare mein bhi deliberate nahi karte; jaise, koi bhi Spartan Scythians ke liye best constitution ke baare mein deliberate nahi karta. Kyunki in mein se koi bhi cheez humare khud ke efforts se brought about nahi ki ja sakti.

Hum un cheezon ke baare mein deliberate karte hain jo humari power mein hain aur kiye ja sakte hain; aur ye actually wo hain jo reh jaata hai. Kyunki nature, necessity, aur chance causes maane jaate hain, aur reason bhi aur har cheez jo insaan par depend karti hai. Ab har class of men un cheezon ke baare mein deliberate karta hai jo unke apne efforts se ki ja sakti hain. Aur exact aur self-contained sciences ke case mein koi deliberation nahi hoti, jaise alphabet ke letters ke baare mein (kyunki humein koi doubt nahi hai ki wo kaise likhe jaayein); lekin jo cheezein humare apne efforts se brought about hoti hain, lekin hamesha same tareeke se nahi, wahi cheezein hain jinke baare mein hum deliberate karte hain, jaise medical treatment ya money-making ke sawaal. Aur hum navigation ki art ke case mein aisa zyada karte hain gymnastics ke case se zyada, kyunki ye kam exactly work out ki gayi hai, aur isi tarah baaki cheezon ke baare mein bhi same ratio mein, aur zyada arts ke case mein bhi sciences ke case se zyada; kyunki humein pehle wale ke baare mein zyada doubt hai. Deliberation un cheezon se concerned hai jo ek certain tareeke se most part hoti hain, lekin jinme outcome obscure hai, aur un cheezon mein jo indeterminate hain. Hum important questions par deliberation mein doosron ko madad ke liye bulate hain, khud par distrust karte hue ki hum decide karne ke equal nahi hain.

Hum ends ke baare mein nahi balki means ke baare mein deliberate karte hain. Kyunki ek doctor deliberate nahi karta ki wo heal karega ya nahi, na hi ek orator ye ki wo convince karega, na hi ek statesman ye ki wo law aur order produce karega, na hi koi aur apne end ke baare mein deliberate karta hai. End set karke, wo consider karte hain kaise aur kis means se ise attain kiya jaaye; aur agar ye kai means se produce hone lagta hai to wo consider karte hain kis se ye most easily aur best produce hoga, jabki agar ye ek hi se achieve hota hai to wo consider karte hain ki ye is se aur kis means se achieve hoga, jab tak wo first cause tak pahunch jaate hain, jo discovery ke order mein last hai. Kyunki jo aadmi deliberate karta hai wo inquire aur analyse karta lagta hai us tareeke se jaisa describe kiya gaya hai jaise wo ek geometrical construction ko analyse kar raha ho (sab inquiry deliberation nahi lagti — jaise mathematical inquiries — lekin saari deliberation inquiry hai), aur jo analysis ke order mein last hai wo becoming ke order mein first lagta hai. Aur agar hum ek impossibility par aa jaayein, hum search chhod dete hain, jaise agar humein money chahiye aur ye nahi mil sakti; lekin agar ek cheez possible lagti hai hum ise karne ki koshish karte hain. 'Possible' cheezon se mera matlab wo cheezein hain jo humare apne efforts se brought about ho sakti hain; aur ye ek sense mein un cheezon ko bhi include karti hain jo humare friends ke efforts se brought about ho sakti hain, kyunki moving principle humme hai. Investigation ka subject kabhi instruments hote hain, kabhi unka use; aur isi tarah doosre cases mein bhi — kabhi means, kabhi ise use karne ka mode ya ise bring about karne ke means. Ye lagta hai, to, jaisa kaha gaya hai, ki insaan actions ka ek moving principle hai; ab deliberation un cheezon ke baare mein hai jo agent khud ke through ki jaani hain, aur actions khud ke alawa doosri cheezon ki khaatir hoti hain. Kyunki end deliberation ka subject nahi ho sakta, sirf means; na hi indeed particular facts iske subject ho sakte hain, jaise ki kya ye bread hai ya ise sahi tareeke se bake kiya gaya hai; kyunki ye perception ke matters hain. Agar humein hamesha deliberate karte rehna hai, to hum infinity tak jaayenge.

Wahi cheez jo deliberate ki jaati hai wahi choose ki jaati hai, siwaye is ke ki choice ka object already determinate hai, kyunki ye wo hai jo deliberation ke result mein decide kiya gaya hai jo choice ka object hai. Kyunki har koi ye inquire karna band kar deta hai ki wo kaise act kare jab wo moving principle ko khud tak aur apne ruling part tak wapas le aata hai; kyunki yahi hai jo choose karta hai. Ye ancient constitutions se bhi plain hai, jinhe Homer ne represent kiya; kyunki kings apni choices ko logo ko announce karte the. Choice ka object un cheezon mein se ek hone ke naate jo humari khud ki power mein hain aur deliberation ke baad desire ki jaati hain, choice humari khud ki power mein cheezon ka deliberate desire hogi; kyunki jab hum deliberation ke result mein ek judgement tak pahunchte hain, hum apni deliberation ke according desire karte hain.

Hum le sakte hain, to, ki humne choice ko outline mein describe kar diya hai; humne iske objects ki nature state kar di hai aur ye fact ki ye means se concerned hai.

**Rational wish ka object end hai, yani good ya apparent good**

**4.** Ki wish end ke liye hai ye pehle hi state kiya jaa chuka hai; kuch sochte hain ye good ke liye hai, doosre apparent good ke liye. Ab jo log kehte hain ki good hi wish ka object hai unhe consequence mein ye admit karna chahiye ki jo aadmi rightly choose nahi karta wo jise wish karta hai wo wish ka object nahi hai (kyunki agar aisa hai, to ye bhi good hona chahiye; lekin ye bad bhi ho sakta hai); jabki jo log kehte hain ki apparent good wish ka object hai unhe admit karna chahiye ki wish ka koi natural object nahi hai, sirf wo jo har aadmi ko good lagta hai. Ab different cheezein different logo ko good appear karti hain, aur, agar aisa hota hai, to even contrary cheezein bhi.

Agar ye consequences unpleasing hain, to kya hum kahein ki absolutely aur truth mein good hi wish ka object hai, lekin har person ke liye apparent good; ki jo truly wish ka object hai wo good aadmi ke liye wish ka object hai, jabki koi bhi chance cheez bad aadmi ke liye aisi ho sakti hai, jaise bodies ke case mein bhi wo cheezein jo truly wholesome hain wo un bodies ke liye wholesome hain jo good condition mein hain, jabki jo diseased hain unke liye doosri cheezein wholesome hain — ya bitter ya sweet ya hot ya heavy, aur waghera; kyunki good aadmi har class of things ko rightly judge karta hai, aur har ek mein truth usse appear hoti hai? Kyunki har state of character ki apni khud ki ideas hoti hain noble aur pleasant ki, aur shayad good aadmi doosron se sabse zyada isi respect mein differ karta hai ki wo har class of things mein truth dekhta hai, jaise wo unka norm aur measure ho. Zyadatar cheezon mein error pleasure ki wajah se lagta hai; kyunki ye ek good lagta hai jab ye nahi hota. Hum isliye pleasant ko ek good ki tarah choose karte hain aur pain ko ek evil ki tarah avoid karte hain.

**Virtue aur vice hamari power mein hain**

**5.** End, to, wahi hai jo hum wish karte hain, means wo hain jinke baare mein hum deliberate karte hain aur unhe choose karte hain, means se related actions choice ke according aur voluntary honi chahiye. Ab virtues ka exercise means se concerned hai. Isliye virtue bhi humari khud ki power mein hai, aur isi tarah vice bhi. Kyunki jahan hamari power mein act karna hai wahan na act karna bhi hamari power mein hai, aur vice versa; isliye, agar act karna, jahan ye noble hai, hamari power mein hai, to na act karna, jo base hoga, bhi hamari power mein hoga, aur agar na act karna, jahan ye noble hai, hamari power mein hai, to act karna, jo base hoga, bhi hamari power mein hoga. Ab agar noble ya base acts karna hamari power mein hai, aur isi tarah unhe na karna bhi hamari power mein hai, aur yahi hai jiska matlab good ya bad hona tha, to virtuous ya vicious hona hamari power mein hai.

Ye saying ki 'koi bhi voluntarily wicked nahi hai na hi involuntarily happy' partly false aur partly true lagti hai; kyunki koi bhi involuntarily happy nahi hai, lekin wickedness voluntary hai. Ya phir humein abhi jo kaha gaya hai use dispute karna hoga, kam se kam, aur deny karna hoga ki insaan apni actions ka ek moving principle ya begetter hai, jaise bacchon ka.

Lekin agar ye facts evident hain aur hum actions ko khud mein moving principles ke alawa kisi aur ko refer nahi kar sakte, to jin acts ke moving principles humme hain wo khud bhi humari power mein aur voluntary hone chahiye. Isse witness bhi bear hota hai individuals dwara apni private capacity mein aur legislators khud dwara bhi; kyunki ye un logo ko punish karte hain aur unse revenge lete hain jo wicked acts karte hain (jab tak unhone compulsion ke under ya ignorance ke result mein act na kiya ho jiske liye wo khud responsible na hon), jabki wo un logo ko honour karte hain jo noble acts karte hain, jaise ki unka matlab doosre ko encourage karna aur pehle ko deter karna ho. Lekin koi bhi un cheezon ke liye encourage nahi kiya jaata jo na to humari power mein hain na voluntary hain; ye assume kiya jaata hai ki hot ya pain mein ya hungry ya isi tarah ki cheezon mein na hone ke liye persuade hone mein koi gain nahi hai, kyunki hum ye feelings none the less experience karenge. Indeed, hum ek aadmi ko uski very ignorance ke liye punish karte hain, agar wo ignorance ke liye responsible maana jaata hai, jaise drunkenness ke case mein penalties double ki jaati hain; kyunki moving principle aadmi khud mein hai, kyunki uske paas drunk na hone ki power thi aur uska drunk hona hi uski ignorance ka cause tha. Aur hum un logo ko punish karte hain jo laws mein kisi cheez ke ignorant hain jo unhe jaanni chahiye aur jo difficult nahi hai, aur isi tarah kisi doosri cheez ke case mein bhi jiske wo carelessness ki wajah se ignorant maane jaate hain; hum assume karte hain ki ignorant na hona unki power mein hai, kyunki unke paas care lene ki power hai.

Lekin shayad ek aadmi aisa kism ka insaan hai jo care nahi leta. Phir bhi wo khud apni slack lives ki wajah se aise insaan banne ke liye responsible hain, aur log khud unjust ya self-indulgent hone ke liye responsible hain, is tarah ki wo cheat karte hain ya drinking-bouts aur isi tarah ki cheezon mein apna time spend karte hain; kyunki particular objects par exercised activities hi corresponding character banaati hain. Ye kisi bhi contest ya action ke liye training karne wale logo ke case se plain hai; wo poore time activity practice karte hain. Ab ye na jaanna ki particular objects par activities ke exercise se hi states of character produce hoti hain ek thoroughly senseless person ka mark hai. Phir, ye irrational hai ye suppose karna ki jo aadmi unjustly act karta hai wo unjust hona nahi chahta ya jo aadmi self-indulgently act karta hai wo self-indulgent hona nahi chahta. Lekin agar bina ignorant hue ek aadmi wo cheezein karta hai jo use unjust banayengi, wo voluntarily unjust hoga. Phir bhi ye follow nahi hota ki agar wo wish kare to wo unjust hona stop kar dega aur just ban jaayega. Kyunki na hi jo aadmi ill hai wo un terms par well ban jaata hai. Hum ek case suppose kar sakte hain jisme wo voluntarily ill hai, incontinently jeekar aur apne doctors ki disobey karke. Us case mein ye tab usse open tha ill na hona, lekin ab nahi, jab usne apna chance throw away kar diya hai, jaise jab aapne ek stone jaane diya hai to use recover karna bahut der ho chuki hai; lekin phir bhi ise throw karna aapki power mein tha, kyunki moving principle aap mein tha. Isi tarah, unjust aur self-indulgent aadmi ke liye bhi shuru mein open tha is kism ka insaan na banna, aur isliye wo unjust aur self-indulgent voluntarily hain; lekin ab jab wo aise ban chuke hain unke liye aisa na hona possible nahi hai.

Lekin sirf soul ke vices hi voluntary nahi hain, balki body ke bhi kuch logo ke liye, jinhe hum accordingly blame karte hain; jabki koi bhi un logo ko blame nahi karta jo nature se ugly hain, hum un logo ko blame karte hain jo exercise aur care ki kami ki wajah se aise hain. Isi tarah weakness aur infirmity ke respect mein bhi; koi bhi ek aadmi ko reproach nahi karega jo birth se blind hai ya disease se ya blow se, balki use pity karega, jabki har koi ek aadmi ko blame karega jo drunkenness ya kisi aur kism ki self-indulgence se blind hua ho. Body ke vices mein se, to, jo humari khud ki power mein hain wo blame kiye jaate hain, jo humari power mein nahi hain wo nahi. Aur agar ye aisa hai, to doosre cases mein bhi jo vices blame ki jaati hain wo humari khud ki power mein honi chahiye.

Ab koi keh sakta hai ki sab log apparent good ko aim karte hain, lekin appearance par unka koi control nahi hai, balki end har aadmi ko uske character ke corresponding ek form mein appear karta hai. Hum reply karte hain ki agar har aadmi somehow apni state of character ke liye responsible hai, to wo khud bhi somehow appearance ke liye responsible hoga; lekin agar nahi, to koi bhi apne khud ke evildoing ke liye responsible nahi hai, balki har koi evil acts karta hai end ki ignorance ki wajah se, ye sochte hue ki in se wo best paayega, aur end ko aim karna self-chosen nahi hai balki kisi ko ek aankh ke saath born hona chahiye, jaisa ki, jisse rightly judge kiya jaaye aur truly good choose kiya jaaye, aur wo achha nature se endowed hai jo isके saath well endowed hai. Kyunki ye wo hai jo greatest aur most noble hai, aur jo hum kisi doosre se nahi le sakte ya seekh sakte, balki bilkul waisa hi rakhna chahiye jaisa ye humein birth par diya gaya tha, aur isके saath well aur nobly endowed hona natural endowment ki perfect aur true excellence hogi. Agar ye true hai, to, virtue vice se zyada voluntary kaise hogi? Dono logo ke liye equally, good aur bad, end appear karta hai aur nature se ya jaise bhi ho fix kiya jaata hai, aur baaki sab kuch isi ke reference se hi log jo bhi karte hain karte hain.

Chahe, to, ye nature se na ho ki end har aadmi ko waisa hi appear kare jaisa ye appear karta hai, ya kuch bhi khud us par depend karta hai, ya end natural hai lekin kyunki good aadmi means ko voluntarily adopt karta hai virtue voluntary hai, vice bhi utni hi voluntary hogi; kyunki bad aadmi ke case mein bhi equally wahi present hai jo khud usse apni actions mein depend karta hai chahe uske end mein na ho. Agar, to, jaisa asserted hai, virtues voluntary hain (kyunki hum khud somehow apni states of character ke part-causes hain, aur ye persons of a certain kind hone se hi hai ki hum end ko is tarah ya us tarah set karte hain), vices bhi voluntary honge; kyunki inke liye bhi same true hai.

Virtues ke general reference mein humne inka genus outline mein state kar diya hai, yani, ki wo means hain aur states of character hain, aur ki wo, apni khud ki nature se, un acts karne ki taraf tend karti hain jinse wo produce hoti hain, aur ki wo humari power mein aur voluntary hain, aur reason prescribe karta hai us tarah act karti hain. Lekin actions aur states of character same tareeke se voluntary nahi hain; kyunki hum apni actions ke masters hain shuru se le kar end tak, agar humein particular facts pata hain, lekin halaanki hum apni states of character ki shuruaat control karte hain, gradual progress obvious nahi hai, jitni ye illnesses mein bhi nahi hai; kyunki ye humari power mein tha, phir bhi, is tareeke se ya is tareeke se na act karna, isliye states voluntary hain.

Chaliye individual virtues lete hain, phir bhi, aur kahein wo kya hain aur kis kism ki cheezon se concerned hain aur kaise concerned hain; isi waqt ye plain ho jaayega ki wo kitni hain. Aur pehle chaliye courage ke baare mein baat karein.

## COURAGE

**Courage fear aur confidence ki feelings se concerned hai — strictly speaking, battle mein death ke fear se**

**6.** Ki ye fear aur confidence ki feelings ke respect mein ek mean hai ye already evident kiya jaa chuka hai; aur plainly jo cheezein hum fear karte hain wo fearful cheezein hain, aur ye, bina qualification ke bolen to, evils hain; isi wajah se log fear ko evil ki expectation bhi define karte hain. Ab hum sab evils ko fear karte hain, jaise disgrace, poverty, disease, friendlessness, death, lekin brave aadmi in sab se concerned nahi maana jaata; kyunki kuch cheezon ko fear karna even right aur noble hai, aur unhe fear na karna base hai — jaise disgrace; jo ise fear karta hai wo good aur modest hai, aur jo nahi karta wo shameless hai. Wo, phir bhi, kuch logo dwara brave kaha jaata hai, word ke ek naye meaning ki taraf transference se; kyunki usme kuch hai jo brave aadmi jaisa hai, kyunki brave aadmi bhi ek fearless person hai. Poverty aur disease ko humein shayad fear nahi karna chahiye, na hi generally un cheezon ko jo vice se proceed nahi karti aur khud aadmi ki wajah se nahi hain. Lekin in se fearless aadmi bhi brave nahi hai. Phir bhi hum ye word usse bhi apply karte hain ek similarity ki wajah se; kyunki kuch log jo war ke dangers mein cowards hain wo liberal hain aur money ke loss ke face mein confident hain. Na hi ek aadmi coward hai agar wo apni wife aur bacchon ko insult ka ya envy ka ya isi kism ka fear karta hai; na hi brave agar wo flogged hone jaa raha hai to confident hai. Kis kism ki fearful cheezon se, to, brave aadmi concerned hai? Surely greatest se; kyunki koi bhi usse zyada likely nahi hai jo awe-inspiring hai uske against apna ground stand karne mein. Ab death sabhi cheezon mein sabse fearful hai; kyunki ye end hai, aur koi bhi cheez dead ke liye ab good ya bad nahi maani jaati. Lekin brave aadmi even death ke saath sab circumstances mein concerned nahi lagta, jaise sea mein ya disease mein. Kis circumstances mein, to? Surely noblest mein. Ab aise deaths battle mein hoti hain; kyunki ye greatest aur noblest danger mein hoti hain. Aur ye correspondingly city-states mein aur monarchs ke courts mein honoured ki jaati hain.

Properly, to, use brave kaha jaayega jo ek noble death ke face mein fearless hai, aur un sab emergencies mein jo death involve karti hain; aur war ki emergencies is kind ki highest degree mein hain. Phir bhi sea mein bhi, aur disease mein, brave aadmi fearless hai, lekin seamen ki tarah nahi; kyunki usne safety ki hope chhod di hai, aur is shape mein death ke thought ko disliking kar raha hai, jabki wo apni experience ki wajah se hopeful hain. Usi waqt, hum courage un situations mein dikhaate hain jahan prowess dikhane ka opportunity ho ya jahan death noble ho; lekin in kism ki death mein in mein se koi bhi condition fulfil nahi hoti.

**Courage ka motive noble hai: opposite vices, cowardice aur rashness, ki characteristics**

**7.** Jo fearful hai wo sab logo ke liye same nahi hai; lekin hum kehte hain ki kuch cheezein even human strength se pare fearful hain. Ye, to, har kisi ke liye fearful hain — kam se kam har sensible aadmi ke liye; lekin jo fearful cheezein human strength se pare nahi hain wo magnitude aur degree mein differ karti hain, aur isi tarah wo cheezein jo confidence inspire karti hain. Ab brave aadmi utna hi dauntless hai jitna aadmi ho sakta hai. Isliye, halaanki wo even un cheezon ko fear karega jo human strength se pare nahi hain, wo unhe face karega jaisa usse karna chahiye aur jaisa reason directs karta hai, noble ki khaatir; kyunki yahi virtue ka end hai. Lekin ye possible hai in cheezon ko zyada, ya kam, fear karna, aur phir se un cheezon ko fear karna jo fearful nahi hain jaise wo ho. Jo faults committed hote hain unme se, ek us mein consist karta hai ki hum kya fear na karein use fear karna, doosra jaise fear na karna chahiye waise fear karna, teesra jab fear na karna chahiye tab fear karna, aur waghera; aur isi tarah confidence inspire karne wali cheezon ke respect mein bhi. Jo aadmi, to, right cheezon ko face karta hai aur fear karta hai right motive se, right way mein aur right time par, aur jo corresponding conditions ke under confidence feel karta hai, wo brave hai; kyunki brave aadmi case ke merits ke according aur jaise bhi reason directs karta hai us tarah feel aur act karta hai. Ab har activity ka end corresponding state of character ke conformity mein hai. Ye true hai, isliye, brave aadmi ke liye bhi jitna doosron ke liye. Lekin courage noble hai. Isliye end bhi noble hai; kyunki har cheez apne end se define hoti hai. Isliye ye ek noble end ke liye hai ki brave aadmi endure karta hai aur courage ke direct kiye anusaar act karta hai.

Jo log excess mein jaate hain unme se, jo fearlessness mein exceed karta hai uska koi naam nahi hai (humne pehle hi kaha hai ki bahut si states of character ke naam nahi hain), lekin wo ek tarah ka madman ya pain ke prati insensitive hoga agar wo kuch bhi fear na kare, na earthquakes na waves, jaise kehte hain Celts nahi karte; jabki jo aadmi confidence mein exceed karta hai unke baare mein jo really fearful hai wo rash hai. Rash aadmi, phir bhi, boastful bhi maana jaata hai aur sirf courage ka ek pretender; sab surat mein, jaise brave aadmi fearful cheezon ke respect mein hai, waise hi rash aadmi appear hona chahta hai; aur isliye wo use imitate karta hai jahan wo kar sakta hai. Isi wajah se unme se zyadatar rashness aur cowardice ka ek mixture hain; kyunki, in situations mein confidence display karte hue, wo really fearful cheezon ke against apna ground hold nahi karte. Jo aadmi fear mein exceed karta hai wo coward hai; kyunki wo dono cheezein fear karta hai jo usse nahi karni chahiye aur jaise nahi karni chahiye, aur saari similar characterizations usse attach hoti hain. Wo confidence mein bhi lacking hai; lekin wo painful situations mein apne fear ke excess ke liye zyada conspicuous hai. Coward, to, ek despairing kism ka person hai; kyunki wo sab kuch fear karta hai. Brave aadmi, doosri taraf, opposite disposition rakhta hai; kyunki confidence ek hopeful disposition ka mark hai. Coward, rash aadmi, aur brave aadmi, to, same objects se concerned hain lekin unke prati differently disposed hain; kyunki pehle do exceed aur short fall karte hain, jabki teesra middle, jo right position hai, hold karta hai; aur rash men precipitate hain, aur dangers ko beforehand wish karte hain lekin jab wo unme hote hain to draw back karte hain, jabki brave men action ke moment mein excited hote hain, lekin beforehand collected.

Jaisa humne kaha hai, to, courage confidence ya fear inspire karne wali cheezon ke respect mein ek mean hai, un circumstances mein jo state ki gayi hain; aur ye cheezein choose ya endure karta hai kyunki aisa karna noble hai, ya kyunki aisa na karna base hai. Lekin poverty ya love ya kisi aur painful cheez se escape karne ke liye die hona brave aadmi ka mark nahi hai, balki ek coward ka; kyunki troublesome se fly karna softness hai, aur aisa aadmi death ko isliye endure nahi karta kyunki ye noble hai balki evil se fly karne ke liye.

**Paanch kism ke courage jo improperly courage kehlaate hain**

**8.** Courage, to, is kism ki cheez hai, lekin naam paanch aur kisms par bhi apply hota hai. (1) Pehle citizen-soldier ka courage aata hai; kyunki ye true courage se sabse zyada like hai. Citizen-soldiers dangers face karte lagte hain laws dwara imposed penalties ki wajah se aur otherwise incur hone wale reproaches ki wajah se, aur aisi action se milne wale honours ki wajah se; aur isliye wo peoples bravest lagte hain jinme cowards ko dishonour mein rakha jaata hai aur brave men ko honour mein. Ye is kism ka courage hai jo Homer depict karte hain, jaise Diomede aur Hector mein:

*"Pehle Polydamas hoga jo mujhpar reproach heap karega tab; aur Hector ek din Trojans ke beech apni vaunting harangue utter karega: 'Afraid tha Tydeides, aur mere face se bhaag gaya.'"*

Ye kism ka courage humari earlier described ki gayi cheez se sabse zyada like hai, kyunki ye virtue ki wajah se hai; kyunki ye shame ki wajah se hai aur ek noble object (yani honour) ki desire aur disgrace ki avoidance, jo ignoble hai, ki wajah se hai. Koi bhi same class mein un logo ko bhi rank kar sakta hai jo apne rulers dwara compel kiye jaate hain; lekin wo inferior hain, kyunki wo jo karte hain wo shame se nahi balki fear se karte hain, aur avoid karte hain jo disgraceful hai use nahi balki jo painful hai use; kyunki unke masters unhe compel karte hain, jaise Hector karta hai:

*"Lekin agar main kisi dastard ko dekhu jo fight se door cowers, vainly wo aisa insaan dogs se escape karne ki hope karega."*

Aur jo unhe apne posts dete hain, aur unhe beat karte hain agar wo retreat karein, wo bhi same karte hain, aur isi tarah wo jo unhe trenches ke saath ya isi kism ki cheez ke saath unke peeche draw up karte hain; ye sab compulsion apply karte hain. Lekin ek aadmi ko compulsion ke under brave nahi hona chahiye balki isliye ki brave hona noble hai.

(2) Particular facts ke respect mein experience bhi courage maani jaati hai; yahi indeed reason hai ki Socrates ne socha ki courage knowledge thi. Doosre log ye quality doosre dangers mein exhibit karte hain, aur professional soldiers ise war ke dangers mein exhibit karte hain; kyunki war mein bahut si empty alarms lagti hain, jinka in logo ne sabse comprehensive experience liya hai; isliye wo brave lagte hain, kyunki doosre facts ki nature nahi jaante. Phir, unka experience unhe attack aur defence mein sabse zyada capable banata hai, kyunki wo apne arms use kar sakte hain aur unke paas wo kism ke hain jo attack aur defence dono ke liye best hone ki likelihood rakhte hain; isliye wo armed men ki tarah unarmed ke against fight karte hain ya trained athletes ki tarah amateurs ke against; kyunki aise contests mein bhi sabse brave men best fight nahi karte, balki wo jo strongest hain aur jinke bodies best condition mein hain.

Professional soldiers, phir bhi, cowards ban jaate hain jab danger unpar bahut zyada strain daalta hai aur wo numbers aur equipment mein inferior hain; kyunki wo pehle fly karte hain, jabki citizen-forces apne posts par die karte hain, jaisa actually Hermes ke temple mein hua. Kyunki doosre ke liye flight disgraceful hai aur death un terms par safety se preferable hai; jabki pehle wale ne shuru se hi danger face kiya is assumption par ki wo stronger hain, aur jab unhe facts pata chalte hain wo fly karte hain, death ko disgrace se zyada fear karte hue; lekin brave aadmi aisa person nahi hai.

(3) Passion bhi kabhi-kabhi courage maani jaati hai; jo log passion se act karte hain, jaise wild beasts jo unpar rush karte hain jinhone unhe wound kiya hai, wo brave maane jaate hain, kyunki brave men bhi passionate hote hain; kyunki passion sab cheezon se zyada danger par rush karne ke liye eager hai, aur isi wajah se Homer ki 'apni passion mein strength daali' aur 'unki spirit aur passion ko aroused kiya' aur 'hard breathed panting' aur 'uska blood boiled'. Kyunki ye saari expressions passion ke stirring aur onset ko indicate karti lagti hain. Ab brave men noble ki khaatir act karte hain, lekin passion unhe aid karti hai; jabki wild beasts pain ke influence ke under act karte hain; kyunki wo attack karte hain kyunki unhe wound kiya gaya hai ya kyunki wo afraid hain, kyunki agar wo forest mein hain to wo kisi ke near nahi aate. Isliye wo brave nahi hain kyunki, pain aur passion se driven, wo bina koi bhi perils foresee kiye danger par rush karte hain, kyunki us hisaab se to even gadhe bhi brave honge jab wo hungry hon; kyunki blows unhe apne food se drive nahi karenge; aur lust bhi adulterers ko bahut saari daring cheezein karwaati hai. Ye creatures brave nahi hain, to, jo pain ya passion se danger ki taraf driven hote hain. Passion ki wajah se hone wala 'courage' sabse natural lagta hai, aur courage lagta hai agar choice aur motive add kiye jaayein.

Men, to, aur beasts bhi, pain feel karte hain jab wo angry hote hain, aur pleased hote hain jab wo apna revenge exact karte hain; jo in reasons se fight karte hain, phir bhi, wo pugnacious hain lekin brave nahi; kyunki wo na to noble ki khaatir act karte hain na reason ke direct kiye anusaar, balki feeling ki strength se; unme, phir bhi, courage se kuch akin hai.

(4) Na hi sanguine log brave hain; kyunki wo danger mein confident hain sirf kyunki unhone often conquer kiya hai aur bahut se foes ke against. Phir bhi wo brave men se closely resemble karte hain, kyunki dono confident hain; lekin brave men earlier state kiye gaye reasons ki wajah se confident hain, jabki ye isliye hain kyunki wo sochte hain ki wo strongest hain aur kuch bhi suffer nahi kar sakte. (Drunken men bhi is tarah behave karte hain; wo sanguine ban jaate hain.) Jab unke adventures succeed nahi karte, phir bhi, wo run away karte hain; lekin ye brave aadmi ka mark tha ki wo un cheezon ko face kare jo aadmi ke liye terrible hain, aur lagti hain, kyunki aisa karna noble hai aur na karna disgraceful. Isi wajah se ye bhi maana jaata hai ki sudden alarms mein fearless aur undisturbed hona ek zyada braver aadmi ka mark hai un mein hone se jo foreseen hain; kyunki ye ek state of character se zyada proceed hona chahiye, kyunki preparation se kam; foreseen acts calculation aur reason se choose kiye ja sakte hain, lekin sudden actions apni state of character ke according honi chahiye.

(5) Jo log danger ke ignorant hain wo bhi brave appear karte hain, aur wo sanguine temper wale logo se bahut door nahi hain, lekin inferior hain kyunki unke paas self-reliance nahi hai jabki inke paas hai. Isi wajah se sanguine log kuch time ke liye apna ground hold karte hain; lekin jo log facts ke baare mein deceive kiye gaye hain wo fly karte hain agar unhe pata chale ya suspect ho ki ye wo se different hain jo unhone suppose kiya tha, jaisa Argives ke saath hua jab wo Spartans se mile aur unhe Sicyonians samjha.

Humne, to, brave men ke character ko aur un logo ke character ko bhi describe kiya hai jo brave maane jaate hain.

**Courage ka pain aur pleasure se relation**

**9.** Halaanki courage confidence aur fear se concerned hai, ye dono ke saath equally concerned nahi hai, balki zyada un cheezon ke saath jo fear inspire karti hain; kyunki jo in cheezon ke face mein undisturbed hai aur inke prati jaisa usse behave karna chahiye waisa behave karta hai wo un logo se zyada truly brave hai jo confidence inspire karne wali cheezon ke prati aisa karte hain. Painful cheezon ko face karne ki wajah se, to, jaisa kaha gaya hai, men ko brave kaha jaata hai. Isi wajah se courage pain bhi involve karti hai, aur justly praised hai; kyunki painful ko face karna pleasant se abstain karne se harder hai. Phir bhi jo end courage apne saamne set karti hai wo pleasant lagta hai, lekin attending circumstances se conceal hota hai, jaise athletic contests mein bhi hota hai; kyunki jo end boxers aim karte hain wo pleasant hai — crown aur honours — lekin jo blows wo lete hain wo flesh aur blood ke liye distressing hain, aur painful, aur isi tarah unka poora exertion; aur kyunki blows aur exertions bahut saare hain end, jo but small hai, appear karta hai ki isme kuch bhi pleasant nahi hai. Aur isi tarah, agar courage ka case similar hai, death aur wounds brave aadmi ke liye painful aur uski will ke against honge, lekin wo unhe face karega kyunki aisa karna noble hai ya kyunki na karna base hai. Aur jitna zyada wo virtue ki entirety se possessed hai aur jitna zyada happy hai, utna hi zyada wo death ke thought par pained hoga; kyunki aise aadmi ke liye life best worth living hai, aur wo knowingly greatest goods lose kar raha hai, aur ye painful hai. Lekin wo none the less brave hai, aur shayad even zyada, kyunki wo us cost par noble deeds of war choose karta hai. Ye case nahi hai, to, sab virtues ke saath ki inka exercise pleasant hai, siwaye is extent ke ki ye apna end attain karta hai. Lekin ye quite possible hai ki best soldiers is kism ke men na hon balki wo jo less brave hain lekin unke paas koi aur good nahi hai; kyunki ye danger face karne ke liye ready hain, aur wo apni life trifling gains ke liye sell karte hain.

Itna, to, courage ke liye kaafi hai; iski nature ko outline mein grasp karna difficult nahi hai, kam se kam, jo kaha gaya hai usse.

## TEMPERANCE

**Temperance sirf kuch touch ki pleasures tak limited hai**

**10.** Courage ke baad chaliye temperance ke baare mein baat karein; kyunki ye irrational parts ki virtues lagti hain. Humne kaha hai ki temperance pleasures ke respect mein ek mean hai (kyunki ye kam, aur same tareeke se nahi, pains se concerned hai); self-indulgence bhi isi sphere mein manifest hoti hai. Ab, isliye, chaliye determine karein ki wo kis kism ki pleasures se concerned hain. Hum bodily pleasures aur soul ki pleasures ke beech distinction assume kar sakte hain, jaise honour ka love aur learning ka love; kyunki har ek ka lover isme delight leta hai jiska wo lover hai, body kisi bhi tarah affected nahi hoti, balki mind zyada; lekin jo log aisi pleasures se concerned hain unhe na to temperate na hi self-indulgent kaha jaata. Na hi, phir se, wo log jo doosri pleasures se concerned hain jo bodily nahi hain; kyunki jo log stories sunne aur batane ke fond hain aur jo bhi aata hai uspar apne din spend karte hain wo gossips hain, lekin self-indulgent nahi, na hi wo jo money ya friends ke loss par pained hote hain.

Temperance bodily pleasures se concerned honi chahiye, lekin in sab se bhi nahi; kyunki jo log vision ke objects mein delight karte hain, jaise colours aur shapes aur painting, unhe na to temperate na self-indulgent kaha jaata hai; phir bhi ye possible lagta hai in mein bhi delight karna chahe as one should ya excess ya deficient degree tak.

Aur isi tarah hearing ke objects ke saath bhi; koi bhi un logo ko self-indulgent nahi kehta jo music ya acting mein extravagantly delight karte hain, na hi unhe temperate jo aise karte hain jaise unhe karna chahiye.

Na hi hum ye naam un logo ko apply karte hain jo odour mein delight karte hain, siwaye incidentally; hum un logo ko self-indulgent nahi kehte jo apples ya roses ya incense ke odour mein delight karte hain, balki un logo ko jo perfumes ya dainty dishes ke odour mein delight karte hain; kyunki self-indulgent log in mein isliye delight karte hain kyunki ye unhe unki appetite ke objects yaad dilaate hain. Aur koi bhi doosre logo ko bhi dekh sakta hai, jab wo hungry hote hain, food ki smell mein delight karte hue; lekin is kism ki cheez mein delight karna self-indulgent aadmi ka mark hai; kyunki ye uske liye appetite ke objects hain.

Na hi insaan ke alawa kisi aur animal mein in senses se connected koi pleasure hai, siwaye incidentally. Kyunki dogs hares ki scent mein delight nahi karte, balki unhe khaane mein, lekin scent ne unhe bataya ki hares wahan hain; na hi lion ox ke lowing mein delight karta hai, balki use khaane mein; lekin usne lowing se perceive kiya ki ye near hai, aur isliye lowing mein delight karta lagta hai; aur isi tarah wo 'a stag ya wild goat' dekhne ki wajah se delight nahi karta, balki kyunki wo iska meal banane wala hai. Temperance aur self-indulgence, phir bhi, is kism ki pleasures se concerned hain jinme doosre animals share karte hain, jo isliye slavish aur brutish lagti hain; ye touch aur taste hain. Lekin taste ka bhi wo little ya koi use nahi karte lagte; kyunki taste ka business flavours ko discriminate karna hai, jo wine-tasters aur dishes season karne wale log karte hain; lekin wo shayad hi in discriminations mein pleasure lete hain, ya kam se kam self-indulgent log nahi lete, balki actual enjoyment mein, jo sab cases mein touch ke through aata hai, food ke case mein bhi aur drink ke case mein bhi aur sexual intercourse ke case mein bhi. Isi wajah se ek certain gourmand ne pray kiya ki uska throat crane se longer ban jaaye, ye implying karte hue ki wo contact hi tha jisme wo pleasure leta tha. Isliye jo sense self-indulgence se connected hai wo senses mein sabse widely shared hai; aur self-indulgence justly reproach ka matter lagti hai, kyunki ye humme insaan ke roop mein nahi balki animals ke roop mein attach hoti hai. Aisi cheezon mein delight karna, to, aur unhe sab se upar pyaar karna, brutish hai. Kyunki touch ki pleasures mein bhi sabse refined eliminate ki ja chuki hain, jaise gymnasium mein rubbing aur consequent heat se produce hone wali; kyunki self-indulgent aadmi ki characteristic contact poori body ko affect nahi karti balki sirf certain parts ko.

**Temperance aur uske opposites, self-indulgence aur 'insensibility', ki characteristics**

**11.** Appetites mein se kuch common lagti hain, doosri individuals ke peculiar aur acquired; jaise food ki appetite natural hai, kyunki har koi jo isके bina hai wo food ya drink ke liye crave karta hai, aur kabhi-kabhi dono ke liye, aur 'bed' ke liye bhi (jaisa Homer kehta hai) agar wo young aur lusty hai; lekin har koi in mein se is ya us kism ke liye crave nahi karta, na hi same cheezon ke liye. Isliye aisi craving hamari khud ki lagti hai. Phir bhi iske paas certainly kuch natural bhi hai; kyunki different cheezein different kism ke logo ko pleasant hain, aur kuch cheezein sab ke liye chance objects se zyada pleasant hain. Ab natural appetites mein few wrong jaate hain, aur sirf ek direction mein, yani excess ki; kyunki jo bhi offer hota hai use surfeited hone tak khaana ya peena natural amount ko exceed karna hai, kyunki natural appetite apni deficiency ki replenishment hai. Isi wajah se in logo ko belly-gods kaha jaata hai, ye implying karte hue ki wo apna belly right se zyada fill karte hain. Ye entirely slavish character ke log hain jo aisa ban jaate hain. Lekin individuals ke peculiar pleasures ke respect mein bahut se log wrong jaate hain aur bahut se tareekon se. Kyunki jabki log jo 'so-and-so ke fond' hain unhe isliye kaha jaata hai kyunki wo ya to wrong cheezon mein delight karte hain, ya zyadatar logo se zyada, ya wrong way mein, self-indulgent log in teeno tareekon se exceed karte hain; wo dono un cheezon mein delight karte hain jinme unhe nahi karna chahiye (kyunki wo hateful hain), aur agar unhe un mein se kuch mein delight karna chahiye jinme wo delight karte hain, to wo isse zyada karte hain jitna unhe karna chahiye aur zyadatar men se zyada.

Plainly, to, pleasures ke respect mein excess self-indulgence hai aur culpable hai; pains ke respect mein koi bhi, jaise courage ke case mein, temperate nahi kaha jaata inhe face karne ke liye ya self-indulgent na karne ke liye, balki self-indulgent aadmi isliye kaha jaata hai kyunki wo pleasant cheezein na paane par jitna usse hona chahiye usse zyada pained hota hai (even uska pain pleasure se caused hota hai), aur temperate aadmi isliye kaha jaata hai kyunki wo pleasant ki absence par aur usse abstinence par pained nahi hota.

Self-indulgent aadmi, to, sab pleasant cheezon ke liye crave karta hai ya un cheezon ke liye jo most pleasant hain, aur apni appetite se led hota hai in cheezon ko sab kuch ke cost par jaane ke liye; isi wajah se wo pained hota hai jab wo unhe paane mein fail hota hai aur jab wo simply unke liye craving karta hai (kyunki appetite pain involve karti hai); lekin ye absurd lagta hai pleasure ki wajah se pained hona. Jo log pleasures ke respect mein short fall karte hain aur inme unse kam delight karte hain jitna unhe karna chahiye wo shayad hi milte hain; kyunki aisi insensibility human nahi hai. Doosre animals bhi different kism ke food ko distinguish karte hain aur kuch mein enjoy karte hain aur kuch mein nahi; aur agar koi aisa hai jise kuch bhi pleasant nahi lagta aur kuch bhi kisi doosri cheez se zyada attractive nahi lagta, wo aadmi se kuch quite different hona chahiye; is kism ke person ko naam nahi mila kyunki wo shayad hi occur hota hai. Temperate aadmi in objects ke respect mein ek middle position occupy karta hai. Kyunki wo na to un cheezon mein enjoy karta hai jinme self-indulgent aadmi sabse zyada enjoy karta hai — balki inhe dislike karta hai — na hi generally un cheezon mein jo usse nahi karni chahiye, na hi is kism ki kisi bhi cheez mein excess tak, na hi wo pain ya craving feel karta hai jab wo absent hon, ya sirf ek moderate degree tak karta hai, aur usse zyada nahi jitna usse karna chahiye, na jab usse nahi karna chahiye, aur waghera; lekin jo cheezein, pleasant hote hue, health ke liye ya good condition ke liye hain, unhe wo moderately aur jaise usse karna chahiye desire karega, aur doosri pleasant cheezein bhi agar wo in ends ke hindrances nahi hain, ya noble ke contrary nahi hain, ya uske means se pare nahi hain. Kyunki jo in conditions ko neglect karta hai wo aisi pleasures ko unse zyada pyaar karta hai jitni wo worth hain, lekin temperate aadmi aisa person nahi hai, balki wo kism ka person hai jise correct reason prescribe karta hai.

**Self-indulgence, cowardice se zyada voluntary hai: self-indulgent aadmi ki spoilt child se comparison**

**12.** Self-indulgence cowardice se zyada ek voluntary state jaisi hai. Kyunki pehla pleasure se actuate hota hai, doosra pain se, jinme se ek choose kiya jaana chahiye aur doosra avoid kiya jaana chahiye; aur pain us aadmi ki nature ko upset aur destroy karta hai jo ise feel karta hai, jabki pleasure aisa kuch nahi karta. Isliye self-indulgence zyada voluntary hai. Isi wajah se ye zyada reproach ka matter bhi hai; kyunki iske objects ke prati accustomed hona aasan hai, kyunki life mein is kism ki bahut si cheezein hain, aur inke prati habituation ka process danger se free hai, jabki terrible objects ke saath reverse case hai. Lekin cowardice apni particular manifestations se different degree mein voluntary lagti hai; kyunki ye khud painless hai, lekin in mein hum pain se upset hote hain, isliye hum apne arms bhi throw down kar dete hain aur doosre tareeko se khud ko disgrace karte hain; isi wajah se hamare acts even compulsion ke under kiye gaye maane jaate hain. Self-indulgent aadmi ke liye, doosri taraf, particular acts voluntary hain (kyunki wo unhe craving aur desire ke saath karta hai), lekin poori state kam voluntary hai; kyunki koi bhi self-indulgent hona crave nahi karta.

Self-indulgence naam childish faults ko bhi apply hota hai; kyunki wo humne jo consider kiya hai usse ek certain resemblance rakhte hain. Kaun kis ke baad kaha jaata hai, ye humare present purpose ke liye koi farak nahi padta; plainly, phir bhi, baad wala pehle ke baad kaha jaata hai. Naam ka transference ek bad nahi lagta; kyunki jo base desire karta hai aur jo quickly develop hota hai use ek chastened condition mein rakha jaana chahiye, aur ye characteristics sab se zyada appetite aur child ke belong karte hain, kyunki bacche actually appetite ke beck aur call par jeete hain, aur unme hi pleasant ki desire strongest hai. Agar, to, ye ruling principle ke obedient aur subject nahi banega, to ye great lengths tak jaayega; kyunki ek irrational being mein pleasure ki desire insatiable hai even agar ye gratification ke har source try kare, aur appetite ka exercise iski innate force ko increase karta hai, aur agar appetites strong aur violent hain wo even calculation ki power ko bhi expel kar dete hain. Isliye unhe moderate aur few hona chahiye, aur unhe kisi bhi tarah rational principle ke against nahi hona chahiye — aur yahi hai jise hum ek obedient aur chastened state kehte hain — aur jaise child ko apne tutor ki direction ke according jeena chahiye, waise hi appetitive element ko reason ke according jeena chahiye. Isi wajah se temperate aadmi mein appetitive element ko reason ke saath harmonize karna chahiye; kyunki noble hi wo mark hai jise dono aim karte hain, aur temperate aadmi un cheezon ke liye crave karta hai jinke liye usse karna chahiye, jaise usse karna chahiye, aur jab usse karna chahiye; aur yahi hai jo reason directs karta hai.

Yahan hum temperance ka apna account conclude karte hain.
